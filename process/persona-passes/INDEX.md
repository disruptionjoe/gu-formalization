# Persona Passes — Navigation Index

41 persona passes grouped by lens family (35 original + 6 added 2026-06-26 for the RS-middle-map / observer-creates-reality sprint). Each pass follows a structural template: (a) clearest leverage of the discipline on the four core questions, (b) strongest first-principles construction, (c) what fails or is forced, (d) named first-principles obstructions, (e) tagged-claim summary.

## How to read

Each lens family applies a Hegelian dialectical move within its discipline to test whether the no-go theorems (Witten 1981, Nielsen-Ninomiya, Freed-Hopkins, Distler-Garibaldi) are class-relative or class-invariant. The convergence across all 35 passes is summarized in the syntheses (see `../process/syntheses/INDEX.md`).

If you want fast convergence: read `../process/syntheses/00e-problem-shape-distributed-systems-meta-synthesis.md`, then return to whichever lens family is closest to your discipline.

If you want the full dialectical structure: read in pass order 01 → 35, then the syntheses 00 → 00e.
