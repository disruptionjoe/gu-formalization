# Constructor / Counterfactual — Heterodox Problem Shape (Observer-Substrate Co-Arising)

## (a) Thesis — fixed substrate, observer as a physical process within it

The mainstream constructor reading and the entire 5-loophole synthesis (P1 stochastic, P2 quantum-channel, P3 nondeterminism, P4 higher/derived, P5 Cartan) all share one premise: the substrate is fixed-prior, and the observer is a physical subsystem that performs measurement-class tasks on it. Witten 1981 and Freed-Hopkins are theorems about *the* fixed substrate (smooth manifolds, cobordism categories). Constructor optimism, applied within this premise, gets routed around at Witten but caught at Freed-Hopkins, exactly as 20-constructor-theory-loophole concluded. Chirality is a counterfactual property of a fixed bundle.

## (b) Heterodox antithesis — observer and substrate co-arise

Drop the fixed-substrate premise. Five heterodox lineages, each refusing it differently:

- **Hossenfelder / 't Hooft superdeterminism.** Hidden variables correlate observer-choices with quantum outcomes. The "free" observer is an artifact of measure-zero ensemble averaging; the substrate predetermines which projections occur. Chirality is then a *predetermined correlation* between observer-substrate co-realization, not a property of a bundle reduction.
- **Tononi IIT.** Observers are maximally integrated information subsystems (Φ-max). `[speculation]` The observerse projection is the carving where Φ peaks: the substrate carves into observer + observed precisely along the maximal-Φ partition. Chirality emerges as a Φ-asymmetry of the integration topology, not a Levi-Civita reduction.
- **Marletto constructor-of-information beyond substrate.** Information is substrate-independent in a counterfactual sense. Push harder: the *substrate itself* is a counterfactual property of which constructors are possible. Substrate and observer mutually constitute one another through which tasks are possible-with-each-other.
- **Panpsychist computational substrate.** Every substrate-fragment carries some observer-like aspect. The "observerse" is the fragment where observer-aspect is maximal. No clean substrate / observer divide; only gradients.
- **Wheeler delayed-choice extended cosmologically + Lewis modal realism.** The substrate is the modal multiverse; the 4D observed is one path retroactively definitized by present measurement. Chirality is selected, not derived. `[speculation]` The "observerse projection" is the act of modal selection itself.

All five refuse the same premise: that a substrate exists prior to and independent of the observer that carves chirality from it.

## (c) Aufhebung — problem shape

There is no observer-independent substrate. The "observerse projection" is the *constitutive act* by which observer and observed simultaneously come into mutual existence. Witten 1981 and Freed-Hopkins are facts about *a specific observer-substrate pairing* (the smooth-Levi-Civita-cobordism pairing that mainstream physics inherits from the Cartesian observer-frame). Other observer-substrate pairings, constituted differently, have **different obstruction classes**.

This Aufhebung preserves the no-go theorems (they hold within the pairing they describe), preserves the antithesis (alternative pairings are conceptually coherent), and transcends both by relocating the question: chirality is not a property of *the* substrate; it is a property of *the pairing*. Different pairings instantiate different chirality-task possibility statuses. `[speculation]` GU's "observerse projection" is then heterodoxly read as the *naming of a pairing*, not the reduction within a pairing.

## (e) Falsifiable sub-question

Is Freed-Hopkins **pairing-invariant or pairing-relative**? Concretely: does the Freed-Hopkins cobordism / invertible-field-theory anomaly classification depend on the Cartesian observer-substrate pairing (smooth manifold + measurement-as-subsystem), such that a Tononi-IIT-pairing or Wheeler-Lewis-modal-pairing instance of "chirality task" lives outside its obstruction domain? Or does it lift to every coherent pairing? If pairing-invariant, the heterodox dialectic closes at the same wall 20-constructor-theory-loophole hit. If pairing-relative, the lane has a genuinely new substrate-pairing axis to explore, distinct from 00c's substrate-class axis.
