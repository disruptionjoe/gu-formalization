# Problem Shape — Cartan / Twistor / Advanced Differential Geometer (Heterodox)

**Date:** 2026-05-28
**Baseline accepted:** Computation is substrate. Geometry is observer-frame artifact. The bundle is emergent. No-go theorems constrain observer-frames, not substrate. (00c)
**Persona:** Cartan geometries (in Cartan's original pseudo-group sense), Penrose-Newman twistor / pre-geometry, generalized and exceptional generalized geometry, hyperkahler and ADHM-class moduli, super-Cartan, loop-group / Kac-Moody Cartan structures. Strong heterodox lean.

## (a) Thesis (mainstream-in-domain)

Textbook Cartan geometry reads a 4-manifold with Levi-Civita connection as the maximally symmetric Klein-pair model bent by curvature; tractor calculus computes invariants on associated bundles; twistor theory in its standard form treats PT as a useful complex auxiliary for self-dual sectors. On this reading, the substrate-bundle inversion changes nothing: the Cartan connection is still a section of a principal bundle over the 4-manifold, the structure group is a finite-dimensional Lie group, and chirality is whatever the Levi factor of a parabolic subgroup grants. Witten 1981 / Freed-Hopkins act on the bundle level as in the orthodox synthesis.

## (b) Heterodox antithesis

Six moves the textbook reading refuses:

1. **Penrose pre-geometry.** Twistor space is **prior to** spacetime, not auxiliary. PT (a complex 3-fold) is the substrate-level object; the 4-manifold is reconstructed from incidence relations among alpha-planes. Spacetime points are *derived* from holomorphic curves in PT. The observer-frame "bundle" is a Penrose transform output, not an input. `[speculation]` In the 00c Aufhebung this maps cleanly: PT is the computational substrate, the observerse 4-manifold is the observer-frame artifact.

2. **Googly graviton / nonlinear graviton.** The Penrose-Newman googly problem is that anti-self-dual gravitons live in PT naturally while self-dual (googly) ones do not. Chirality is **built into the formulation at the substrate level**, not installed by reduction. Chiral asymmetry is a property of the twistor substrate's complex structure, not of any bundle Witten 1981 can touch.

3. **Generalized geometry (Hitchin-Gualtieri).** Drop the smooth tangent bundle as the primitive. The primitive is $TM \oplus T^*M$ with structure group $O(d,d)$ and a B-field twisting. SUSY without compactification arises in this setting. `[speculation]` GU's "14D observerse" could be read as a generalized 4-manifold whose extra structure is **not a fiber bundle in the classical sense** but a generalized tangent extension with U-duality data.

4. **Exceptional generalized geometry.** Extend further: tangent bundle by $E_{7(7)}$ or $E_{8(8)}$ representations. M-theory U-duality at the geometric level. `[speculation]` 14 is not in the exceptional dimension list, but the *extended tangent* of a 4-manifold under exceptional generalized geometry can carry $E_7$-representation content where SM-class chirality lives natively in the exceptional structure, not in a KK harmonic.

5. **Cartan pseudo-groups (Cartan's original).** Cartan's framework permits **infinite-dimensional Lie pseudo-groups** as structure groups: transitive Lie pseudo-groups (Cartan's classification: 6 simple infinite types incl. all symplectomorphisms, all volume-preserving, all contact). `[speculation]` The substrate's effective structure group could be a pseudo-group, not a finite-dimensional Lie group, putting it outside the smooth-principal-bundle hypotheses of Witten 1981 from the start.

6. **Super-Cartan / loop-group Cartan.** Super-Cartan geometry on supermanifolds bakes chirality into the $\mathbb{Z}/2$ grading; Kac-Moody / loop-group Cartan structures put WZW chiral data directly into the connection. Chirality is a structural feature of the connection's home, not an emergent feature of fiber spectra.

## (c) Aufhebung as a problem-shape question

Thesis says Cartan-geometric structure sits on a smooth principal bundle with finite-dimensional Lie structure group. Antithesis says the substrate is twistorial / generalized / pseudo-group-structured and the bundle is an observer-frame readout. The within-domain Aufhebung: **the observer-frame Cartan bundle is the projection of a substrate-level twistor-or-generalized object whose structure pseudo-group carries chirality as an intrinsic complex / Z2-graded / U-duality property, not as a representation-theoretic feature of a finite-dim Lie group reduction.**

Problem-shape:

> **What class of substrate object (a Penrose-style twistor pre-geometry, a Hitchin-Gualtieri generalized geometry with non-trivial B-field, an exceptional generalized geometry with $E_7$ extended tangent, or a Cartan pseudo-group connection) projects to an observer-frame 4-manifold whose induced Cartan connection carries chirality at the substrate-level structure, and what is the Freed-Hopkins anomaly invariant of that substrate class?**

## (d) What WRK-326 should ask next from this lens

Stop asking whether the metric bundle admits a parabolic / G_2 / tractor reduction that delivers chirality (the 15-loophole question). Candidates: twistor 3-fold over an emergent 4-manifold; generalized 4-mfld with $E_7$-extended tangent; Cartan pseudo-group connection with infinite-dim structure group; super-twistor with built-in Z2 grading.

> **Does a Penrose-Newman twistor space PT in complex dimension 3 admit a U-duality-compatible extension whose Penrose transform produces an observer-frame Cartan connection on a real 4-manifold with built-in chiral $\mathbb{Z}/2$ grading, and does that extension's Freed-Hopkins anomaly invariant match the SM signature?** `[speculation]`

A positive answer hands WRK-326 a substrate candidate adjacent to Penrose's decades-old program with chirality structurally present at the substrate level rather than installed at reduction.
