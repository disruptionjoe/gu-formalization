## The decision in one sentence

Three convergent v3 results (internal origin artifact factorization iff + internal origin artifact BvN obstruction & C_MPR + internal origin artifact 9-tuple frame) are ready for internal origin artifact Phase 3 publication dispatch; you pick whether they ship as **one big paper**, **three coordinated companion papers**, or **three independent papers in different venues**.

---

## Recommendation

**Option II — tight companion set (3 papers with mutual citation, coordinated arXiv co-release within 1-2 weeks of each other).**

Why: the convergence is the load-bearing finding; companion-set framing makes it visible to readers without burying any single paper under a 30-50 page integrated load; per-paper reviewer overhead is bounded; reputation strategy is "research program signal" rather than "one big swing" or "diversified portfolio"; the v3 source notes (especially internal origin artifact v3 §2.5 recommending "O5 companion-to-internal origin artifact" structure) already pre-figure this shape.

5-persona dialectic converges 5/5 on Option II with explicit trade-offs named.

## The six trade-offs under Option II (recommendation)

If you lock Option II, you accept:

1. **Coordination overhead.** ~120-180 min agent for shared definitions (C_MPR, 9-tuple), shared abstracts (companion footers), and submission timing.
2. **Cluster timing risk.** Three coordinated arXiv releases within 1-2 weeks; one paper's slow revision drags the cluster effect.
3. **Consistent disclosure obligation.** One GU-affiliation disclosure decision (Phase 2 Q4) governs all three papers; inconsistency surfacing later is a reputational risk.
4. **No single paper carries the full architectural story.** Readers picking up one paper get one theorem; the C_MPR convergence is visible only across the set.
5. **Three revision cycles.** Each paper has its own; ×3 vs Option I (×1) or Option III (×3 decoupled).
6. **Higher total agent-time.** ~1560-1980 min total (vs ~600-900 min for Option I), but parallelizable as 3 sub-agent workers (real-time ~600-780 min).

If any trade-off is unacceptable, fall back:
- Trade-off #1 unacceptable → fall back to Option I.
- Trade-off #2 unacceptable → fall back to Option III.
- Trade-off #3 unacceptable → fall back to Option III.
- Trade-off #4 unacceptable → fall back to Option I.
- Trade-off #5 unacceptable → fall back to Option I.
- Trade-off #6 unacceptable → fall back to Option I or Option III.

## Joe-decisions remaining after the path lock

These decisions are scoped per option and can be answered in the same walk OR deferred to Phase 3 dispatch contract:

### Under any option:

1. **Authorship (Q3 from Phase 2 editor pass).** Solo for arXiv first remains default. Under Option II, co-authoring opportunity clusters on Paper A (internal origin artifact v3, most physics-leaning). Under Option III, three independent co-author decisions.
2. **GU-affiliation visibility (Q4 from Phase 2 editor pass).** Option A (none), B (minimal mention), or C (explicit). Default Option B. Under Option II / III, decision must be applied consistently across all papers.
3. **arXiv-first vs journal-direct.** Editor's recommendation across all options is arXiv-first mandatory; this is not really a choice but confirming default keeps the option open.

### Under Option I only:

4. **Title (Q1 from Phase 2 editor pass).** One title needed. Long descriptive "Monotone Provenance, Non-Monotone Readout: A Factorization Iff Theorem, a Value-Lattice-Distributivity Obstruction, and a Shadow/Source Meta-Frame for No-Go Evasions" or shorter alternative.
5. **Single-venue choice.** CMP (reach) or LMP (realistic).

### Under Option II only:

6. **Three titles per paper.** Paper A "Monotone Provenance, Non-Monotone Readout..."; Paper B "The Value-Lattice-Distributivity Wall..."; Paper C "The Shadow / Source Meta-Frame...". Confirm or revise per paper.
7. **Three-venue commitment.** Paper A → LMP; Paper B → CMP; Paper C → arXiv-only or Foundations of Physics. Confirm or revise.
8. **arXiv co-release window.** 1-2 weeks default. Confirm or specify.
9. **Companion-footer text style.** "This paper is part of a three-paper companion set; companion paper [B / C] is at arXiv:XXXX.XXXXX and arXiv:YYYY.YYYYY" or alternative.

### Under Option III only:

10. **Three titles, three venue commitments, three independent disclosure decisions.** Higher decision load than Options I or II.
11. **Cross-reference policy.** Light cross-reference in related work or no cross-reference at all.

### Cross-cutting (resolves before any Phase 3 worker dispatches):

12. **Chat-agent validation brief outcome.** Has the external brief returned? If yes, what did it surface? If it surfaces sleight-of-hand in C_MPR, all three options may collapse and shaping reopens.
13. **internal origin artifact C_MPR convergence synthesis status.** Has the sibling artifact landed? If yes, canonical naming is locked there; if no, Template II's coordination pass produces interim canonical naming.
