## 0. The three v3 results being packaged

Single sentence per result, in canonical (v2.1 / v3) post-meta-reframe language:

- **internal origin artifact v3 — Factorization Iff Theorem.** Primitive iff `R : E → G is monotone iff every generator-weight in the readout group's positive cone` (the signed-readout monotonicity criterion), with the lattice-gauge anomaly content reframed as a sub-corollary. ~9.5–10/10 publish-grade for cross-disciplinary venues; ~9.5/10 for physics-only venues.
- **internal origin artifact v3 — BvN Obstruction + C_MPR Construction.** Two-faced unified verdict: direct adjunction `C_ClassicalDistribLR ⇄ C_GW` is OBSTRUCTED-CONFIRMED-SHARPER (value-lattice-distributivity wall, Birkhoff-von Neumann 1936 generalized), AND layered factorization `C_GW_loc → C_MPR → C_Shadow` is CONSTRUCTED-AS-CORRECTED at architectural/object level. Stronger reflectivity/free-adjoint/fibration claims remain OPEN. ~9/10 publish-grade.
- **internal origin artifact v3 — 9-Tuple Meta-Template.** META-FRAME HOLDS / META-THEOREM PARTIAL split-verdict. 9-tuple `(T_X, C_X, U, ρ, C_U, ft, ex, rr, arch)` as classification frame with operational checklist; universal-completeness named as open conjecture. Publishable as frame paper with companion to a worked instance. ~7–8/10 standalone; rises to ~8–9/10 when published as companion to a worked-instance paper.

These three results all converge on the **C_MPR architecture** (monotone-provenance + signed/non-monotone-readout factorization) and on the **classical-value-lattice wall** as universal obstruction, per the v2.1 parallel-artifact synthesis and the (pending) internal origin artifact C_MPR convergence synthesis.
