# The Firewall-Boundary Hypothesis

> This is now the repository's PRIMARY research question and primary falsification target. It is to be
> attacked, not defended (see "Research philosophy" below). Canon status here means "this is the question
> we are organizing around," not "this is established physics."

## The pivot

The repository's question has evolved. It is no longer:

> "Can Geometric Unity be completed as a CLOSED mathematical system that reproduces known physics?"

It is now:

> "Does every successful reconstruction inevitably converge on a FIREWALL-LIKE BOUNDARY object, rather
> than on a closed internal completion?"

## Motivating belief (recorded as a working belief, not a result)

The maintainer's working belief is that the overall geometric picture of GU is substantially correct --
not by decision, but because independent reconstruction work keeps converging on the same large-scale
shape: Standard Model structure, the quantum-mechanical framework, the overall geometric architecture,
and potentially the three-generation structure. This belief is the MOTIVATION for the pivot; it is not
itself a claim this repository asserts as proven. The repository's verifiable claims remain
reconstruction-grade and honestly hedged elsewhere in canon.

## The hypothesis

Across the reconstruction, the remaining major obstruction has consistently appeared at what would
traditionally be the "closed-system" boundary. The new hypothesis reinterprets that obstruction:

> If GU describes reality correctly, then reality may not be a mathematically CLOSED system. The geometry
> naturally TERMINATES AT AN INTERFACE. The missing mathematical object looks less like "one more internal
> equation" and more like a constrained boundary condition: a mathematically firewall-like ADAPTER that
> separates the observable geometric system from whatever determines the source action governing its
> evolution.

In the language of the generation-sector audit (`canon/no-go-quaternionic-parity-generation-sector.md`),
the "external object" that the under-determination and the quaternionic-parity constraint both point to is
exactly a candidate for this boundary adapter. The interface-specification framing of the papers
(`draft-papers/generation-sector-no-go-note-2026-06-27.md`) is the same idea stated for one sector.

## What is and is NOT being claimed

- NOT claimed: that an external reality has been demonstrated. It has not. Nothing here establishes
  anything outside the mathematics.
- NOT claimed: that GU is proven, or that the boundary reading is proven over a closed completion.
- Claimed only: that IF GU is correct, the mathematics itself appears to PREFER a boundary object over a
  closed internal completion. That preference is what makes the firewall a genuine, testable hypothesis
  rather than a metaphor. The verdict (boundary vs closed) is currently undecided and the facts do not
  settle it; only sustained falsification attempts can move confidence.

## Explicit falsification criteria (attack these)

The hypothesis WEAKENS if any of the following succeeds:

1. **Closed completion exists.** A completely closed internal source action can be constructed without
   introducing contradictions (no import, no hand-tuned rank/scalar, anti-trap and the other guards intact).
2. **Boundary is removable.** A mathematically equivalent reconstruction exists that removes the boundary
   object entirely.
3. **Alternative completion.** Another completion explains the same successful physics (SM, QM,
   three-generation structure) while eliminating the firewall interpretation.
4. **Artifact of choices.** The apparent firewall is shown to be an artifact of current reconstruction
   choices (most pointedly: the Cl(9,5)=M(64,H) signature/representation, which is the standing
   rep-canonicity caveat across the audit).
5. **Physics survives boundary rejection.** The SM, QM, and the three-generation structure can all still
   be derived if the boundary interpretation is rejected.

If any answer is YES, the firewall hypothesis weakens and the finding is recorded. If repeated independent
reconstruction attempts instead keep converging on a boundary object occupying the SAME structural role,
confidence increases that the geometry is revealing something genuine rather than exposing a gap.

## Current evidence status (honest, as of 2026-06-28)

The generation-sector campaign (CONSTRUCT-01..07) is the first round of evidence, and it leans toward the
hypothesis WITHOUT establishing it:

- SUPPORTS (boundary-preferring): the closed/internal routes are obstructed or under-determined -- every
  metric so(9,5) connection gives index 0 (C-05); the prime 3 is absent from the native spectrum (C-04);
  GU-native operators force an even index, so an odd count needs a non-quaternionic object FOREIGN to the
  internal algebra (C-07 -- but see Falsification log Round 1: this parity leg is REP-CONTINGENT and was
  WEAKENED); and the non-equivariant compensator was argued necessary via that parity route -- so "GU
  reaches outside its symmetry by parity" is no longer warranted as stated. These looked like the
  signatures of a system that terminates at an interface; round 1 showed the parity one is rep-specific.
- DOES NOT YET ESTABLISH: the count is UNDER-determined, not contradictory (C-06). Under-determination is
  consistent with BOTH "open by design (firewall)" AND "closed-completable by an (as-yet-unbuilt) internal
  object." A closed completion is obstructed for the routes tried, NOT proven impossible. Falsification
  criterion 1 is therefore genuinely live.
- STANDING CAVEAT: everything rests on the Cl(9,5)=M(64,H) reconstruction; criterion 4 (artifact of the
  rep choice) is the single most likely way the hypothesis dies.

So the current honest reading is: the mathematics has REPEATEDLY preferred a boundary object so far, but
not under enough independent reconstructions, and not against a serious closed-completion attempt, to call
the convergence genuine. That is precisely the work this hypothesis now organizes.

## Research philosophy

Treat this exactly like every prior hypothesis in this repository: do NOT defend it; attempt to KILL it.
If it survives repeated, serious elimination attempts, it earns increasing attention. If it fails, record
precisely why and replace it with the better explanation. This hypothesis supersedes the prior default
assumption that the correct endpoint of the reconstruction must necessarily be a closed mathematical
system; that assumption is now itself one of the things on trial.
