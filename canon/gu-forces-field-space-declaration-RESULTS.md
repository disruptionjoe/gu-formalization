# How much does GU force the completion of SG4?

The carrier-bit campaign established that the generation-count carrier (A vs B) is **not decided short
of SG4** -- the built source action's field-space declaration. This swing asks the sharper,
bounded question the Science Advisory Council endorsed over a free source-action build: **taking GU's
STATED commitments as premises, how much of the completion do they FORCE?** A free candidate-completion
would p-hack the carrier (you build the action that yields the bit you want). Instead: two independent
enumerations over the completion's field-space cells, with every commitment predeclared as an ALLOW
constraint (zero hard eliminations coded), asking only what the CONJUNCTION of commitments narrows.

## The verdict (both legs agree): TILT + RESIDUAL (B-leaning)

- **At GU-stated-commitments + published-theorem grade: TILT+RESIDUAL (B-leaning).**
- **At symbol grade: LEAVES-A-FAMILY.**
- **FORCES (a unique carrier) is UNATTAINABLE from GU-as-stated.**

GU's commitments do real work -- two genuine amputations and a strong one-directional tilt -- but they
do not close to a point. What is left is a **measured 2-bit residual**, and that residual is exactly SG4.

## The two real amputations (GU-as-stated genuinely rules these OUT)

1. **C1 "we will never find spacetime supersymmetry" amputates carrier A's generic form.** A's natural
   home is a 4d supergravity gravitino (ghost-subtracted, -42 = 21 sigma / 8). The generic 4d-SUGRA
   reading requires spacetime SUSY, which GU explicitly disavows. So A's *generic* SUGRA carrier is
   cut. (A is NOT fully cut -- see below: it survives via the graded-IG door, an upstairs route that
   "no spacetime SUSY" does not shut.)
2. **C4/C7 VZ-consistency amputates the uncured full/minimal/charged RS reading.** An uncured
   Rarita-Schwinger field of the full/minimal/charged type carries the Velo-Zwanziger acausality; GU's
   consistency commitments rule that reading out. VZ forces a three-branch fork
   {ker-Gamma cure (-> B) | gauge the constraint (-> A) | remain inconsistent}, NOT B uniquely.

## The tilt (B-leaning, ~6 commitments, ZERO counter-tilts)

Roughly six distinct GU commitments each texture the completion toward carrier B (the geometric
gamma-traceless -38 = 19 sigma / 8, order-3 class (0,2,1)/3): the geometric/tautological-field posture,
the "mass is a variable" modulus, the ker-Gamma cure being the geometrically natural VZ resolution, the
Nikulin order-3 equivariant channel living on the actual K3 geometry, and the anchor-scale graded-IG
algebra favoring the geometric slot. The honest transcript count is **3 clearly-B-textured vs 1
ambiguous-A**; NONE lean A. But a tilt is not a force: B's own cure requires unstated non-minimal
couplings, so B is not forced either.

## The residual (what GU-as-stated leaves genuinely open): a 2-bit SG4 freedom

The completion is not narrowed to a point but to a **2-dimensional residual** -- neither bit alone
forces a unique carrier:

- **Bit 1 -- invariance-selection:** does SG4 gauge the graded-IG scalar-spinor eps sub-slot? [this IS
  the SG4 declaration]
- **Bit 2 -- phase:** chiral/unbroken vs massive/super-Higgs (the "mass is a variable" modulus).

The four corners map **bijectively** onto the four known completions:

| invariance-selection | phase | carrier | value |
|---|---|---|---|
| graded-IG SUSY door OPEN | chiral | **A** | -42 |
| ker-Gamma cure | massive | **B** | -38 |
| (super-Higgs partial) | massive | -40 | -40 |
| ABSENT | chiral | **live inconsistency** | (uncured) |

The tie-breaker between these corners is exactly the escape-corners TRIPLE test applied to a BUILT
quadratic form (invariance-detection + phase reading). That is SG4. The residual is not a gap in the
analysis -- it is a precise measurement that **the residual IS the source action**.

## Why this is the honest terminus of GU-as-stated

This matches the canon carrier-bit headline verbatim: *"the bit is NOT decided ... short of SG4."* The
exhaustiveness swing already proved the DECIDER IS UNIQUE (nothing routes around SG4). This swing
measures HOW MUCH of the decider GU's own commitments pin down without building it: enough to amputate
two readings and tilt hard toward B, not enough to force. **GU-as-stated cannot force the carrier; it
narrows to a measured 2-bit residual = SG4, B-leaning.** Pushing past this point requires either
BUILDING SG4 (which risks p-hacking the carrier unless the build is itself forced) or a frame-change
(the records-as-rows reinterpretation, per the campaign historian) -- it cannot be extracted from
GU-as-stated by any further enumeration.
