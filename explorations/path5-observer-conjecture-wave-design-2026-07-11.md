## 1. Honest framing (scouting, not one-shot proof)

The conjecture's legs are genuine research problems -- modular theory in an indefinite (Krein) metric is
UNDERDEVELOPED, the filtration<->section map does not exist yet, and the Lawvere closure needs the partition and
agreement predicate made precise. A single swing will NOT prove the theorem. So each branch's deliverable is a
REACHABILITY MAP for its leg: what is constructible now, what needs new mathematics, what is blocked -- plus as
much of the construction as it can genuinely complete (with machine-checked pieces where possible). The wave's
output is: "is the first theorem within reach, or a longer campaign, and exactly what is the critical path?"

## 2. The four constructive legs (cross-shared branches)

**Branch A -- the Krein modular conjugation (the load-bearing assumption).** Set up modular theory for the
region's admissibility algebra in the INDEFINITE (Krein) metric and test whether the C-operator (the firewall,
W54) IS a Krein-space modular conjugation `J`: does it satisfy `J^2 = 1`, `J A J = A'` (commutant), and modular
covariance? Establish what the type of the algebra is (must be type III for the Bisognano-Wichmann horizon =
firewall picture). Reachability: how much of Tomita-Takesaki survives an indefinite metric? Cite the existing
Krein/PT-modular literature; identify the exact obstruction if it fails. THIS IS THE CRITICAL-PATH LEG -- the
whole conjecture rests on it.

**Branch B -- the filtration <-> section map (the missing map).** Construct (or characterize the obstruction to)
the map `{F_tau}` (an observer's record/information filtration) `<->` `Sect(Met(X^4))` (a source action = a
section of the metric-on-metrics). Is it an isomorphism (observer-states <-> source-actions)? What structure must
`{F_tau}` carry (a modular/Connes-cocycle flow?) to match a section? If it needs sibling-repo (temporal-issuance)
machinery, state exactly what. This is the map that turns "observer = source action" from words into a function.

**Branch C -- three generations from the firewall (the count leg, most checkable).** Prove (or test) that the
firewall's codimension-1 stratification forces EXACTLY THREE strata (interior / boundary / exterior = Individual /
Regional-holonic-sheaf / Global), that this caps the sheaf cohomology tower at 3, and that {1,3} = {no-firewall,
observer-present}. Check the concrete PREDICTION against the actual anomaly-inflow structure: is the MIDDLE (2nd)
generation the boundary-localized one? This leg is the most likely to yield a machine-checkable result and the
sharpest test of the whole picture (if the generation<->stratum signature is absent, the count leg fails).

**Branch D -- the Lawvere no-closure impossibility (the payoff theorem frame).** With A's firewall-as-partition
and B's admissibility algebra, set up the Lawvere point-surjection / fixed-point frame: the firewall grading-flip
as the fixpoint-free endomorphism, admissibility as the agreement predicate, self-reference as the diagonal.
Prove as much as possible of "no J-consistent closure across the firewall without a residual free selection," or
reduce it to precise lemmas on A and B. State what the theorem would give (in-principle-unforceability as a
theorem; path 4 postulate-P unconditional).

## 3. The falsifier (blind; presents, does not decide)

**Branch E -- the self-dual-square forcing test (the conjecture's own kill switch).** Blind to A-D. Try to show
the ARENA FORCES A VALUE -- specifically, does color-kinematics / Willmore uniqueness / the self-dual double-copy
force the count or the action norm onto carrier B (a value forced by the geometry alone)? If it does,
in-principle-unforceability is FALSE and the conjecture is refuted. Present the strongest case (strength-tagged);
the orchestrator weighs it against the constructive legs and decides. The conjecture PREDICTS this fails to force
-- so a genuine forcing here is the decisive falsification, and a failure-to-force is a (weak) corroboration.

## 5. Wave protocol + pre-registration
1. Deploy A-D (cross-shared) + E (blind), parallel.
2. Orchestrator synthesis (DECIDER, weighing E not vetoing): assemble the reachability map -- is the first theorem
   within reach? what is the critical path (expected: Branch A, the Krein modular conjugation)? does E's forcing

3. Design wave 2: the critical-path leg as a focused campaign, or -- if A is blocked (Krein modular theory
   obstructed) -- the honest verdict that the conjecture needs mathematics that does not yet exist (a real, if
   deflationary, result: "here is the precise missing mathematics").
Pre-registered outcomes:
- **A holds + B constructible + C's signature present + E fails to force:** the first theorem is within reach ->
  mount the campaign. The strongest outcome.

  precisely as the critical dependency (honest, still valuable).
- **E forces a value (self-dual-square):** the conjecture is FALSIFIED (the arena forces the value); "arena, not
  value" itself is wrong -> a major, clean result the other direction.
- **C's generation<->stratum signature absent:** the three-generations leg fails; the observer story survives but
  loses its most concrete anchor -> narrow the conjecture.
