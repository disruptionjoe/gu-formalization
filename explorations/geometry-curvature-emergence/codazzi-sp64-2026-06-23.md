## 3. The Full Codazzi Equation with j_s

```

  = R^{Y^14}_{ab c}{}^i
  + (pr_N(F^Psi))_{ab c}{}^i
```

where:
- The left side is the antisymmetrized covariant derivative of the second fundamental form
- R^{Y^14}_{ab c}{}^i is the ambient Riemann tensor component with one normal index
- pr_N(F^Psi)_{ab c}{}^i is the normal-projected Sp(64) gauge curvature

```

  = [rho_*(R^{Y^14})]_{ab c, j}
  + [F^Psi]_{ab, cj}
```

where:
- B_j = j_s(B): the Sp(64)-valued second fundamental form
- D_{a^0}: adjoint covariant derivative on ad(P_s) using the tautological connection
- [rho_*(R^{Y^14})]_{ab c, j}: the Sp(64)-curvature image of the ambient Riemann tensor
- [F^Psi]_{ab, cj}: the Sp(64) gauge curvature (the non-tautological part)

### 3.2 Contraction and the 4D Einstein equation

Contracting a with c in the Codazzi equation (using g^{ac}):

```
g^{ac}(D_{a^0} B_j)_{abc} - (D_{a^0} H_j)_b
```

The right side has two contributions:

**This is the full Codazzi equation for the Sp(64) bundle**: the pulled-back
