research-process advice. Five members reason independently from first
principles, then are ranked, then a chairman synthesizes. Each member gives

## Member 1 — Orthodox physicist (standard QFT/GR/geometry)

**Steelman.** Strip the romance and this is a **superselection sector label plus
a self-adjoint-extension question**, both textbook. σ is a genuine
superselection charge: two sectors, each with a positive-definite physical inner
product, no local observable connecting them — that is exactly Wick-Wightman-
Wigner, and "the theory doesn't fix which sector you're in" is the *normal*
status of a superselection label (like a global charge sign or a θ-vacuum
angle). θ is the phase of a self-adjoint extension of a symmetric operator on a
manifold with a noncompact end — von Neumann deficiency indices, Weyl's
limit-point/limit-circle, exactly the setting where "boundary conditions are
extra data the bulk operator doesn't fix." τ is a discrete moduli/index label.
Nothing here is outside the standard toolbox: the "missing piece" is the
ordinary fact that a differential operator plus a bundle does not fix its
superselection sector or its boundary condition — you supply those, or a
symmetry/anomaly argument fixes them.

**What comes through / what's outside / what it means.** Through the boundary
comes a finite label (a sector charge, a BC phase, a generation count). Outside
is the space of consistent completions — moduli of the theory. It MEANS GU, like
any gauge/geometry theory, has a moduli of admissible quantizations and the
physics picks a point; predictive content lives in what is *invariant* across
the moduli, and σ/θ/τ are the coordinates that aren't.

**How the horns move me.** If **θ dissolves** (Q1a-FORCED holds at the true
end), that is the *stronger* orthodox outcome, not a weaker one: essential
self-adjointness means the operator fixes its own domain, the "boundary freedom"
was a compact-collar artifact, and one of the three moduli coordinates simply
wasn't there — completely standard (limit-point is the generic case). If **θ is
real**, it's an honest `U(1)` of BCs, also standard. On **σ-supply**: orthodoxy
says a superselection label is never supplied from inside by construction — the
Q2-FREE verdict is the expected one and needs no exotic machinery; if the
defense-attorney pass found an internal handle that supplied σ, *that* would be
the surprise, and I'd bet against it.

**Three escalating questions.**
- **Q1 (grounded):** Compute the true-end deficiency indices of the actual
  `J`-symmetric `A~` in the `K_S` (Krein) metric — do they equal the Hilbert-model
  count, so that limit-point (unique domain, θ absent) is a *theorem* and not a
  sampled lean?
- **Q2 (cross-domain):** Is σ a genuine superselection charge with an
  associated conserved current and a `-1`-valued order operator, i.e. does it sit
  in a DHR-style sector analysis — and if so, what is its statistics/dimension,
  the standard invariants a superselection label carries?
- **Q3 (wild):** Is there any laboratory or cosmological observable *other than*
  the DE sign that rides σ — could two "sectors" of the same GU vacuum
  interfere given a suitable reference frame (Aharonov-Susskind), making the
  "unspeakable" bit operationally speakable after all?

**MODE: EXACT-AND-REAL-AND-TRUE** (the structures — superselection, deficiency
indices, discrete moduli — are literally what these objects are). **CONFIDENCE:
80%** — because the identification of each piece with a known structure is
tight and verifiable; the 20% doubt is that the *welding* of σ to a "record
arrow / first person" is not standard and I am bracketing it as interpretation,
not physics.

**What comes through / outside / means.** What comes through the boundary,

certify a particular bit. Outside is the value, and the philosophically careful

named falsifier**, not a closed theorem. It MEANS GU has produced a candidate
example of a physical theory locating an *indexical* necessity — but "indexical"
must be kept distinct from "chosen by a conscious observer"; the math supports the
former and is silent on the latter.

**How the horns move me.** If **θ dissolves**, that is a *nice epistemic
