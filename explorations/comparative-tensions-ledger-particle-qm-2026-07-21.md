# Comparative-tensions ledger — particle physics + QM

**What this is.** A fair, line-itemed comparison across the particle-physics + QM
cluster. For each rival theory: (1) its **earned keep** — what it *genuinely,
uniquely* predicts that is **confirmed / new / useful**, split into **CONFIRMED**
vs **CONTESTED/unconfirmed**; (2) its **tensions / anomalies / open problems /
failures**; (3) an **honest GU comparison** — `BETTER` / `TIES` / `SILENT` /
`WORSE` + one line of why.

**The discipline (non-negotiable, from the repo charter and the 2026-07-21
grades).** GU is **NARROW**. Given the two external data `sigma` (Z/2 orientation
bit) and `tau` (Z/3 generation datum) supplied and read correctly, GU **hosts**
most of physics at **consistency-to-hosting grade** — QM at hosted-with-one-import
(`R1_COND`), the SM at hosted-with-**six typed imports** (`R1`), GR vacuum
cancellation / cosmological perturbations / arrow at consistency-conditional
(`R0_COND`). **Nothing reaches Rung 2 (native forcing).** It has a **native but
NOT forced** generation count (`3 = dim Lambda^2_+`), and — as of 2026-07-21 — a
**conditional, proposal-grade "Dirac-forced"** neutrino reading. It does **NOT
out-predict the SM in the SM's own arena** (no cross-section, no g-2, no precision
electroweak observable is GU-derived). Every genuine GU win in the parsimony
ledger lives **off** this arena (the sign-cluster `4->1` bit collapse; the DE
no-phantom-crossing). This document does not move any of that; it consumes it.

**Verdict legend for column 3.** `BETTER` = GU renders non-brute / predicts
something the rival leaves brute or open, at equal coverage. `TIES` = comparable
standing (often: GU hosts the same structure without deriving it). `SILENT` = GU
does not address this joint (out of scope / needs-more) — an **uncharged
non-win**, never counted as a win. `WORSE` = the rival at least parametrizes or
predicts the thing and GU imports it or forces the wrong observable.

## 1. STANDARD MODEL + QM / QFT

*The most precisely tested theory in the history of science.*

### A. Earned keep

**CONFIRMED (the genuine, unique, load-bearing wins):**
- **QED to ~12 significant figures.** Electron anomalous magnetic moment `a_e`,
  Lamb shift, hydrogen/positronium spectra — agreement at the `10^-12` level. The
  single most precise theory-vs-experiment match ever achieved.
- **Electroweak unification, predicted-then-found.** `W` and `Z` boson masses
  predicted before discovery (1983); neutral currents (1973); the entire Z-pole
  precision program (LEP/SLC) fit to `10^-3`–`10^-4`.
- **The Higgs boson**, predicted (1964) and found at 125 GeV (2012) — with the
  right couplings scaling with mass, as required.
- **Predicted-then-found particles:** charm, top, tau, gluon, tau-neutrino; three
  fermion generations organized.
- **QCD asymptotic freedom** (deep-inelastic scaling, jets, running `alpha_s`) and
  the full LHC cross-section program — consistent to date across many orders of
  magnitude in rate.
- **CP violation** in the quark sector (kaons, B-mesons) via the CKM phase;
  CKM unitarity over-constrained and consistent.

**CONTESTED / receding anomalies (NOT clean wins, NOT clean failures):**
- **Muon `g-2`.** The Fermilab measurement vs the SM prediction — but as the
  hadronic-vacuum-polarization input moved to lattice QCD (BMW et al.) and CMD-3
  `e+e-` data, the **tension has substantially receded** (the 2025 theory
  consensus is much closer to experiment). A moving, contested number, not an
  established BSM signal.
- **B-meson flavor anomalies** (`R_K`, `R_D(*)`, angular observables): several have
  **regressed toward the SM** with more data; a few `R_D(*)` hints persist —
  contested, not established.

### B. Tensions / failures / open

- **Hierarchy / naturalness** — the Higgs mass is quadratically sensitive to any
  high scale; no SM reason it sits at 125 GeV.
- **Strong-CP** — `theta_QCD < ~10^-10` with no dynamical reason (an unexplained
  small number / near-exact accidental CP in QCD).
- **~19 free parameters** (minimal SM), **~26–28** once neutrino masses+mixings and
  `theta_QCD` are included — all fitted, none derived.
- **Flavor puzzle** — charged-fermion masses span ~6 orders of magnitude; the
  mixing-angle patterns (near-diagonal CKM, large PMNS) are unexplained.
- **Neutrino masses + mixings** — require physics **beyond** the minimal SM
  (massless-neutrino SM is falsified by oscillations; see §2).
- **No gravity** — no quantum-gravity sector.
- **No dark-matter candidate**; **no dark-energy** account.
- **Metastable vacuum** — measured top/Higgs masses place the EW vacuum near /
  just inside the metastability boundary.
- **Baryogenesis** — SM CP violation is far too small for the observed
  matter–antimatter asymmetry.

### C. GU vs the SM+QM/QFT — per-joint

| SM joint / arena | GU verdict | one-line why |
|---|---|---|
| Precision observables (g-2, EW fits, QED, cross-sections) | **WORSE** | GU derives **none** of them; it hosts SM content with 6 typed imports and predicts nothing in-arena |
| QM foundational sign (Krein superselection) | **TIES** | hosted at `R1_COND` (one typed import); positive sector appears for the right orientation — a fit given `sigma`, not a derivation |
| Hierarchy / naturalness | **SILENT** | OUT-OF-SCOPE in the parsimony ledger; GU offers nothing |
| Strong-CP (`theta_QCD`) | **SILENT (needs-more)** | the proposal-grade `w1` reflection anomaly is the same T-odd sector but explicitly does **NOT** pin `theta_QCD -> 0` (an anomalous T does not enforce the T-symmetric vacuum; `theta` tracks `sigma`) |
| Flavor puzzle (masses/mixings) | **WORSE** | masses/mixings **imported**; the two natural native routes (mu-tau Weyl on `Lambda^2_+`; Krein-on-family) are **banked-obstructed**; only "generation universality" survives, which forces degeneracy — the wrong observable |

and **WORSE** (flavor).

## 2. NEUTRINO SECTOR

*The one laboratory-confirmed crack in the SM.*

### A. Earned keep

**CONFIRMED (genuine, first laboratory BSM physics):**
- **Neutrino oscillations => neutrinos have mass and mix** (Super-Kamiokande
  atmospheric 1998; SNO solar 2001; KamLAND; Daya Bay `theta_13` 2012). This is
  the **only** direct laboratory evidence of physics beyond the minimal SM.
- **Three mixing angles** (`theta_12`, `theta_23`, `theta_13`) and **two
  mass-squared splittings** (`Delta m^2_21`, `|Delta m^2_31|`) measured.

**CONTESTED / open (measured-but-not-settled, or not yet observed):**
- **Majorana vs Dirac** nature — unknown; hinges on neutrinoless double-beta decay
  (`0nubb`), **not observed** (only lower limits on the half-life).
- **Mass ordering** (normal vs inverted) — mild preference for normal, not settled.
- **CP phase `delta_CP`** — hints (T2K/NOvA) but **not** established at discovery
  significance.
- **Absolute mass scale** — only bounds (KATRIN kinematic; cosmology on `sum m_nu`).
- **Sterile neutrinos** (LSND/MiniBooNE) — largely disfavored, contested.

### B. Tensions / failures / open

- Dirac vs Majorana unresolved (the `0nubb` question).
- Ordering, `delta_CP`, absolute scale all open.
- **Why the masses are so tiny** (`< ~0.1 eV`) — part of the flavor puzzle; seesaw
  and leptogenesis remain speculative.

### C. GU vs the neutrino sector

| neutrino joint | GU verdict | one-line why |
|---|---|---|
| Existence of oscillations / mass | **SILENT (hosts)** | neutrino mass enters via the imported flavor/Yukawa block; GU adds nothing native |
| Mixing angles, ordering, `delta_CP`, scale | **WORSE** | all imported; native flavor routes banked-obstructed (as in §1) |
| **Dirac vs Majorana** | **TIES / conditional-BETTER-in-principle** | GU offers a **conditional, proposal-grade "Dirac-forced" reading** the SM leaves open: Pin+ `T^2=(-1)^F` => Kramers/**pseudoreal** carrier => a **symmetric Majorana mass is FORBIDDEN => Dirac forced** (0nubb ABSENT), consistent with `S=H^64`, `J^2=-1`. **But** it is `needs-more`, **source-gated** on the physical-`nu` <-> quaternionic-carrier identification, and may **reduce to the un-forced 126-vs-16 route** |

**Row summary — GU vs neutrino sector: TIES (one conditional edge).** GU is
SILENT/WORSE on every already-measured neutrino quantity. Its **single**
particle-physics-facing original item is the conditional, proposal-grade
**Dirac-forced** reading (predicting `0nubb` **absent**) — a *genuine but ungraded*
directional edge the SM does not attempt, and one that would sit in **mild tension
with the SO(10) 126-Higgs Majorana seesaw** (see §4). It is a slot to watch, not a
banked win.

## 3. SUSY / MSSM

*A framework built to solve the SM's tensions — not (yet) seen.*

### A. Earned keep

**CONFIRMED: none.** No superpartner has been observed. SUSY's "keep" is a set of
**theoretical virtues / consistency successes**, not confirmed predictions:
- **Stabilizes the hierarchy problem** — cancels the quadratic Higgs-mass
  divergence (its founding motivation).
- **Gauge-coupling unification** — the three SM couplings meet far more precisely
  at `~2x10^16 GeV` in the MSSM than in the bare SM (SUSY's best circumstantial
  evidence — a genuine quantitative near-hit, but circumstantial).
- **A dark-matter candidate** — the lightest neutralino (LSP) as a WIMP, if
  R-parity is conserved.
- **Light-Higgs consistency** — the MSSM bounds `m_h <~ 135 GeV`; the observed
  125 GeV falls in range (a retrodictive success).
- Radiative electroweak symmetry breaking.

### B. Tensions / failures / open

- **Not seen at the LHC** — squarks/gluinos excluded to `~2 TeV+`; the "natural"
  parameter space is largely gone => **fine-tuning returns** (the little-hierarchy
  problem SUSY was meant to remove).
- **125 GeV Higgs needs heavy/mixed stops** — itself in tension with naturalness.
- **Direct DM detection** (LZ, XENONnT) has excised much neutralino-WIMP space.
- **~105 new free parameters** in the MSSM (soft-breaking sector).
- **SUSY flavor/CP problem** — generic soft terms overproduce FCNCs and EDMs;
  needs alignment/degeneracy.
- **Proton decay** via dimension-5 operators in SUSY GUTs — constrained.

### C. GU vs SUSY

| SUSY joint | GU verdict | one-line why |
|---|---|---|
| Superpartner spectrum | **SILENT** | GU predicts no superpartners; it neither competes nor is refuted here |
| Hierarchy stabilization | **SILENT** | GU is OUT-OF-SCOPE on the hierarchy problem — the very thing SUSY exists to solve |
| Gauge unification | **TIES/WORSE** | GU hosts the SO(10) chain but makes **no** quantitative coupling-unification prediction; SUSY does |
| DM candidate | **SILENT (both strained)** | GU's mirror/DM route is `RESOLVED_NO_GO` / out of scope; SUSY had a candidate now under direct-detection pressure |
| Parameter economy | **BETTER (narrowly)** | GU's honest knob count (~14) is far below the MSSM's ~105 — but partly because GU **covers less** |

**Row summary — GU vs SUSY: SILENT (on SUSY's home turf).** SUSY genuinely
*addresses* joints (hierarchy, unification, a DM candidate) that GU is **SILENT**
on — but every SUSY solution is now under heavy empirical strain and costs ~105
unseen parameters. GU makes **no** falsified prediction here and imports fewer
knobs; neither out-predicts the other. This is a case where the rival tries where
GU declines, and the rival's attempt is unconfirmed.

## 4. GUTs — SO(10), SU(5)

*The cluster where GU is most directly comparable — because GU HOSTS the SO(10)
structure.*

### A. Earned keep

**CONFIRMED / genuine organizing successes:**
- **Charge quantization** — electric charge is quantized because it is a generator
  of a simple group (explains `Q_p = -Q_e` exactly). Observed. (Not uniquely GUT,
  but a clean success.)
- **Fermion unification** — SO(10) packs a **full SM generation + right-handed
  neutrino into one 16 spinor**; anomaly-free; fixes the hypercharge assignments.
  A genuine, elegant organizing win.
- **Neutrino-mass smallness via seesaw** — SO(10) naturally contains `nu_R`, giving
  the type-I seesaw that **postdicts** the tiny observed neutrino masses.
- Partial **`b`–`tau` Yukawa unification**.

**CONTESTED / unconfirmed / refuted:**
- **Proton decay** — predicted (`p -> e+ pi^0` etc.) but **NOT observed**
  (Super-K: `tau_p > ~1.6x10^34 yr`). This **rules out minimal non-SUSY SU(5)**;
  SO(10) and SUSY-GUTs survive only at longer lifetimes.
- **Gauge unification** — minimal SU(5) does **not** quite unify without SUSY.
- **Magnetic monopoles** — predicted, not seen (inflation dilutes them; not a clean
  refutation).

### B. Tensions / failures / open

- **Proton decay not seen** — minimal SU(5) dead; the flagship prediction remains
  unconfirmed for all variants.
- **Doublet-triplet splitting** — keeping the Higgs doublet light while the color
  triplet is superheavy is a fine-tuning.
- **Minimal SU(5)** fails quantitative unification.
- Many fermion-sector parameters remain free.

### C. GU vs GUTs

| GUT joint | GU verdict | one-line why |
|---|---|---|
| Fermion organization (16 of SO(10)) | **TIES (hosts)** | GU **uses** this exact structure: "all 16 Spin(10)->G_SM chains R0-equivalent, exact SM content per 16, anomalies zero, chirality genuine" — but the selector is **imported**, not derived |
| Charge quantization / hypercharge | **TIES (hosts, bought)** | inherited via the chain; `sin^2 = 3/8` hypercharge normalization is **BOUGHT**, not earned |
| Gauge unification (quantitative) | **WORSE** | GU makes no coupling-unification prediction; the GUT does |
| Breaking chain + Higgs + spectrum | **WORSE** | these are exactly GU's **6 typed imports**; the native selector is `BOUNDED_NO_GO` |
| **Proton decay** | **SILENT** | GU commits to no GUT-scale breaking dynamics, so it predicts **no** proton-decay rate — neither refuted (like minimal SU(5)) nor confirmed. A coverage gap, not a win |
| Neutrino seesaw (126-Higgs Majorana) | **conditional TENSION** | GU's proposal-grade **Dirac-forced** reading (§2) would **conflict** with the standard SO(10) 126-Majorana seesaw — a genuine, testable divergence *if* GU's reading banks |

**Row summary — GU vs GUTs: TIES (a hosting layer on SO(10)).** GU literally
**hosts** the SO(10) 16-spinor organizing win — charge quantization, anomaly
freedom, genuine chirality — but via **6 typed imports**, deriving none of it. It
makes **no** proton-decay prediction (SILENT: not refuted like minimal SU(5), but
uncovered), and its conditional Dirac-forced neutrino reading is in **mild tension**
with the SO(10) 126-Majorana seesaw. GU is best read here as a *reconstruction
layer that consumes the SO(10) success*, not a rival GUT.

## 5. Synthesis — where GU sits in the particle-physics + QM cluster

**One-line honest standing.** *In the arena of particle physics, GU does not
compete: it HOSTS the SM / SO(10) content at consistency grade with ~6 typed
imports, predicts NONE of the precision observables that make the SM the
most-tested theory in history, and on the SM's own deepest puzzles it is SILENT
(hierarchy), SILENT/needs-more (strong-CP), and WORSE (flavor). Its one genuinely
particle-facing original item is a conditional, proposal-grade "Dirac-forced"
neutrino reading (0nubb ABSENT) that the SM leaves open — a real but ungraded
potential edge. Every other GU advantage lives OFF this arena — the sign-cluster
4->1-bit parsimony collapse and the DE no-phantom-crossing falsifiable, both
cosmological/foundational, not collider.*

**Does GU offer anything on the four questions the brief flags?**

- **Strong-CP:** **No, beyond "needs-more."** The `w1` reflection ('t Hooft)
  anomaly touches the same T-odd sector, but `operator-grade-anomaly-banking`
  is explicit that it does **NOT** pin `theta_QCD -> 0` — an anomalous T does not
  enforce the T-symmetric vacuum; `theta` tracks `sigma`. GU declines the
  over-claim. **SILENT / needs-more.**
- **Hierarchy:** **Nothing.** OUT-OF-SCOPE in the parsimony ledger. **SILENT.**
- **Flavor:** **Worse than the SM's parametrization.** Masses/mixings are
  imported; the two natural native routes (mu-tau Weyl on `Lambda^2_+`;
  Krein-on-family) are **banked-obstructed**; the only survivor is "generation
  universality" (identical `(+32,-32,0)` signatures) which **forces degeneracy** —
  the *opposite* of the observed hierarchy. The generation count `3 = dim
  Lambda^2_+` is a **native value but not forced**. **WORSE**, with one weak
  parsimony flavor (the native-3).
- **Neutrinos beyond today's findings:** **One conditional edge, and only one.**
  The proposal-grade, source-gated **Dirac-forced** reading (0nubb absent) is the
  single GU item that points at a particle-physics measurement the SM leaves open.
  It is `needs-more`, may collapse to the un-forced 126-vs-16 route, and sits in
  tension with the SO(10) seesaw — so it is a **watch-item, not a banked win**.

**The honest shape of GU's standing in this cluster.** GU is a **narrow hosting /
reconstruction layer**, not a competitor to the SM, SUSY, or the GUTs *as
predictive engines in their own arena*. Where the rivals earn their keep — SM on
precision observables, GUTs on fermion organization and charge quantization, SUSY
on the hierarchy/unification/DM package (unconfirmed) — GU either **hosts the same
structure without deriving it** (SM content, SO(10) 16-spinor) or is **SILENT**
(hierarchy, DM, superpartners, proton decay). Its genuine explanatory contribution
is a **parsimony** one — collapsing four separately-posited signs (QM Krein, GR
vacuum, DE, arrow) into one external bit `sigma` — and it is **off** the
particle-physics arena, cashing out as a single observable (the DE
no-phantom-crossing) plus a proposal-grade parity-cosmology second leg. Inside
particle physics proper, GU's *only* forward-looking, non-imported, potentially
distinctive item is the conditional Dirac-forced neutrino reading. Everything else
is imported, hosted, or silent. **No GU inflation: it does not out-predict the SM
where the SM is strong, and it does not solve the puzzles SUSY and the GUTs were
built to solve.**
