## 2. Persona 1 -- coupled-system field theorist: the backreaction m^2, sign and ceiling

**The sign is right (BR1).** Couple the record matter to the scalaron the way any
matter couples in an induced/`f(R)` gravity: in the Einstein frame a non-relativistic
record-matter density `rho` enters the scalaron's effective potential as
`V_eff(phi) = V(phi) + rho A(phi)` with `A(phi) = e^{beta phi}`, `beta = 1/sqrt(6) > 0`.
The effective mass is `m_eff^2 = V_{,phiphi} + rho beta^2 A(phi)`, and the matter term
`rho beta^2 A(phi)` is STRICTLY POSITIVE. So the record-matter backreaction pushes the
effective mass UP: the chameleon sign is exactly the stabilizing sign the lens hoped
for. This is the honest pro-completion result, and it is computed, not asserted.

**But the density-dependent-mass CHANNEL is structurally absent (BR2, the decisive
finding).** The standard f(R) chameleon does its real work not through the direct
`rho A` term but by pushing the background curvature `R` to high values in high-density
regions, so that the vacuum scalaron mass `m^2(R) = (1/3)(f'/f'' - R)` RISES with
density. That mechanism requires `f''(R)` to VARY with `R` -- i.e. curvature structure
beyond `R^2`. GU has none: W126 proved the induced potential sector is EXACTLY
quadratic (`c_3 = c_4 = ... = 0`), so `f'' = -2/9` is CONSTANT, and

```
m^2(R) = (1/3)(f'/f'' - R) = a1/(6 a2) = -1/2 ,   dm^2/dR = 0 .
```

The scalaron mass is DENSITY-INDEPENDENT. High record density does not raise it. The
very property that makes the tachyon robust and unliftable (the exact degree-2
potential, the same fact W126 used to kill the beyond-4th-order and loop-`R^3` rescues)
is ALSO what disables the chameleon density-mass channel. This is the wave's new
structural content: the left-out ingredient cannot help through the channel that
usually does the lifting, because GU's own no-higher-curvature theorem forbids it.

**And the residual direct-coupling lift is ~120 OOM too weak (BR3).** The only
surviving matter contribution is the direct `rho beta^2 A` term. Its magnitude is set
by the ambient record-matter density, which cosmologically is `rho ~ H^2 M_pl^2`, so
the lift is `~ beta^2 H^2` in field units. The tachyon lives at the induced-gravity
scale `mu` (the one free dimensionful scale `mu_DW`, SA-G2 / H36): `|m_vac^2| ~ mu^2`.
The ratio is `(H/mu)^2`:

```
mu = M_pl :   (H0/M_pl)^2   = 3.5e-121
mu = mu_DW:   (H0/mu_DW)^2  = 3.9e-61     (mu_DW = 2.3 meV, the DE scale)
```

Right sign, hopelessly small. To make the lift COMPETE one must set `mu ~ H` -- which
is precisely the W138 G5 de Sitter relabeling (content-free, a novelty kill), not a
stabilization. So persona 1: **backreaction sign POSITIVE, magnitude IR-inert, and the
density-mass channel structurally absent.**

## 3. Persona 2 -- driven-dissipative / control theorist: does the one-way promotion place the pole?

The control-systems hope (W155 persona 4A): `m_0^2 < 0` is a right-half-plane pole, and
an open-loop RHP pole is not a verdict -- a feedback loop can move it into the LHP. The
measurement-gated promotion (records get confirmed, a one-way process) is the candidate
stabilizing loop. Now with the ACTUAL coupling, not a toy.

**The bandwidth fails by 30 to 60 orders (DD1).** A single real RHP pole at growth rate
`gamma = |m_0|` is stabilizable only if the feedback loop gain-bandwidth product exceeds
`gamma` (the Bode gain-bandwidth limitation, PORTED). The tachyon e-folds at the induced
scale, `gamma ~ mu`. The promotion feedback clock is the record-promotion rate: W160
measured the frontier fluctuation correlation length at `~0.58` e-fold of the count
clock, i.e. roughly ONE promotion per Hubble time, so the loop bandwidth is
`omega_fb ~ H`. The maximum per-e-fold accretion gain is `g = 3.1` (W143). The pole
displacement the loop can deliver is `Delta ~ g omega_fb ~ 3.1 H`, and

```
gamma/H = mu/H = 1.6e30 (mu = mu_DW)  to  1.7e60 (mu = M_pl) ,   vs  gain 3.1 .
```

The feedback is 30 to 60 orders too SLOW to catch the pole. This is the same scale
mismatch as persona 1, now in the time domain: an IR (Hubble-rate) loop cannot move a
UV (induced-scale) pole.

**And the one-way promotion is a DRIVE, not a restoring feedback (DD2).** The promotion
process is MONOTONE (`N` only accretes -- this is time's arrow, W151). The mean it
sources, `Lambda = c/sqrt(N)`, is therefore monotone DECREASING (`Q_mean = dLambda/dt < 0`
for `Ndot > 0`) -- W154's withdrawal obstruction, reproduced here. A monotone process
has no oscillatory / restoring component at the pole frequency; it is overdamped. Worse
for the completion story: the record-accretion drive rolls the scalaron DOWN the hilltop
(roll = record accretion, W155 persona 1A), which points the SAME way as the instability.
So the coupled record dynamics is a destabilizing drive, not a stabilizing feedback.
Persona 2: **driven-dissipative stabilization DOES-NOT.** Shown, not asserted -- it fails
the bandwidth ledger and the process is monotone.

## 6. Persona 5 -- adversarial skeptic: no-free-lunch and EFT validity

**Steelman "the coupling does not stabilize."** The pro-completion case wants either a

control-systems no-free-lunch, and it is the same ledger as the bandwidth bound.

range, and the record-accretion drive rolls the field DOWN the hilltop. So where the
coupling acts, it tends to DRIVE the runaway rather than restore it. Honest call: the
coupling is neither a robust lift (IR-negligible) nor a guaranteed worsening -- it is
IR-inert either way; the tachyon's fate is set by the pure-gravity sector, exactly as
the prior waves found.

**Where the skeptic must stop.** None of this KILLS GU; it kills the completion story.
The backreaction is a real, correctly-signed effect that is simply the wrong size and
