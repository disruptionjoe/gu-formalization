their own substrate is the correct fundamental ontology of reality** and builds the
most coherent, committed, strongly-opinionated version of the FULL story from that

no analogy-vs-real ratings, no hedging. The constructions are NOT synthesized and

- The one banked shadow: the relative sign `c = σ_R1·σ_R2` is a product-of-odds =

class of the arrow-of-time line bundle over `F ≃ RP³` (the `S³→RP³` spin double

The null stratum is a **genuine boundary/horizon**. The `q<0`, `K_S`-null ends are

degenerates — a Cauchy-horizon-like or caustic locus. This is the completely
