# The WHAT-IS-THIS wave

(w₁(L_time) = Čech gluing obstruction = loop fixed-point count), tied to the record

## Structure (Joe's spec)
A WAVE of 4 sub-swings. EACH is ONE sub-agent running MULTIPLE PERSONAS INLINE
(members reason independently in one worker → synthesize; NEVER one agent per persona).
Each swing asks "what IS this [facet], really?" and delivers: (a) each member's
characterization from their lens; (b) a synthesized deepest characterization; (c) each
claim tagged ANALOGY vs EXACT-AND-REAL; (d) any INDEPENDENT consequence or productive
next-step (not just philosophy — a lemma, a test, a construction handle). Discipline:
exploratory but HONEST — flag analogy vs exact, never over-claim a characterization as
forced, keep any posit labeled, ground in the banked facts.

- **Swing 1 — THE LEMMA (highest leverage: everything converges here).** What IS the
  operator-grade base-uniting map (F↔S³) / product-uniformity theorem, and how do we
  approach it? Inline team: (i) Krein–Mourre operator theorist, (ii) APS / index-theory
  / spectral-flow theorist, (iii) differential geometer (the F=GL(4,R)/O(3,1) ↔ S³

  Deliver: what the lemma IS, its cleanest proof route, a tractable sub-case, and what
  it unlocks (it gates the pencil theorem, shard-Z/3=trit, the two-time z*, leg-a).

- **Swing 2 — THE BIT (ontology of σ).** What IS σ, most fundamentally? Inline team:
  (i) information theorist (1 external bit / oracle), (ii) spin/Clifford geometer
  (w₁ / holonomy / belt-trick), (iii) philosopher of physics (the arrow of time /
  record direction), (iv) quantum-foundations (superselection charge / einselection).
  Deliver: the deepest characterization — is σ "the direction of time," "a spin
  obstruction," "a superselection charge," "an oracle bit" — and which framings are

- **Swing 3 — THE OUTSIDE (the boundary and what's beyond it).** What IS the boundary

  boundary IS and what "outside" means — a bigger universe, a final boundary, an oracle,
  or nothing — and whether any reading yields a downstream test.

- **Swing 4 — THE PROCESS (the record-issuance dynamics).** What IS the record-issuance

  (iii) consensus theorist (issuance = ordering/finalization), (iv) process philosopher

  and any independent consequence (a rate relation, a finality invariant).
