comparative-tensions ledger. Each rival TOE-candidate gets three columns:

## 1. String / M-theory

**(1) Genuine unique contribution.**
- *CONTESTED / structural (real, not empirical TOE confirmation):* a
  **consistent perturbative quantum gravity** (UV-soft graviton amplitudes);
  gravity arises *necessarily*, not by hand; **AdS/CFT** (Maldacena 1997) — a
  deep, heavily-used gauge/gravity holographic duality that has paid off far
  outside string theory (QCD-adjacent, condensed matter, quantum-information);
  **microscopic black-hole entropy** `S = A/4` counted for extremal BHs
  (Strominger-Vafa 1996); large pure-math yield (mirror symmetry, dualities).
  These are genuine intellectual products.
- *CONFIRMED distinctive prediction as a TOE:* **none.** No string-specific
  number has been measured; SUSY, the framework's most natural handle, is
  **unseen at the LHC**.

**(2) Tensions / failures / open.** The **landscape** (`~10^500` metastable
vacua, no unique low-energy world) dissolves predictivity into anthropics; **SUSY
absent** at accessible energy; the **non-falsifiability critique** (Woit, Smolin)
bites precisely because the landscape can re-fit almost anything; moduli
stabilization is not unique; the Swampland program is a conjectural attempt to
claw predictivity back.

**(3) GU vs string — SPLIT.**
- *Parsimony / joints:* **BETTER** — the parsimony ledger (Sec 6) grades
  string's `~10^500`-vacua **selection debit** as the **worst brute-facts-per-
  explanation of the whole set**: it "addresses" the most but explains the least.
  GU's ~14 imported knobs + one selection bit leave **fewer joints brute at equal
  coverage**.
- *Falsifiability:* **BETTER** — GU has one cheap, rigid kill test (DE
  no-crossing); the landscape structurally *lacks* a single gun.
- *Worked QG machinery:* **WORSE** — string has an actual perturbative
  S-matrix and AdS/CFT; GU's "one arena" is architectural + an `O(M^2)`
  vacuum-*cancellation* identity (per-leg leg 4, `R0_COND`), **not** a quantized
  graviton amplitude or a holographic duality. On the depth of the QG
  construction, string is ahead.

Immirzi parameter** is a free input tuned to BH entropy; **matter / the SM is not

**(3) GU vs LQG — SPLIT.**
- *TOE coverage:* **BETTER** — LQG says nothing about the gauge group,
  generations, or the DE sector; GU at least **hosts** the SM content (per-leg

## 4. Causal set theory

**The standout genuine ahead-of-data prediction in the whole cluster — credited
in full.**

**(1) Genuine unique contribution.**
- *CONFIRMED (rare, genuine, ahead of data):* **Sorkin's prediction of a small,

  volume gives `Lambda ~ 10^-120` in Planck units — **of order the observed

  discovery** of accelerating expansion. This is a genuine order-of-magnitude
  **prediction that roughly matched later observation** — the cluster's crown
  jewel, and rarer than anything string/LQG/GU has produced.
- *CONFIRMED / structural:* **built-in Lorentz invariance** (discreteness with no
  preferred frame — consistent with all null LIV searches by construction).

**(2) Tensions / failures / open.** **Dynamics incomplete** — classical
sequential growth exists, but a full quantum measure / path-sum over causal sets
is unfinished; **no worked matter sector**; **phenomenology otherwise thin**
beyond `Lambda` and Lorentz invariance; the **everpresent-`Lambda`** model
predicts `Lambda` *fluctuating in sign*, which carries its own tensions with data
and needs further model-building; recovering continuum geometry / locality (the
Hauptvermutung / non-locality issue) is subtle.

**(3) GU vs causal sets — SPLIT on the `Lambda` axis (the sharp comparison).**
- *`Lambda` MAGNITUDE:* **WORSE** — causal sets genuinely **predicted the order

## 5. Other TOE candidates (brief, fair)

### 5a. Twistor theory / scattering amplitudes

**(1)** *CONFIRMED-useful (structural):* **on-shell methods, BCFW recursion, the
amplituhedron** (Arkani-Hamed-Trnka) — reformulate scattering amplitudes (esp.
`N=4` SYM) as positive geometry, trivializing computations intractable by Feynman
diagrams; these feed **real collider predictions** and recast
locality/unitarity as emergent. Genuine and used. *As a TOE:* **no distinctive
empirical prediction.**
**(2)** Primarily a **reformulation / computational framework**, not a complete
TOE; **gravity is harder** (double-copy/KLT is a partial bridge); Penrose's
original twistor-TOE stalled on the **"googly problem"** (full nonlinear /
anti-self-dual gravity).
**(3) GU vs twistor/amplitudes — SILENT (orthogonal).** Amplitudes is a
computational reformulation that selects no gauge group and says nothing about
cosmology; GU has no on-shell machinery. They answer different questions —
**neither better nor worse**; not genuine rivals.

### 5b. E8 theories (Lisi, "An Exceptionally Simple TOE")

**(1)** *Aspiration only:* embed SM gauge group + gravity + three generations in a
single **E8** connection — a "one group" unification aesthetically parallel to
GU's "one geometry." Spawned useful rep-theory scrutiny. **No confirmed
prediction.**
**(2) Largely REFUTED:** **Distler-Garibaldi (2009)** proved you **cannot embed
three chiral generations** in E8 this way — any such embedding necessarily drags
in **non-chiral (mirror) fermions**; you cannot reproduce the SM's chiral
content. Widely regarded as a decisive obstruction; predicted particles never
appeared.
**(3) GU vs E8 — BETTER (instructive parallel).** E8 was **killed by a
chirality / mirror no-go**. GU has confronted **exactly this class** honestly:
its class-level result is that the observerse is **intrinsically vectorlike and
cannot force its own chirality / generation count from inside** (linear leg
theorem-grade; README Purpose), so it **externalizes** the count (`sigma`, `tau`)
rather than claiming to derive it. GU **internalized the very no-go that killed
E8** and set its claim to match — the more disciplined version of the "one
object" dream. **BETTER on epistemic hygiene** about the shared obstruction.

### 5c. Connes-Chamseddine non-commutative geometry (spectral SM) — GU's closest structural competitor

**(1) Genuine unique contribution — the strongest SM-structure rival.**
- *CONFIRMED (postdiction, genuine and deep):* from a **spectral triple**
  (a finite noncommutative algebra `x` the spacetime manifold) and the **spectral
  action principle**, Connes-Chamseddine **DERIVE the full SM gauge group**
  `SU(3)xSU(2)xU(1)`, the **fermion content with correct hypercharges** (including

  connection in the discrete/noncommutative direction** — the Higgs is *unified
  with the gauge sector geometrically*. This is a real **derivation of SM
  STRUCTURE from geometry** — precisely GU's kind of "one geometric arena" claim,
  and the strongest such rival.
- *FALSIFIED-then-patched (a genuine falsifiable number):* the first-form spectral
  unification relation predicted **`m_H ~ 170 GeV`**, later **excluded** by
  Tevatron/LHC; Connes et al. then added structure (a real scalar `sigma` / the

**(2) Tensions / failures / open.** **Euclidean signature** (the framework is
naturally Euclidean; Lorentzian NCG is hard) — a real foundational tension; the
first Higgs-mass prediction was **wrong**, and the fix **reduced predictivity**;
**Yukawas / fermion masses are still imported**; the choice of finite algebra is
partly input; the spectral action is **classical/effective — no complete quantum

**(3) GU vs NCG — SPLIT; this is the closest rivalry, and NCG leads on its axis.**
- *SM-STRUCTURE (the axis GU wants to win):* **WORSE** — NCG **derives** the gauge
  group + hypercharges + Higgs-as-connection from a spectral triple; GU only
