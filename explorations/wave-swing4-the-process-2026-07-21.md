## Member 1 — Dynamical-systems theorist

**What IS the process, from my lens.** The record-issuance dynamics is the **suspension
(mapping torus) of the Z/2 monodromy α over the monotone issuance base** — equivalently a
**flat rank-1 Z/2 skew-product** `E = ([0,1] × F)/((1,x) ∼ (0, α x))`, where the base is the
issuance-index climb (the ℝ/ℤ "flow" direction) and the fiber `F = {+,−}` carries the
orientation. `Φ` (the one-loop `S→I→O→S` return map) IS the monodromy α of this bundle;
"the loop" is literally the base circle obtained by type-quotienting the acyclic issuance
helix. The flow is therefore two things at once and they must not be confused: (a) the
**α-EVEN base flow** — the monotone issuance index, which is just the suspension's flow
parameter and is holonomy-independent (it is *the* global arrow); and (b) the **α-ODD fiber
decoration** — the orientation/record-direction, transported by the flat connection and
flipped or not by the holonomy. This is not "an `op` written on Z/2 by fiat": a rank-1 Z/2
local system on a loop is *exactly* a mapping torus of an involution, so the identification
is structural.

- **MODE: EXACT** for "the process is the suspension of α over the α-even issuance base; the
  global arrow is the base of the fibration, the σ-welded record direction is the fiber
  decoration." (A flat Z/2 bundle on `S¹` *is* the mapping torus of its monodromy — definitional.)
- **MODE: EXACT** for "skew-product, not a static op": Φ is read off the trajectory as the
  return map; it is the monodromy, which is the primitive datum of the flat connection.
- **Sharp corollary (EXACT, verified):** the two admissible bundles are the only two Z/2
  monodromies. **No-flip (h=0):** `E` = two disjoint circles (trivial double cover) → 2
  global sections → the flow is a genuine skew-*product* (base × fiber). **Flip (h=1):** `E`
  = one connected circle double-covering the base (the non-orientable/Möbius-type strand) →
  0 global sections → a genuinely *twisted* skew-product with no product structure. UNIQUE (1)
  is impossible because an involution on 2 points has 1 or 2 orbits, never a single fixed
  orbit-plus-nothing. This is the banked never-UNIQUE trichotomy, now as covering-space
  connectivity.

## Member 2 — Temporal-issuance / H¹(finality sheaf) theorist

**What IS the process, from my lens.** Issuance is the **gluing of observer-local finalized
bindings into a shared section**; "a record" is a *binding* whose reversal-cost `μ` has
crossed threshold for a holder-set (hard-to-undo, externally conferred, never self-attested —
Gödel); "finality" is that rising reversal-cost, and "shared reality" is the **global section
of the finality sheaf, which exists iff its Čech H¹ vanishes** (DRIVING-HYPOTHESIS §H0(b)).
So the process is a *descent problem run forward in issuance-order*: at each seam the dynamics
attempts to glue the local orientations into one third-person-well-defined orientation, and
**the H¹ obstruction is the process-fact "the local finalizations DID NOT glue."** What the
obstruction MEANS as a process fact: it is not noise and not a failure of enough hardening —
local bindings can each be perfectly final and still fail to cohere globally (co-creation
succeeds locally, fails globally). σ is precisely the **holonomy datum of the orientation
local system** — a degree-1 class external to the loop's local dynamics, which is why no
amount of local finalization fixes it.

- **MODE: EXACT** for "finality of the shared orientation ⇔ H⁰≠0 (a global section exists)
  ⇔ H¹=0 ⇔ third-person well-definedness ⇔ Gorard causal invariance." (This is the banked
  triple; TI states it independently as the finality-sheaf descent.)
- **MODE: EXACT (new, verified)** the **χ(S¹)=0 balance:** on the issuance loop the finality
  sheaf is a rank-1 local system on a circle, so `dim H⁰ = dim H¹` (Euler characteristic of
  `S¹` is 0). Process meaning: **the multiplicity of ways the third person can consistently
  agree equals the dimension of the obstruction when it cannot.** Agreement and obstruction
  are the same size — finality is "balanced." When H¹=0 there is exactly a 1-dim space of
  consistent global orientations (σ selects a point in it); when H¹≠0 that space is empty and
  the obstruction is 1-dim.
- **MODE: ANALOGY→EXACT boundary** the E002 bridge caution transfers: `μ`, `κ_i`, `G_ij`,
  `Ω_ij` are *observer-readout* machinery, not source primitives. So "the process" at
  source-grade is only the transport + the holonomy; the finality *records* are the readout.
  Do not over-source the sheaf: H¹ lives on the readout side and *reports* the source
  monodromy; it does not author it.

holders rising past threshold — the standard "k-deep confirmation is irreversible" — which is
**external** (redundancy across holders), never a node's own say-so.

### What PUSH+PULL means physically

PUSH+PULL is the signature of an **open system with a flat transport**: a **boundary write at
genesis** (PUSH) and **holonomy-only transport thereafter** (PULL).

- **PUSH (EXACT):** the fiber's initial point — which sheet of the double cover the history
  rides on — is a **boundary datum**, not fixed by the flow. In consensus terms the genesis
  block is externally signed; in bundle terms it is the choice of trivialization at the seam;
  in finality terms it is the external prescription "required at the null stratum." The
  *absolute* orientation is written from outside the loop, at the seam.
- **PULL (EXACT):** thereafter the connection is **flat**, so transport is path-only: the
  inside can **verify relative consistency** (α-even checks: does orientation still agree with
  record-direction across the co-flip weld?) but **cannot read the absolute value** (α-odd,
  needs the seed). This is precisely hash-chain verification without the nonce, or FHE
  computation without decryption — the token is carried and checked, never opened.

Physically, then: **the process transports a bit it can neither create nor read; the arrow of
time is a boundary condition the issuance flow conducts.** "Hosts but never mints" = flat
transport of an externally-set holonomy. This is the dynamical restatement of Q2-FREE and it
is a *second, independent* mechanism for σ's externality (holonomy/flatness), distinct from
the static Schur map-blindness.
