# Pre-registration: revised B.5 observation-to-family campaign

## Objective

Construct or decisively obstruct a source-owned derived physicalization map

\[
\Theta_{\mathcal S,g}:
\mathcal P_{\mathcal S}(g^*\mathcal C_{\rm roll})
\longrightarrow E_g\otimes\mathbb C^3
\]

that turns the author-stated ambient two-spinor-plus-one-RS architecture into
three observer-facing physical family slots. The target must be actual
physical cohomology. The previously proposed
\(T_{192}=W_{-2}\oplus W_0\oplus W_{+2}\) target is forbidden: its three
weights belong to a tangential spin-1 isotypic carrier and are not three
invariant family copies.

Construction authority for B.5 remains assigned to P2C. This GU campaign
produces a conditional construction/falsification packet and does not reclaim
ownership.

## Frozen viewpoint stack

Every arrow must be typed at one of four levels:

1. third-person global GU geometry and field/BV complex on \(Y^{14}\);
2. section pullback and subgroup restriction along \(g:X^4\to Y^{14}\);
3. lossy physical reduction: BV/BRST cohomology, gauge quotient, spectral or
   low-energy projection, mirror removal, and families pushforward;
4. first-person reference/preparation/record, including external \(S_3\),
   orientation, scale, and the corrected unnormalized I6 pairing.

Pullback is not physical reduction. A measurement or post-selection is not a
field-representation quotient. A cross-level dimension mismatch is not by
itself an obstruction; a same-level character or naturality mismatch is.

## Success certificate

`B5-FAMILY-CONSTRUCTION` requires one target-free diagram that supplies:

- a native \(\operatorname{Sp}(32,32;\mathbb H)\)/super-IG odd module and
  explicit nonzero bracket into the connection/one-form sector;
- the rolled complex's missing middle differential or an exact source-owned
  replacement, with square/closure identities stated;
- a physical cohomology object with exactly three copies of one internal
  Pati-Salam/SM family type and no unpriced mirror/extra modes;
- associated-grade provenance from two spinor carriers and one RS carrier;
- naturality over the groupoid of admissible metric sections;
- every \(SU(2)_+\) breaking, ghost quotient, low-energy projector, and
  external \(S_3\) reference exposed rather than imported from the target;
- compatibility with B.5's BV master equation, families pushforward,
  boundary spectral section, and BV-to-boundary-Dirac map; and
- provenance separation among manuscript content, podcast claims, standard
  machinery, and repo reconstruction.

Calling several choices "one functor" does not make them canonical. Its
mapping cone or an equivalent exact certificate must exhibit every discarded
mode.

## Predeclared outcomes

- **B5-FAMILY-CONSTRUCTION:** the success certificate is met.
- **B5-FAMILY-OBSTRUCTION:** an exact zero Hom-space, character mismatch,
  cohomology multiplicity mismatch, unavoidable mirror class, or naturality
  obstruction rules out the entire frozen construction class.
- **B5-FAMILY-UNDERDEFINED:** a consistent diagram exists only after a named
  source formula or selector that the available sources do not define. The
  result must identify the smallest missing datum and the exact test it would
  unlock.
- **B5-FAMILY-FORK:** native GU and conventional physical reductions give
  different answers. The result must state which construction each answer
  inhabits; neither transfers silently.

An obstruction to a zero-order or equivariant bridge does not kill a
differential/derived bridge unless its symbol and cohomology class are also
covered. Conversely, "the unreleased differential might fix it" is
`UNDERDEFINED`, not a construction.

1. **Source/BV constructor:** maximize the strongest source-compatible
   diagram; price every bracket, differential, quotient, and B.5 gate.
2. **Representation obstructionist:** compute characters, branching,

attack the combined diagram rather than vote among reports.
