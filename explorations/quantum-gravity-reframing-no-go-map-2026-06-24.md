# Quantum-Gravity Reframing No-Go Map

## 1. Verdict

The steelman is legitimate but narrow:

```text
GU may reject "quantize the 4D GR metric" as the primary construction path.
That is a framing exit from metric-first quantum gravity, not a solution to
quantum recovery, QFT recovery, exact GR recovery, anomalies, measurement, or
spin-3/2 consistency.
```

What this legitimately bypasses:

- the requirement that the fundamental variable be an operator-valued 4D metric
  or perturbative spin-2 field;
- no-go statements whose hypotheses are explicitly the smooth compact
  Kaluza-Klein / single-E8 / standalone-RS classes that GU does not inhabit;
- rhetorical pressure to solve every problem by first quantizing Einstein's
  metric.

What it does not bypass:

- a source-closed primary action/operator on the GU source geometry;
- a reduction/shadow theorem recovering exact classical GR where claimed;
- a QFT shadow extraction certificate: Hilbert space or algebra of observables,
  state, dynamics, locality/microcausality or replacement condition, unitarity or
  reflection positivity, anomaly checks, and a measurement/state-preparation map;
- the live VZ, SM finite-control, generation-count, anomaly-shadow, and observer
  forcing gates.

The decision-grade status is therefore:

```text
NOT-QG-FRAMING: valid as a primary-framing exit.
SOURCE-GEOMETRY: replacement objective specified only at interface level.
GR-RECOVERY: still owed; exact Schwarzschild/Kerr are blocked by action/IG gates.
QFT-RECOVERY: still owed; the named QFT-shadow certificate is a schema and says
              the current repo is blocked before state-space/state extraction.
```

Three late-arriving schema files were read before finalizing this map. They sharpen the
contract but do not close the recovery claims:

```text
source-geometry-not-quantized-gravity-contract:
  reframing accepted as contract, not proof; source-to-shadow certificates required.

qft-shadow-extraction-certificate:
  schema defined; QFT shadow blocked before state-space and state extraction.

gr-shadow-recovery-certificate:
  certificate specified; true GR shadow not currently certified.
```

The first exact missing object is not another broad QG essay. It is a
source-to-shadow proof object:

```text
QG-SHADOW-01:
  input:
    source-closed GU interface
    (S_GU, D_GU, variation branch, section map, source law, boundary data)

  output:
    R_GR certificate
      exact low-energy GR shadow or explicit failure mode

    R_QFT certificate
      Hilbert space or observable algebra
      state/vacuum or density matrix
      dynamics/propagator or path-integral replacement
      locality/microcausality or stated replacement condition
      positivity/unitarity/reflection-positivity condition
      anomaly and extra-mode audit
      measurement/state-preparation map
```

The late-arriving source-geometry, QFT-shadow, and GR-shadow files define the contract
and certificate schemas. They do not supply the first concrete witnesses. The first child
blockers are:

```text
QFTStateSpaceExtractionCertificate
QFTStateExtractionCertificate
ObservableAdmissibilityCertificate
ELProjectedGRShadowTheorem(branch)
```

Until those are supplied, "not quantizing GR" remains a legitimate research posture but
cannot be cited as quantum-gravity recovery.
