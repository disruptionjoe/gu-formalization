## 0. Five-lens council (inline, mandatory wave shape)

**Lens 1 — Quantum-foundations theorist.**

selection, superselection, positivity of the physical inner product,
records as the operational substrate of temporal order. The strongest
foundations move is the operational one — the two Krein anchors are gauge
until a charged observable exists; the payload flip is physical because it

measurement problem." The toy Born-rule check is a consistency certificate
on a finite quotient, nothing more. If the dossier drifts one sentence
toward "explains quantum probability," a competent referee burns the whole
thing.

**Lens 2 — Mathematical physicist.**
*Approach:* numbered propositions with exact hypotheses, each carrying its
machine receipt, with the epistemic type of each result marked explicitly:
ENUMERATED (finite inventory checked, exhaustiveness unproven), EXACT

(structural remark, no theorem). Define the class C_0 before any claim
that quantifies over it. Never let an enumerated computation wear a

operation splits the pair" is proven only over the enumerated generator
set and its 16 composites. The dossier must carry that qualifier in the
claim itself, not in a footnote, or the central proposition is overstated.

**Lens 3 — Philosopher of time.**
*Approach:* police the arrow language. The defensible claim is
covariation — whatever fixes the sector fixes the record direction at zero
extra cost — never "the arrow of time IS eps." The Z/2 is relational (the

**Lens 4 — Hostile referee.**

## 3. Framing for a foundations audience

### 3.1 The honest headline

The aspiration headline is: **"the arrow of time and quantum positivity
are one coin."** Its honest, publishable form is:

> In a class of finite oriented Krein systems whose record laws contain no
> underived sign, the choice of positive-norm physical sector and the
> direction of record accumulation are a single Z/2 datum: no cost-free
> operation flips one without the other, and prying them apart costs
> exactly one additional bit. A minimal action realizes the class with the
> direction Noether-derived; an independent audit found a full-scale
> candidate law to be a member; and the datum is storable topologically —
> locally invisible, globally readable through at most one identified
> channel.

Why this is the right headline and not a weaker one: the result is not
"these two things are correlated in our model." It is a *price theorem*
shape: independence is not forbidden, it is *priced*, and the price is
exact (one bit). That converts a metaphysical-sounding identification into
an accounting statement a referee can attack — which is precisely what
makes it worth a paper. And the relational caveat is a feature, not a
hedge: a convention costs nothing to flip; this datum costs exactly one
bit to flip asymmetrically. That posted, machine-checked price is the
non-conventional content — the coin has two faces but no engraved
"heads": which face is physical is relational; THAT the two labelings
(sector, direction) ride the same face is not.

### 3.2 What it does NOT claim (the non-claims are load-bearing)

1. **No GU verdict.** Nothing here validates Geometric Unity, its (9,5)
   package, or any physical prediction of it. The W229 audit is a
   membership statement about a candidate law's sign structure, rendered
   conditionally, on the law as built.
2. **No cosmological claim beyond the conditional readout.** O1 says: IF
   a deviation from w = −1 is detected AND the energy-sign co-variance is
   derived AND a reference sector is fixed, THEN the sign is an
   orientation-consistency datum. Current data are orientation-silent.
   No detection is predicted.
3. **No arrow of time simpliciter.** The claim is covariation with an
   exact separation price, inside a class. It does not say which
   direction is the future, does not derive a thermodynamic arrow
   (no coarse-graining, no fluctuation theorem, no H-theorem — "record
   direction" is a monotone register at this grade), and does not assert
   the TaF finality identification (bar(b) = finality-axis polarity
   remains OPEN; the two-anchor relabeling freedom is retained
   throughout).
4. **No Born-rule or measurement claim.** The toy Born-rule check is a
   positivity/normalization certificate on a finite quotient.
5. **No claim of exhaustive generality.** The zero-import inventory is
   enumerated, not characterized; the cohomological grade is open
   (Section 5, G-A2); the class is finite-dimensional.
6. **And one sharp negative IS claimed:** the same bit provably cannot
   also carry the generation/parity demand in the quaternionic class
   (P6) — the paper would be the rare kind that reports its own
   headline's ceiling as a theorem.

### 3.3 Why a foundations audience should care

Three hooks, in descending order of solidity:

- **Krein/pseudo-Hermitian quantization has a known sign ambiguity** (the
  choice of C-operator / fundamental symmetry; non-unique at
  degeneracies). This result types that ambiguity's resolution as the
  SAME datum as a record arrow, with the identification priced rather
  than postulated — a new kind of statement about an old ambiguity.
- **Records-first arrows are usually assumed, not derived.** Standard
  accounts locate the arrow in initial conditions plus coarse-graining;
  here a record direction is Noether-derived from the same structure that
  fixes positivity, with a machine-checked no-free-sign inventory. The
  vacuum result (P10: zero orientation witness — "one absence seen
  twice") gives the account an unusual unification check: the GR-side and
  QM-side failures of the construction are literally the same absence.
- **Topological storage with a single global readout** (P7 + O1) is a
  clean, falsifiable-in-principle signature shape: a bit that no local
  experiment can read, that one global observable could.

(P9) — W229's law was built months before C_0 was defined and could have
failed the audit; the SGN1 control exhibits exactly what failure looks
like (unconfined content has NO definite direction). A definition cannot
audit an independently built object. **Conceded:** P1 standing alone
would be a thin result; the paper must be framed on P2 + P4 + P9, with P1
as the class-level lemma.

### R2. Class-generality (gerrymandering)

*"C_0 is bespoke: finite-dimensional, one signature instance, a specific
record-law shape. The 'exactly one bit' price may be an artifact of the
class boundary you drew."*

examined (audit discipline, Section 1 of the membership audit). The
record-law shape J = eps·q is the W229 lineage, i.e. inherited from the
object under study, not invented for the theorem. The hostile scenario
(a zero-import split appearing at a grade the finite inventory cannot
see) is pre-registered in-repo as a named route to N = 5, not hidden.
**Standing concession (un-rebutted):** the enumerated inventory has no
exhaustiveness proof, the cohomological/BV grade has more involutions
than the action level, and the class has one signature instance at
theorem strength. Until G-A1/G-A2 (Section 5) are done, "no zero-import
operation splits the pair" means "none in the enumerated inventory," and
a referee who insists the abstract say exactly that is right.
