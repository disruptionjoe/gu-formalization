## Extraction contract (the discriminating rule, stated before the ledger)

A demand row is graded **CONSTRUCTION-GRADE-SOURCED** only if it carries a
**non-empty verbatim primary-source locus** AND a **construction-grade
specification** — a typed relation / matrix / condition, not a verbal shape and
not a self-professed gap. Three failure routes all collapse to SOURCE-SILENT:

1. no locus in the corpus at all;
2. a locus that is only a **verbal shape** (a gesture at a construction the
   author has not released / cannot reliably transcribe); or
3. a locus where the **author explicitly disclaims** the datum ("good luck",
   "I don't know what to do", "not stuff I know").

A conventional-physics completion present *in the source* (BRST, positive-Hilbert
APS, standalone Rarita-Schwinger, replicated family) is logged **COMPARATOR/
IMPORTED** and never counted as the native datum. In this corpus, even the
comparators are repo-side reconstruction imports, not spoken by Weinstein — so
within the *primary* corpus the comparator column is empty.

This is the same rule for every row: it is what fails D1-D4 and what catches
D-PLANT. A control that cannot fail proves nothing; this one can and does
(D-PLANT), so its silence on D1-D4 is load-bearing.
