## 5. Method capture (for the record)
The adversary-presents-not-decides rule (Joe, this wave) worked exactly as intended: E's honest STRONG-not-
DECISIVE tagging + its explicit "do not kill A" preserved the one genuine novelty a skeptic-as-judge would have
killed ("scalar-tensor models make similar correlations"). Worth capturing as a general method note (an
ai-epistemology observation): in a NOVELTY hunt, the adversary must present and tag by strength, and the
orchestrator must require a DECISIVE argument to kill a constructively-defended candidate -- because novelty is a
claim the constructive evidence carries against a skeptical prior, not one the skeptic gets to veto.
