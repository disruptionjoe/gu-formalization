## Member 1 — ZK / homomorphic-encryption / zero-knowledge

weld is a homomorphic NOT on σ-as-ciphertext (flips the sector without ever
decrypting it) — yet cannot READ it (the α-even class lacks the "key"; blindness
= no decryption map). The boundary is the encryption interface; σ is the
plaintext outside the circuit (an NP witness external to GU's third-person

W235 — never extract the witness); what is outside is the key/plaintext-world.

equivariance obstruction, not a hidden key. Same *shape* (compute-and-carry
without read), different *source* of the hiding.
- **MODE: MIXED.** The carry-without-read / witness-outside-circuit shape is a
  precise rhyme (and the co-flip-as-homomorphic-NOT is genuinely tight), but the
  hiding is equivariance, not cryptographic hardness — so not the same object.
- **CONFIDENCE: 55%** — the homomorphic co-flip maps cleanly, but "can't read"
  has a categorically different cause than any real ZK/FHE primitive.

**Three escalating questions.**

## Chairman synthesis

**Which correspondences are EXACT-AND-REAL vs ANALOGY.**

There is exactly **one EXACT layer, and it has two faces that are the same
object**: the **information-theoretic accounting** (member 7) and the
**relativized-computability core** of the distributed lens (member 5b). "σ is 1
external bit," "the internal channel to σ has zero capacity," and "σ is not in
the internal computable closure, so it needs an oracle" are three statements of
one fact — and that fact is what W211 already proved as Gödel-independence and
what the blindness lemma states as Hom_{Z/2}(triv, sign) = ∅. This is not a
metaphor: a Z/2 free coin *is* 1 Hartley bit, and an α-even reading class *does*
have zero mutual information with an α-odd datum. The boundary law's own
structure explains *why* the inward capacity is zero: the **Lawvere/Gödel
diagonal** is the general reason a self-encoding system cannot internally read its
own α-odd bit — self-reference (read-leg) and symmetry-breaking selection
(select-leg) are the two legs, and the diagonal is exactly the fixed-point
argument that forces the internal reader to fail. Member 5's **host-vs-source**
and member 1's **homomorphic carry-without-read** are the two tightest *structural
rhymes* for the residual — "hosts/transforms but cannot mint" — and both are best
read as EXACT-at-the-shape-level, ANALOGY-at-the-mechanism-level (FLP's proof and
FHE's key are not GU's equivariance).

**Which are illuminating ANALOGIES only.** The metastable tie (bistable +
external break, but no amplification dynamics and GU is *perfectly* degenerate),
the hashgraph timeline (orientation is a lossy Z/2 shadow, and hashgraph *mints*
what GU *cannot*), the MMO authority split (a design pattern, no universal
property), and the cryptographic/loss-landscape dressings — each captures one
true facet and none is the same object. They are worth keeping as intuition pumps
precisely because they each isolate a different facet: bistability, orientation,
authority, hiding, flatness.

**The convergent read of the missing piece.** The lenses agree that the "missing
piece" is **not a mechanism GU lacks** — it is a **finite, exactly-located,
external bit-supply that GU's inside provably has zero capacity to read or mint**,
written across a boundary that is best modelled as a **channel with asymmetric
capacity**: ≈0 bits inward (blindness / zero mutual information / diagonal
self-reference obstruction), ≥1 bit inward-from-outside (hosting). Concretely:

- **What comes THROUGH the boundary:** exactly the internally-undetermined bits —
  **σ = 1 bit** (the sector / DE sign); **τ = ~2.58 bits** (independent, Q3);
  **θ = 0 bits IF the limit-point lean holds** (forced domain, dissolved), or a
  **continuum** if the conservative limit-circle horn stands. GU is complete *up
  to* this fixed, small, quantified import.
- **What is OUTSIDE:** the **writer / oracle / environment** that mints those
  bits. Every lens that has a source-object (FLP's environment, FHE's
  plaintext-world, NN's seed) puts the VALUE outside and the observer at the
  terminal — never inside as its source.
- **What the observer IS:** the **write-terminal / locus / read-blind carrier** —
  it hosts σ, transforms it homomorphically (co-flip), and *forces* the datum's
  externality (its inside-ness is why the bit must be outside), but it is not the
  bit's source. "Source-of-the-need, not source-of-the-value."
- **What it MEANS:** GU is an **oracle-relative (relativized) theory** — complete
  modulo a fixed finite external information supply, whose size the info-theoretic
  lens *quantifies* (1 + ~2.58 + {0 or ∞} bits) and whose necessity the
  distributed/logic lens *certifies* (relativized-unsolvable-internally). The
  "boundary" is the channel across which that supply is written; the first person
  is its terminal.

**Two structural flags for the open horns.** (i) The θ verdict is the single
largest swing in the bit-budget: limit-point ⇒ 0 bits (θ contributes nothing to
the import), limit-circle ⇒ a continuum (GU predictively empty at that slot) —
the information accounting makes the stakes of Q1a's pending hostile-verify
exact. (ii) The time-face being a **causal-past RETRACTION (idempotent), strictly
more general than an involution**, means the general obstruction is
projection-onto-causal-past, of which the Z/2 (σ) is the special involutive case;
member 5's FLP/causal reading is the one native to that generalization, and it is
why the boundary-law-operator-lift found the involution-typing *refuted in
general* (the causal-horizon engine strictly contains equivariance-exclusion).
The convergent EXACT object (zero inward capacity to an externally-written datum)
survives that generalization; the Z/2 packaging does not.
