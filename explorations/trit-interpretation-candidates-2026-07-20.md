# What is the trit?

## The load-bearing fact (binds every candidate)

Conditional forcing (commit f513fcf) + the trit-internal check (commit
0314958, K-a): the minimal external input forcing the order-3 count is
Z/6 = Z/2 (the payload orientation bit) x Z/3 (the trit). The two
factors are COPRIME hence INDEPENDENT; the bit is the CUBE of the Z/6
anchor. The trit is EXTERNAL (not supplied by the N4 identification;
the geometry supplies the target cube-roots but no fiber-side Z/3). The
trit is CYCLIC (order-3 rotation / cube roots of unity), NOT a nesting
or chain — this is the first discriminator any story must pass.

## Joe's originating idea

Three generations = three relative capability spaces: the global
("in quotes"), the regional/accessible capability space of the
observer, and the observer themselves. Tension flagged by all five
personas: nested scopes are a CHAIN (partial order); the trit is a
CYCLE. Fit requires the three to ROTATE roles, not stack.

## The five candidates (persona-sourced), each with its discriminator

1. **ROLE-ROTATION (orthodox rescue of Joe's idea).** The three are not
   nested but rotate whose-frame-reads-whom: world-from-observer,
   region-from-world, observer-from-region — a genuine 3-cycle.
   Discriminator: does the trit carry a preferred cycle ORIENTATION (a
   chirality on the 3-cycle)? Yes -> role-rotation; no -> mundane phase.
2. **{WORLD, ACCESS, STANDPOINT} (heterodox; the convergence candidate).**
   The three are the diagonal boundary's own three relationships:
   third-person structure, the accessible region (P2C restriction),
   first-person standpoint — PROVEN distinct tonight (L1). Generations =
   the three stances a describer can take to what it describes.
   Discriminator: does the trit's Z/3 match P2C's access-layer structure
   element-for-element (cross-repo computation)?
3. **THREE-PHASE OBSERVER CLOCK (wild frontier; TaF-native).** The trit
   is the minimal internal timekeeper a standpoint needs; generations =
   matter at each of three phases of the observer's tick. Discriminator:
   any three-CYCLIC (not merely three-fold) signature in mass/mixing
   data — needs data we cannot yet compute; flagged data-blocked.
4. **MUNDANE PHASE / PRIMITIVE Z/3 (orthodox null baseline).** The trit
   is just a phase in an internal circle, the plain mod-3, meaning
   nothing beyond itself. Discriminator: is the Z/3 reducible to
   bit-plus-something or primitive? Coprimality says primitive — which
   PARTIALLY defeats the null (a primitive trit is not "nothing," it is
   a genuine third irreducible datum) and pushes toward candidate 2/5.
5. **ACCESS AS THE IRREDUCIBLE THIRD (philosopher).** The boundary law
   proves a TWO (structure | standpoint); the trit is a THREE; the extra
   thing is REACHABILITY — neither pure structure nor pure standpoint,
   exactly P2C's subject. Discriminator: same as candidate 2, sharpened
   — is "access" a primitive third category or a blend collapsing the
   three to the proven two?

## Convergence (four of five personas)

Candidates 2 and 5 are the same claim; the role-rotation (1) and the
clock (3) both need a third irreducible thing that 2/5 supply (access).
The mundane null (4) is defeated by coprimality. So the field reduces to
ONE lead hypothesis — the trit indexes {world, access, standpoint},
access being the P2C-formalized middle rung — with ONE cheap decisive
test: the trit-vs-P2C-access-layer match.
