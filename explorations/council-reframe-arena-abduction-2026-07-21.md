### (ii) The sharpest read of the coincidence-stacking — evidence or artifact, and the test

**Split the four signs into three internal + one external.** The QM Krein sign, the
GR vacuum sign, and the arrow are theoretical sign-slots *inside* GU, co-varying

furniture you did not place.
