(`temporal-issuance/DRIVING-HYPOTHESIS-OBSERVER-ISSUANCE.md`): a three-layer pipeline in which the apparent
binary "observers create reality vs. substrate is fundamental" dissolves. In that pipeline, **GU's role is the
bottom layer only** -- the **fixed possibility-space / task-space** (richer substrate) that observers *access
and project from* but do not author.

The council repeatedly recast Joe's phrase "uploaded all the potential space" as **access to a
substrate-external possibility-space, not part-contains-whole ownership** -- explicitly GU/ruliad territory,
and explicitly preserving **projection != finality**. GU supplies *what can be projected*; finality (a TaF
question) and issuance (a TI question) are downstream and are NOT GU's to claim.

- No credibility manufacture: this pointer confers nothing on any GU claim and is not an obligation. It exists
