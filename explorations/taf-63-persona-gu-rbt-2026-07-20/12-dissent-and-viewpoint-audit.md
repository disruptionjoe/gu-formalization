# Hostile dissent and viewpoint audit

## Bottom line

The 63 responses contain a strong convergence around the missing native
physicalization, but that convergence can conceal several materially different
criticisms. They are not all variants of "build B.5":

1. some concern whether a native BV-BFV/source object exists at all;
2. some concern whether any such object is natural over observer sections;
3. some concern whether its output is a positive operational quantum theory;
4. some concern whether a finite or compact construction reaches the continuum;
5. some concern whether the search and repository can detect their own adaptive
   fitting, correlated evidence, semantic drift, and fork contamination; and
6. some concern whether native and conventional completions would be
   empirically distinguishable even if both can host the observed sector.

The panel therefore supports the first-person/third-person level correction,
but not a first-person solution of the third-person construction problem. The
load-bearing missing bridge is still a source-owned physicalization family
`Fam_g`: a native BV-BFV action/differential and domain whose cohomology removes
the mirror and extra complement, carries positive operational semantics, varies
naturally over the observer-section groupoid, and supports a downstream record
map without target leakage. The exact typed stack is recorded in
`explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md`; the
construction targets and obstructions are in
`explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`.

This is a dissent audit, not the panel's final synthesis. In particular, it
does not use frequency or persona agreement to change a mathematical or
physical claim. P56's minority warning applies to this panel itself: multiple
personas sharing the same frozen brief and dependency graph are not independent
scientific witnesses (`lab/process/construction-space-map.json`;
`VERIFICATION.md`).

## First-person versus third-person: what separates cleanly

The word "first-person" has two legitimate uses in the source adjudication:

- **geometric first person:** choosing a metric section and pulling an ambient
  field back to the observer's four-dimensional arena;
- **operational first person:** choosing a preparation, reference, orientation,
  or post-selection that yields a record.

Neither is the physical quotient between them. P07, P08, P19, P22, P25, P30,
P37, P41, P45, P49, P57, P58, and P61 independently enforce this distinction
from foundations, information, causal, philosophy, language, bundle, GU, game,
network, and relevance-filter perspectives.

### Apparent conflicts that dissolve by level separation

| apparent conflict | adjudication | why it dissolves |
|---|---|---|
| The ambient carriers have dimensions (128,128,1664), whereas observed matter looks like a small repeated family spectrum. | **Not a raw-dimension contradiction.** | A third-person carrier on (Y^{14}) and its observer-subgroup restriction are different representation objects. Restriction can expose the same internal charge type in globally inequivalent carriers. This preserves the possibility of an observer-relative (2+1) shadow without proving its physical multiplicity (P06, P25, P30, P45, P49; `explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md`). |
| The observer-restricted associated grade contains (3E_+\oplus3E_-\oplus X), while the physical target contains three chiral families. | **Compatible as two stages, not equal as objects.** | The former is representation-grade information after `Obs_g`; the latter would be cohomology after `Fam_g`. The level distinction removes the demand that the two ledgers already match before reduction, while exposing the reduction as a new obligation (P01, P02, P08, P37, P43, P46; `explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`). |
| An observer reference or orientation can distinguish a label that an ungraded third-person observable cannot read. | **Possible as downstream readout.** | A reference can read, orient, or relabel an existing physical object without being the upstream mechanism that creates its cohomology. P19 frames the error as conditioning mistaken for intervention; P22 calls it side information; P57 calls it a record that cannot retroactively validate the transition (`explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md`). |
| A compact or third-person operator continuation theorem might coexist with the absence of a first-person sector reader. | **No contradiction.** | Analytic existence/invertibility of a third-person pencil and operational sector readability are different claims. A graph-domain theorem can be correct without defining physical observables or a record channel (P03, P07, P15, P59, P62; `explorations/continuum-pencil-graph-domain-certificate-2026-07-20.md`). |

This level separation is a rose because it blocks a false kill and a false
construction at the same time. It saves the ambient-to-observed possibility
from a naive dimension objection, but it forbids using that rescue as evidence
that the physical reduction has been built.

### Conflicts that do not dissolve

1. **Pullback still does not remove (3E_-\oplus X).** Section restriction is
   neither BV cohomology nor a gauge quotient, spectral reduction, families
   pushforward, or low-energy decoupling. The mirror-chain obstruction remains
   if the actual differential and domain commute with the mirror exchange
   (P01, P06, P23, P37, P46, P50, P52; B.5 campaign).

2. **An observer axis can rigidify the object without making the result
   intrinsic to GU geometry alone.** A selector proved only after fixing an
   axis, orientation, (S_3) frame, or preparation is a result about the pair
   ((g,a)) or ((g,L)). P26 warns that shrinking the morphism class can make
   "natural" true by construction; P41 warns that a semantic change can hide
   the new datum (`GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`;
   `explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`).

3. **Different first-person views need not define one global physical
   object.** Constant cohomology rank, descent on overlaps, loop consistency,
   and holonomy reducible to (S_3) are unproved. A locally visible triple can
   mix by generic (GL(3)) or Berry transport (P02, P04, P12, P17, P45, P58,
   P59; B.5 campaign).

4. **Readable is not yet probabilistic.** The native Krein keep-and-grade fork
   lacks a demonstrated positive normalized state functional, observable
   algebra, representative-independent instruments, and completely positive
   record channel. P07 and P08 make this an operational obstruction, not a
   vocabulary preference (`lab/process/source-object-interface-contract.md`;
   `GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`).

5. **Conditioning is not causation.** Holding the geometry fixed and choosing
   a successful observer description can introduce collider or target-selection
   bias. A boundary/source adapter that encodes the desired output remains an
   upstream confounder rather than an explanation (P19, with parallel warnings
   from P13, P14, P21, and P22; `lab/process/source-object-interface-contract.md`).

6. **The (T_{192}) obstruction remains exact.** Viewpoint separation cannot
   turn three Cartan weights in one tangential spin-1 carrier into three
   invariant family copies. An observer-broken presentation changes the
   symmetry and still does not supply the proposed symmetric source-slot
   dictionary (P04, P06, P10, P26, P43, P49;
   `explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md`).

## The missing bridge, stated without viewpoint equivocation

The panel's sharpest joint bridge specification is:

```text
third-person GU carrier on Y14
    -- Obs_g: source-defined pullback and subgroup restriction -->
section-relative rolled/Krein complex on (X4,g)
    -- Fam_g: source-owned BV-BFV differential, domain, quotient,
              spectral/low-energy reduction, families pushforward -->
positive physical cohomology with exactly the retained three provenance lines
    -- Ref_a: representative-independent preparation/effect/record map -->
first-person operational statistics
```

For this to be more than a fiberwise construction, `Fam_g` must satisfy
base-change/descent over admissible sections, preserve constant physical rank,
source or obstruct an (S_3)-reduction, and state how orientation reversal acts.
For it to be native GU, its action, real form, Krein adjointness, gauge algebra,
boundary data, and losses must come from the native construction rather than an
untyped conventional import. For it to be physical, it must connect to positive
operational probabilities and stable dynamics. These are separable gates, not
one verbal promise (P02, P07, P08, P16, P24, P45, P48, P57-P59;
`lab/process/source-object-interface-contract.md`).

## Minority warnings a convergence summary could erase

### Scientific-object warnings

- **Finite satisfiability may have no continuum model (P15).** A family can pass
  every fixed-(N) algebraic/Krein check while inverse constants diverge, a
  Weyl sequence forms, or mirror cohomology reappears in the limit. The missing
  proposition has a uniform quantifier, not just a larger numerical sample
  (`explorations/continuum-pencil-graph-domain-certificate-2026-07-20.md`;
  `tests/README.md`).

- **Static selection may be dynamically unstable (P16).** Even a correct
  deformation retract or algebraic quotient does not establish an invariant,
  perturbatively stable physical sector under the source dynamics. Mirror or
  extra modes may return through resonances, bifurcations, or transverse
  instability (`lab/process/construction-space-map.json`; B.5 campaign).

- **An alphabet of three is not an order-three dynamics (P17).** Three local
  slots, an external trit, an (S_3) label, and a 3-primary torsion class are
  different structures. A genuine generation dictionary needs natural
  transport or monodromy, not repeated cardinality (B.5 campaign).

- **A source object must be a repeatable constructor, not a fitted map (P24).**
  Robustness over sections, repeated use, catalyst/resource accounting, and
  composability are additional obligations after single-instance existence
  (`lab/process/source-object-interface-contract.md`).

- **Operational equivalence may leave native and conventional stories
  underdetermined (P25).** A native Krein/BV reduction and conventional
  positive-Hilbert/BRST reduction could yield the same records. Without a
  native-only observable or lower fixed input cost, hosting does not decide
  which ontology is physically distinctive (`RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md`).

- **"Natural" depends on the automorphism category (P26).** Fixing an observer
  axis or family frame can make a selector equivariant only after the object
  has been enlarged. Naturality claims must declare the full objects and
  morphisms (`GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`; B.5 campaign).

- **External index structure may permit any integer without selecting three
  (P47).** Locating odd/3-primary data outside the balanced interior is a
  constraint, not a count theorem. A families index, eta term, or boundary
  class still needs an exact map to physical multiplicity
  (`papers/candidates/located-not-forced/STAGING-NOTES.md`).

- **The gauge problem begins before the spectrum claim (P48).** The mixed
  super-IG bracket lacks native real-form closure, a proper classical/quantum
  master action, boundary charge conservation, and Ward identities. Until
  then, physical BV cohomology is not defined (B.5 campaign).

- **Relevance filtering needs feedback bounds (P61).** Even if a spectral
  window removes (X) at one section, discarded modes may feed order-one
  effects into retained observables; rank and gap stability must be shown
  (`explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`).

- **Metabolic scaling supplies no GU inference (P63).** No conserved flux,
  terminal-unit class, space-filling hierarchy, or energy-time optimization is
  present. This is a precise kill of an attractive imported analogy, not a GU
  program kill (`RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md`; `VERIFICATION.md`).

### Evidence and search warnings

- **Nominal test count may greatly overstate effective evidence (P18, P38,
  P56).** Tests share carriers, normalizations, domains, source reconstructions,
  and code. The right audit is an assumption-overlap or dependency stress
  graph, not a witness count (`VERIFICATION.md`; `tests/README.md`).

- **The search denominator is not certified complete (P14).** `UNDERDEFINED`
  axes and expressive lower-order/source maps can expand the hypothesis class
  faster than the ledger prunes it. An empty mapped intersection falsifies the
  frozen class, never GU without an adequacy theorem
  (`lab/process/construction-space-exploration-protocol.md`;
  `lab/process/construction-space-map.json`).

- **Adaptive search needs held-out constraints (P13, P31, P39, P54).** A frozen
  interface is not binding if hidden coefficients, boundary phases, or fork
  switches remain adjustable. Leave-one-sector-out and blinded comparator
  tests are stronger than additional in-sample fixtures
  (`lab/process/source-object-interface-contract.md`).

- **Machine learning can propose but not certify (P20).** A surrogate trained
  on current fixtures inherits their source and fork biases. Exact symmetry,
  anomaly, cohomology, domain, and continuum gates remain authoritative
  (`tests/README.md`; `VERIFICATION.md`).

- **The research policy can reward-hack on clean receipts (P32).** Qualitative
  "information gain" can rationalize a preferred next action unless expected
  dispositions, costs, and rejected alternatives are recorded at decision
  time (`AGENTS.md`; `NEXT-STEPS.md`).

- **"Located, not forced" can become an immunizing move (P27, P50).** Each
  branch-local scope statement may be honest while the program-wide auxiliary
  burden grows without bound. Failed risky tests must shrink the live class or
  increase the recorded import/complexity cost (`RESEARCH-POSTURE.md`;
  `RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md`).

- **One import is not one unit of explanatory cost (P22, P23, P39, P60).** A
  source adapter that supplies quotient, positivity, chirality, labels,
  normalization, and boundary data may contain nearly the whole target. Costs
  require a declared encoding/equivalence and sensitivity ledger, not a raw
  count (`lab/process/source-object-interface-contract.md`).

### Repository-interface warnings

- **Git history is not semantic consistency (P09, P34, P35).** Parallel edits
  can leave status, roadmap, paper, JSON, and test interpretations at different
  grades without a textual merge conflict. A stable claim/fork/dependency
  registry and generated views would address this risk
  (`lab/process/runbooks/claim-status-consistency-quality-workflow.md`).

- **Inference authority is enforced mainly by prose (P36, P40, P55).** A
  copied L2 or historical sentence can lose its grade, jurisdiction,
  supersession, or fork. A claim citator/capability DAG would make the repo's
  real self-correction easier to query (`VERIFICATION.md`;
  `RESEARCH-STATUS.md`).

- **Semantic overload can recreate invalid arrows (P33, P37, P41, P42).** The
  words "observer," "source," "family," "outside," and "selection" span
  distinct types. A controlled vocabulary and typed current-state view are not
  cosmetic if they prevent `Obs_g -> Fam_g` from being inferred by metaphor
  (`GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`;
  `explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md`).

- **Evidence transmission is itself bandwidth-limited (P62).** A large,
  correction-rich repository can be scientifically honest yet operationally
  hard to reconstruct. Minimal proof-carrying packets should complement, not
  replace, the historical record (`VERIFICATION.md`; `REPRODUCE.md`).

## Substantive disagreements that should remain visible

### 1. Minimal local obstruction versus full North-Star weld

P52 argues that the next decisive object is the smallest observer-natural,
real/Krein-compatible first-order complex: classify its symbols and test mirror
acyclicity before building the whole BV-BFV/source apparatus. P49, P50, and P54
largely agree on this cheapest-decisive middle-differential swing.

P21, P51, and especially P53 warn that a clean local theorem can become
"byproduct capture": the actual North Star is one source object supporting all
four sectors without retuning. A local symbol theorem can be exact and still
fail to constrain that source object.

**Adjudication:** the positions can be conditionally reconciled, not averaged.
Run the minimal symbol/acyclicity test only if the candidate class is
source-traceable and each outcome updates the shared interface and construction
map. If the source cannot delimit the class, the correct output is a source gap,
not an ever-larger free symbol search. This preserves P52's decisiveness and
P53's value hierarchy (`lab/process/source-object-interface-contract.md`;
`lab/process/construction-space-exploration-protocol.md`).

### 2. Externality as localization versus externality as relocation

P05 and P47 treat the 2-primary/3-primary split and index obstruction as a
valuable localization theorem: it says where the missing odd/count information
cannot originate. P25, P27, P42, P53, and P60 warn that "outside" is not yet an
explanation; it may merely move the desired structure into an unspecified and
underpriced datum.

**Adjudication:** both are true at different claim grades. Localization is an
earned negative result. Explanation requires an independently sourced object,
an exact transport map, a cost/equivalence ledger, and a target-independent
observable. This disagreement remains live until those are supplied
(`RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md`; located-not-forced paper packet).

### 3. Native-first testing versus an equally strong conventional comparator

Many personas use the conventional construction only as a scope guard: a BRST,
Higgs, APS, replicated-family, or positive-Hilbert completion may work but does
not validate native GU. P25, P31, and P39 go further: build a serious
conventional comparator with the same outputs and charge both sides under one
frozen description/cost language.

**Adjudication:** the comparator is scientifically valuable for distinctiveness
and import pricing, but its success cannot be booked as a native construction.
Conversely, an ordinary positive-Hilbert nonunitarity objection or integer-index
argument cannot be booked as a native kill. The comparison must remain two
typed model identities, never a blended vote (`GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`).

### 4. Repository hardening versus scientific displacement

P09, P33-P36, P40, P41, P55, and P62 recommend claim graphs, semantic typing,
citators, generated maps, and compact packets. P53's byproduct warning applies:
infrastructure can become another finishable substitute for source construction.

**Adjudication:** a small generated claim/fork/viewpoint manifest is justified
where it directly prevents a known invalid cast or stale dependency. A new
general research-information framework is not justified by this panel. The
scientific swing remains the controlling consumer of any hardening work.

## Constraints on the final synthesis

The final synthesis should preserve at least these five clauses:

1. the first-person/third-person distinction is a successful category-error
   correction, not a physicalization result;
2. the missing bridge has analytic, gauge, operational, global/descent, and
   dynamical obligations, not only a representation-theoretic one;
3. the minimal middle-differential swing is high leverage only while tethered
   to a source-traceable shared interface;
4. a native/conventional comparator is useful, but cross-fork successes and
   no-gos never transfer silently; and
5. agreement among 63 personas is a map of recurring risks and opportunities,
   not a scientific confidence multiplier.

The strongest rare warning is P25's: even a successful native physicalization
may remain empirically equivalent to a conventional reconstruction. The
strongest strategic dissent is P52 versus P53: smallest decisive obstruction
versus protection of the full shared-source North Star. The strongest
repository warning is the combined P34/P35/P41 result: semantic history,
construction identity, and viewpoint level are not yet enforced as data, so a
textually clean merge or familiar word can recreate a scientifically invalid
cast.
