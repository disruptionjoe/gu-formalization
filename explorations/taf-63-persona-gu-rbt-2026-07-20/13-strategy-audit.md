# Strategy audit

## Decision

The highest-information next swing is the already isolated **source-licensed
middle-differential class disposition**. Freeze the actual rolled source data,
enumerate the complete native first-order class it permits, and construct or
obstruct the deformation retract that retains all three $E_+$ provenance
lines while making $3E_-\oplus X$ acyclic.

This recommendation is based on dependency leverage, not on a persona vote.
The middle differential is the earliest unresolved arrow on which the family
bundle, operational state space, $S_3$ transport, and four-sector weld all
depend. It also has an unusually sharp three-way return: construction,
class-relative obstruction, or a named source gap. The exact target ledger and
the execution order are already frozen in
`explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`, Section 8.

The operator-wall continuation remains valuable theorem hardening, but it is
an independent third-person continuation theorem and cannot create the missing
first-person sector reader; the repo already says this explicitly in
`explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md`, Sections 3
and 5. Likewise, $S_3$ transport cannot yet act on a physical family bundle
because that bundle has not been constructed.

$3E_+\oplus3E_-\oplus X$, an exact zero-order obstruction, a small live

## Ranked buds: pursue in dependency order

### 1. Disposition the source-licensed middle-differential class

This is the dominant bud because it can remove the highest-centrality unknown
with the least speculative surface area (P01, P06, P23, P43, P46, P48-P50,
P52-P54). It must begin with source extraction, not a freely designed BV
operator.

### 2. Lift any local survivor to a native BV-BFV/​(L_\infty) construction

Only a local candidate that passes mirror, real/Krein, and symbol-exactness
gates should receive lower-order terms, a source-owned domain, a master-action
test, anomaly/closure checks, and global cohomology (P03, P15, P16, P24, P46,
P48, P59, P62). The source-interface requirements live in
`lab/process/source-object-interface-contract.md`; the current conditional
operator-domain boundary is recorded in
`explorations/continuum-pencil-graph-domain-certificate-2026-07-20.md`.

### 3. Globalize physical cohomology over the observer groupoid

After, and only after, a physical cohomology object exists, test constant rank,
base change, descent, loop holonomy, orientation co-flip, and reduction of the
rank-three frame bundle to the declared $S_3$ representation (P02, P04, P12,
P17, P26, P45, P47, P58). This is where an observer-relative multiplicity could
become a global family object.

### 4. Demand operational and four-sector consequences from one frozen packet

For a global survivor, construct a positive operational state/effect system
and record channel, then run the same immutable packet through QM, SM, GR, and
cosmology without retuning. Only after the remaining normalization rank is
known should Lane 2 freeze a target-independent prediction and a serious
conventional comparator (P07, P08, P21, P25, P27, P31, P38, P39, P49, P51,
P53, P60). See `lab/process/construction-space-map.json` and
`RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md`.

## Ranked thorns: attack these, do not merely document them

### 1. The common source/BV-BFV object is absent

This is the minimal cut set for many downstream claims. It currently carries
the quotient, mirror fate, positivity, dynamics, family transport, and
boundary/domain selection without an instantiated packet (P01, P07, P08,
P21, P38, P48-P51, P53, P57, P60). If the source cannot delimit the middle
differential, further internal construction would be model building rather
than source reconstruction.

### 2. Observer restriction can still be mistaken for physical or operational selection

The exact branching is third-person geometry viewed on an observer section.
It is not yet a physical spectrum; first-person preparation or record data are
downstream and cannot repair that missing arrow (P02, P07, P19, P22, P25,
P30, P33, P37, P41, P45, P49, P57-P61). See
`explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md`.

### 3. A flexible adapter can hide the whole target

Counting one source object as one import underprices it if it independently
chooses chirality, polarization, family frame, boundary phase, scale, and
sector maps. Without a class bound and target-blind commitment, passing tests
can measure expressiveness rather than unification (P13, P14, P19, P21, P23,
P27, P31, P39, P44, P49-P51, P60). See
`lab/process/source-object-interface-contract.md`.

### 4. Finite and compact witnesses do not supply the needed uniform continuum object

Pointwise finite passes, compact graph domains, and numerical stability can
coexist with a failed noncompact limit, rank jump, Weyl sequence, or divergent
inverse constant (P03, P15, P18, P46, P47, P54, P59, P62). This remains a real
analytic gate, but it is not the earliest dependency for family
physicalization. See
`explorations/continuum-pencil-graph-domain-certificate-2026-07-20.md`.

### 5. Repository truth is denormalized and correlated

Historical corrections are a rose, but live state still spans Markdown,
JSON, tests, and papers without an enforced claim/fork/dependency schema.
Nominally different results can also share one latent source assumption (P09,
P18, P33-P36, P38, P40, P41, P55, P56, P62). A generated claim DAG would be a
useful support move, but it must not displace the Lane-1 source test.

## Strongest next executable swing

### Objective

**Construct or decisively obstruct the native middle physicalization for the
frozen observer-grade carrier.** The target is not a first-person reading; it
is the missing map between section-relative third-person data and physical
cohomology.

### Execution shape

Use sequential gates with small parallel expert lenses inside a gate:

1. **Source and type freeze.** Extract the exact rolled matrix, filtration,
   lower-order terms, admissible domain/boundary data, real structure, and
   super-IG role. Produce two explicitly separate class specifications:
   native GU and conventional comparator. Do not allow a hybrid implicit cast.
2. **Class-completeness and local algebra.** Enumerate the full
   observer-natural first-order symbol space allowed by the native source.
   Independently audit representation completeness, native real/Krein closure,
   and source provenance. Freeze planted mirror-preserving, non-Krein
   mirror-breaking, and acyclic controls before testing candidates.
3. **Cheap decisive gates.** In order, test mirror commutation, square-zero or
   stated curved-​(L_\infty) closure, and symbol exactness/ellipticity on every
   unwanted irreducible. Kill candidates at the first failed gate.
4. **Global gate.** Only for survivors, fix lower-order terms and the
   source-owned domain, compute global BV cohomology, and demand an explicit
   mapping-cone contraction of $3E_-\oplus X$ while preserving the three
   provenance-distinguished $E_+$ classes.

This sequencing follows the exact campaign contract in
`explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`, not a new B.5
redesign. It also reflects the minimalist and experimentalist warnings that a
full global apparatus should not be built around a local symbol class that has
already failed (P52, P54).

### Predeclared outcomes

- **`B5-MIDDLE-CONSTRUCTION`:** the source-delimited class contains a native
  real/Krein-compatible global BV-BFV candidate with valid closure/domain,
  an explicit deformation retract onto the three $E_+$ provenance lines,
  and an acyclic $3E_-\oplus X$ complement. This advances one candidate;
  it does not yet establish a global family bundle, $S_3$ transport,
  operational quantum theory, or the four-sector North Star.
- **`B5-MIDDLE-OBSTRUCTION`:** class completeness is established and every
  source-compatible candidate either preserves mirror-paired cohomology or
  fails native real/Krein closure, square-zero/master closure, symbol
  exactness, or the global domain/cohomology gate. This obstructs the frozen
  native class. It does not obstruct the conventional BRST/Higgs/positive-
  Hilbert class.
- **`B5-MIDDLE-SOURCE-GAP`:** the sources do not determine a sufficiently
  bounded symbol/action/domain class to run an honest exhaustion. This is not
  a mathematical no-go. It is a decisive provenance result: GU has not yet
  supplied the load-bearing construction.
- **`B5-MIDDLE-METHOD-OBSTRUCTION`:** enumeration completeness or planted
  controls fail, so the test cannot distinguish construction from
  singular/paired controls at the reachable representation or resolution.
  No physical inference follows.

### Stop conditions

Stop rather than enlarging the hypothesis class when any of these occurs:

1. The source does not delimit the differential, lower-order terms, or domain.
   Return `B5-MIDDLE-SOURCE-GAP` before designing a favorable operator.
2. Enumeration completeness cannot be certified. A negative search result is
   then evidence about sampled candidates only, not an obstruction.
3. A candidate's coefficients, orientation, family labels, boundary phase, or
   polarization were chosen after inspecting the desired spectrum. Reclassify
   the datum as an import or reject the native-selection claim.
4. A planted mirror-paired control passes as chiral, a non-Krein control passes
   native closure, or an acyclic control fails. Return method obstruction.
5. The candidate fails mirror, closure, or symbol-exactness gates. Do not spend
   continuum compute on it.
6. A conventional construction is used. Continue it only as a separately
   priced comparator; do not report it as a native rescue or native kill.

## Follow-on decision tree

1. **After local survival:** construct the cyclic BV-BFV/​(L_\infty) lift,
   source-owned graph/asymptotic domain, and global deformation retract. Kill
   on master-equation obstruction, anomaly, domain failure, or mirror-paired
   cohomology (P03, P15, P46, P48, P59).
2. **After global survival:** build the family superconnection over admissible
   observer sections and test constant rank, descent, and $S_3$ holonomy.
   One source-licensed loop with rank jump or holonomy outside the declared
   $S_3$ kills the global family claim for that candidate (P02, P04, P12,
   P17, P45, P58).
3. **After a family-bundle survivor:** construct the positive operational
   state/effect algebra and record map. Negative probabilities,
   representative-dependent statistics, failed complete positivity where
   required, or signalling kill the native operational realization (P07,
   P08, P22, P25, P30).
4. **After operational survival:** serialize one immutable source packet and
   run all four sector cards plus one held-out prediction/comparator without
   retuning. Incompatible shared data or target-dependent maps kill the welded
   corridor, while success advances the candidate under the Rung rubric (P21,
   P27, P31, P39, P51, P53, P60).

## What must wait for missing source data

Do not presently infer or optimize the following as though GU had supplied
them:

- the exact rolled middle matrix, filtration, lower-order terms, and its
  source-owned domain;
- a native BV-BFV master action, gauge/ghost tower, boundary BFV data, and
  anomaly/closure conditions;
- the admissible observer-section groupoid, transport connection, orientation
  behavior, and a sourced $S_3$ reduction;
- the physical positive cone or state functional, observable algebra,
  probability rule, preparation, and record channel;
- a native absolute scale, target-independent normalization, and distinctive
  prediction;
- any claim that the external 3-primary slot produces a rank-three physical
  family object rather than merely locating a possible order-three datum.

These gaps are recorded in `RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md`, `VERIFICATION.md`,
`lab/process/source-object-interface-contract.md`, and
`explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`. Standard
BV-BFV, BRST, APS, index, superconnection, or positive-Hilbert machinery can
state comparators and conditional theorems; it cannot fill a missing GU-
specific source choice without becoming an explicit import.

## Claim-status recommendation

Make **no claim-status, canon, verdict, or public-posture change** from this
panel. Preserve the exact observer-grade branching and bounded no-go results
at their earned grades. Keep the native physicalization, three physical
families, global $S_3$ dictionary, operational quantum sector, and joint
four-sector recovery open and unearned.

Even `B5-MIDDLE-CONSTRUCTION` would initially be a candidate-level conditional
advance requiring global, observer-natural, operational, and four-sector
validation. `B5-MIDDLE-OBSTRUCTION` would close only the exhaustively frozen
native middle class. `B5-MIDDLE-SOURCE-GAP` would justify sharper language
about missing source specification, not a global GU falsification.
