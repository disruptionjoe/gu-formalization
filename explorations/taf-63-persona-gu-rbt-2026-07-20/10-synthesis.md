# TaF 63-persona roses, buds, and thorns on GU

## Disposition

**Outcome: `RBT-COHERENT`.** The panel found a coherent center of gravity across
mathematical, physical, computational, philosophical, systems, operational,
and cross-domain lenses. GU's strongest current asset is not completed
unification physics. It is an unusually explicit system for preserving exact
partial mathematics, adverse results, construction identity, and the boundary
between what is located and what is forced.

The same panel converged on one controlling scientific liability: the native
source-owned physicalization object is absent and is being asked to carry too
many downstream obligations. The best next move is therefore the narrowest
source-traceable test of that object: disposition the source-licensed middle
differential class against mirror-complement acyclicity, native real/Krein
closure, and an explicit deformation-retract certificate.

This result does not change a GU theorem, physics claim, lane, canon, verdict,
or public posture. Persona agreement maps recurring dependencies and risks; it
is not a confidence multiplier for GU.

## Coverage and limits

The canonical TaF roster supplied P01-P63, including P63, even though two
stale TaF helper surfaces still say 62. Nine seven-person batches were completed
before any response was exposed to synthesis. The mechanical gate found:

- exactly 63 persona headings, with every integer P01-P63 appearing once;
- all 12 required fields in every response;
- no synthesis heading in an independent batch; and
- no panel-driven change to claim status, canon, verdict, or public posture.

The counts below use one persona's explicit rose, bud, or thorn as the unit.
Clusters overlap. The common brief already named several central gaps, so the
counts measure independent uptake, sharpening, and prioritization under a
shared dossier, not 63 independent discoveries or votes on truth. The complete
counting rule and persona lists are in `11-convergence-audit.md`; minority and
scope checks are in `12-dissent-and-viewpoint-audit.md`; dependency-ranked
execution is in `13-strategy-audit.md`.

## Roses: what should be preserved

### 1. Disciplined partial knowledge

All 63 principal roses concerned method, construction typing, exact bounded
mathematics, correction behavior, or explicit visibility of missing arrows.
None treated the Standard Model, a physical quantum sector, Einstein dynamics,
or cosmology as natively recovered. This strongly supports the honesty boundary
in `VERIFICATION.md` and `RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md` without raising any scientific
claim grade.

### 2. Corrections are reusable scientific assets

Twenty-eight personas explicitly valued corrections, retractions, retained
negative results, or narrowed overclaims. The retired (T_{192}) family
dictionary, the distinction between compact and noncompact operator results,
and construction-relative no-go scope were repeatedly cited as concrete
strengths. The repo does not merely admit error; it often converts an error into
a sharper future gate.

### 3. Objects, stages, and constructions are increasingly typed

Twenty-eight personas explicitly praised distinctions such as observer
pullback versus physical reduction, multiplicity versus family identity,
Rung-1 hosting versus Rung-2 selection, local versus global objects, and native
GU versus conventional physics. `GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md` and the recent
observer-view adjudication are important anti-category-error artifacts.

### 4. Bounded certificates are valuable because their limits are visible

Thirteen personas highlighted exact or executable partial results whose scope
is stated: formal residual selection, representation and topological
obstructions, graph-domain criteria, finite character ledgers, and typed BV
requirements. The strength is the combination of a hard receipt and an honest
limit, not the number of scripts or pages.

## Buds: the highest-leverage growth directions

### 1. Construct or obstruct a narrow native physicalization class

Thirty-one personas proposed a version of this move. Freeze a source-delimited
middle differential and determine whether it can make
(3E_-\oplus X) acyclic while retaining all three provenance-distinguished
(E_+) lines. This is the highest-leverage bud because it attacks the earliest
unresolved arrow shared by family interpretation, operational states,
(S_3) transport, and the four-sector weld.

### 2. Test explanatory compression rather than joint hosting

Twenty-one personas proposed held-out constraints, target-blind commitment,
perturbation or ablation, dependency stress, or explicit source-cost
accounting. One named source object is not necessarily one small import. Its
cost must reflect how much quotienting, symmetry breaking, normalization,
boundary choice, family labeling, and selection power it contains.

### 3. Make claim, fork, and viewpoint dependencies queryable

Fifteen personas proposed a claim/provenance DAG, stable claim IDs, semantic
diffs, a controlled vocabulary, or minimal proof-carrying packets. The bounded
version is valuable: generate a small current-state manifest from owner truth
that records grade, weakest dependency, construction fork, viewpoint level,
and supersession. This is support work for the scientific swing, not a new
infrastructure program.

### 4. Globalize only after physical cohomology exists

Twelve personas proposed descent, base change, rank stability, observer-loop
transport, holonomy, or an explicit (S_3)-reduction. These are indispensable
follow-on gates, but they cannot act on a physical family bundle before the
physicalization map has constructed one.

### 5. Close the source-domain and uniform-continuum gap

Seven personas separately targeted the noncompact analytic theorem: a
source-derived boundary or asymptotic relation plus uniform inverse/graph
estimates. This remains important third-person theorem hardening. It does not,
by itself, create a first-person sector reader or physical family object.

## Thorns: the liabilities that control the program

### 1. Missing source object and dependency concentration

Thirty-seven personas explicitly identified an absent, untyped, or
underconstrained source-owned arrow behind physical reduction, mirror removal,
family identity, positivity, global transport, dynamics, or operator-domain
selection. This is not a new global falsification; the repo already admits the
gap. The sharper warning is concentration: several apparent advances share one
missing dependency and should not be counted as independent reasons for
physical success.

### 2. Local success may not globalize physically

Twenty-seven personas rejected an inference from a fiber, compact collar,
finite fixture, representation multiplicity, or local typed map to a global
continuum physical sector. Descent, uniform estimates, global cohomology,
dynamics, and operational semantics are distinct gates, not details to be
folded into a local success statement.

### 3. Flexible adapters can imitate unification

Twenty personas warned about target leakage, hidden retuning, unpriced
expressive capacity, and post-selected domains or normalizations. A source
object that is allowed to choose the desired chirality, family frame, boundary
phase, scale, and four independent sector maps can host nearly any result while
selecting none of it.

### 4. Repository scale can reintroduce invalid casts

Fifteen personas warned that Markdown, JSON, tests, papers, and historical
corrections can drift semantically without a line-level merge conflict. In
particular, overloaded uses of *observer*, *family*, *source*, *outside*, and
*selection* can recreate an arrow the mathematics still lacks. This is a
repository-consistency risk, not physics evidence.

### 5. Operational quantum meaning remains unsatisfied

Eleven personas directly required positivity, representative independence,
observables, probability normalization, stable dynamics, or a record channel.
A native Krein carrier or deformation retract is not automatically a quantum
operational theory.

## First-person observer versus third-person geometric object

The panel's most important interpretive result is a four-stage stack:

```text
third-person GU carrier on Y14
    -- Obs_g: pullback and subgroup restriction -->
section-relative geometric field on (X4,g)
    -- Fam_g: source-owned BV-BFV differential, domain, quotient,
              low-energy reduction, and families pushforward -->
positive physical cohomology with exactly the retained provenance lines
    -- Ref_a: preparation, effect, and representative-independent record -->
first-person operational statistics
```

This separation dissolves two apparent conflicts. First, the unequal ambient
carrier dimensions ((128,128,1664)) do not directly contradict repeated
observer-facing charge content; restriction changes the representation object.
Second, a first-person reference may read, orient, or label an already physical
object without being the upstream mechanism that creates it.

It does **not** dissolve the load-bearing problems:

- `Obs_g` does not make (3E_-\oplus X) acyclic;
- a chosen observer axis, orientation, or (S_3) frame enlarges the object and
  must be priced and transformed explicitly;
- local triples need not descend to one global family bundle or have holonomy
  in (S_3);
- a readable label is not a positive normalized probability rule; and
- viewpoint separation cannot rescue the exact obstruction to treating the
  three (T_{192}) Cartan weights as three invariant families.

The first-/third-person distinction is therefore a successful category-error
correction, not the missing physicalization result. Geometric first person and
operational first person must also remain separate meanings of "observer."

## Dissent that must remain visible

### Minimal obstruction versus full North-Star weld

P52 argues for the smallest observer-natural, real/Krein-compatible symbol and
acyclicity test before building the full BV-BFV apparatus. P53 warns that a
clean local theorem can become byproduct capture if it does not constrain the
one shared source supporting all sectors.

The right reconciliation is conditional: run the minimal test only on a
source-traceable candidate class, and require every outcome to update the
shared source interface and construction map. If the source cannot bound the
class, return a source gap rather than designing a favorable differential.

### Externality localizes a missing resource but does not explain it

The 2-primary/3-primary and index obstructions genuinely show where odd or
order-three information cannot originate. Other personas correctly warn that
"outside" becomes immunizing if every missing structure is simply moved into
an underpriced external datum. Explanation additionally requires an
independently sourced object, exact transport, cost ledger, and distinctive
consequence.

### A native success may still be empirically non-distinctive

P25 supplies the strongest rare warning: a native Krein/BV completion and a
conventional positive-Hilbert/BRST completion could produce the same records.
Even a future native physicalization would need a native-only observable,
lower fixed input cost, or another discriminating consequence before it could
decide between the constructions physically.

### Persona agreement is correlated evidence

P18, P38, and P56 warn that tests and personas share assumptions, fixtures,
briefs, and dependency graphs. The panel is useful because different lenses
translate shared gaps into compatible gates. Its numerical agreement is not
63 independent scientific witnesses.

## Next powerful swing

### Objective

**Disposition the complete source-licensed middle-differential class.** The
target is the missing map between section-relative third-person data and
physical cohomology, not a downstream first-person reading.

### Sequential gates with parallel lenses inside each gate

1. **Source and type freeze.** Extract the exact rolled matrix, filtration,
   lower-order terms, admissible domain/boundary data, real structure, and
   super-IG role. Keep native GU and conventional comparator specifications
   separate.
2. **Class completeness.** Enumerate the full observer-natural first-order
   symbol space licensed by that source. Independently audit representation
   completeness, native real/Krein closure, and provenance. Freeze planted
   mirror-preserving, non-Krein mirror-breaking, and acyclic controls.
3. **Cheap decisive gates.** Test mirror commutation, square-zero or declared
   curved-(L_\infty) closure, then symbol exactness/ellipticity on every
   unwanted irreducible. Stop a candidate at its first failed gate.
4. **Global gate for survivors only.** Fix lower-order terms and a source-owned
   domain, compute global BV cohomology, and demand an explicit mapping-cone
   contraction of (3E_-\oplus X) while preserving all three (E_+)
   provenance lines.

### Predeclared outcomes

- **`B5-MIDDLE-CONSTRUCTION`:** a source-delimited native candidate passes
  closure/domain and supplies the explicit deformation retract. This advances
  one candidate only; descent, (S_3), positivity, continuum, and four-sector
  gates remain.
- **`B5-MIDDLE-OBSTRUCTION`:** class completeness is established and every
  source-compatible candidate fails mirror, native closure, symbol exactness,
  or global cohomology. This closes that frozen native class, not conventional
  BRST/Higgs/positive-Hilbert constructions or all possible GU sources.
- **`B5-MIDDLE-SOURCE-GAP`:** available sources do not bound an honest
  symbol/action/domain class. This is a decisive provenance result, not a
  mathematical GU no-go.
- **`B5-MIDDLE-METHOD-OBSTRUCTION`:** enumeration or planted controls cannot
  distinguish the intended cases. No physical inference follows.

Stop rather than enlarge the class after source failure, unproved enumeration
completeness, post-target coefficient or boundary choice, a failed planted
control, or an early mirror/closure/symbol gate. A conventional construction
may continue as a separately priced comparator but never as a native rescue or
native kill.

## Follow-on dependency order

1. Lift a local survivor to a native cyclic BV-BFV/(L_\infty) construction
   with source-owned domain, master-equation and anomaly gates, and global
   deformation retract.
2. Build the physical family over the observer groupoid and test constant rank,
   descent, orientation action, loop holonomy, and (S_3)-reduction.
3. Construct a positive operational state/effect algebra and record channel;
   test representative independence, probability, and stability.
4. Serialize one immutable source packet and run all four sector cards plus a
   held-out prediction and serious conventional comparator without retuning.

The continuum operator theorem can proceed as orthogonal theorem hardening if
it uses a source-owned domain and does not displace the middle-differential
swing. More (S_3) transport work should wait until there is a physical family
object to transport.

## Claim-status recommendation

Make **no claim-status, canon, verdict, Lane, or public-posture change** from
this panel. Preserve the exact observer-grade branching, formal residual
theorem, representation/topology no-gos, and bounded operator results at their
earned grades. Keep native physicalization, three physical families, global
(S_3) transport, operational quantum meaning, and the four-sector recovery
open and unearned.

Even `B5-MIDDLE-CONSTRUCTION` would initially be a candidate-level advance.
`B5-MIDDLE-OBSTRUCTION` would close only an exhaustively frozen class.
`B5-MIDDLE-SOURCE-GAP` would justify sharper language about absent source data,
not a global falsification of GU.
