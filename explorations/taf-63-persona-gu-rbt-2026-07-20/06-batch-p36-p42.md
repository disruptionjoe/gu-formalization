# Independent responses: P36-P42

### P36. Access-Control Systems Expert

- **Summary of understanding:** GU behaves as though claims carry permissions: L1 proof may authorize an exact mathematical statement, L2 computation authorizes only a model result, and L3 interpretation does not inherit either authority automatically. The Rung 0/1/2 ladder similarly limits what a construction survivor may claim. This is a **cross-domain analogy**, not a reduction of research epistemology to computer security; the evidence is the explicit grading in `VERIFICATION.md` and `lab/process/construction-space-exploration-protocol.md`.
- **Strongest insight:** The repository's most valuable security property is least privilege for inference. A Rung-0 consistency pass cannot exercise Rung-2 unification authority, and a result obtained under the conventional positive-Hilbert/BRST construction cannot silently exercise authority in the native `Sp(32,32;H)`/Krein/BV-BFV construction. `GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md` states the necessary jurisdiction boundary.
- **Strongest criticism:** Most of this access control is enforced by prose, reader discipline, and correction banners rather than by an executable authorization graph. In a large repository with stale historical surfaces, a copied sentence can shed its level, assumptions, construction fork, or supersession status and thereby undergo an accidental privilege escalation. This is a repository risk, not evidence that such an escalation has already changed a current verdict.
- **Hidden assumptions:** Readers follow every evidence pointer; correction and retraction status propagates to all downstream uses; dependency metadata remain current; and the standing boundary/source-object assumption is never treated as ambient permission to claim a native construction. The source-owner field in `lab/process/construction-space-map.json` is assumed to constrain, rather than merely annotate, downstream use.
- **Rose:** `VERIFICATION.md` gives outsiders an unusually clear authority boundary, including the explicit concession that the physical observer story is not established. The construction-space protocol also forbids Rung-0/1 survivors from impersonating Rung-2 selection.
- **Bud:** Add a machine-readable claim-capability ledger in which each claim records grade, assumptions, evidence, construction fork, supersession relation, and the strongest downstream claim class it may authorize. A lightweight linter could then reject an unlicensed L2-to-L3 or conventional-to-native cast before prose hardening.
- **Thorn:** The missing `Fam_g` arrow in `explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md` is an authorization gap: section restriction has authority to create an observer-relative representation, not a physical state quotient or three-family observable. Treating `3E_+\oplus3E_-\oplus X` as physical families would exceed the native construction's present permissions; importing ordinary BRST or a positive polarization could define a conventional object but would not grant it native GU authority.
- **Confidence:** 8/10
- **Suggested experiment:** Sample fifty high-level claim uses across current program, paper, canon, and exploration surfaces. Trace each to its evidence, grade, fork, assumptions, and corrections; then plant five deliberate unauthorized casts and test whether an automated policy checker and a blinded human audit both catch them.
- **Suggested theorem or mathematical direction:** Formalize a noninterference theorem for the claim graph: absent an explicit, checked coercion certificate, changes in L2 models or L3 interpretations cannot authorize new L1 propositions, and conventional-fork certificates cannot discharge native-fork obligations. The theorem would be about the repository's proof/claim calculus, not GU physics.
- **Suggested falsification test:** If a representative audit finds every current high-level claim fully traceable, every correction transitively inherited, and all planted grade/fork escalations rejected, then the alleged access-control weakness is not operationally important at the tested scale.
- **Evidence touched:** `VERIFICATION.md`; `GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`; `lab/process/construction-space-exploration-protocol.md`; `lab/process/construction-space-map.json`; `explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md`.

### P37. Type-System Designer

- **Summary of understanding:** GU has an increasingly explicit informal type system: third-person fields on `Y14`, section-relative fields, physical cohomology/family slots, and first-person records are different object types connected by different operations. Evidence level, construction fork, and Rung grade act as additional type indices. This programming-language framing is a **cross-domain analogy**, not a claim that the mathematical categories are literally compiler types.
- **Strongest insight:** The four-level stack in `explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md` identifies the decisive type error: `Obs_g` is a restriction/pullback, while `Fam_g` must include BV/BRST cohomology, gauge quotient, low-energy reduction, and families pushforward. The former cannot be implicitly coerced to the latter.
- **Strongest criticism:** The program's load-bearing physicalization term does not yet type-check because `Fam_g` lacks a source-owned definition, domain, differential, quotient, and target. The exact branching `3E_+\oplus3E_-\oplus X` in `explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md` is therefore representation-typed, not physical-family-typed. The conventional BRST/Higgs/integration-out route is a legitimate foreign implementation of a different interface, but importing it without an explicit adapter would not compile as the native Krein/super-IG construction.
- **Hidden assumptions:** Pullback commutes with the future differential and boundary data; the family pushforward is natural in the metric section; mirror removal and the 1536-mode acyclization preserve the required symmetries; and an external `S_3` local system can be matched to the rank-three multiplicity bundle without target-specific labeling.
- **Rose:** The repository caught and corrected the `T192` cast: three Cartan weight spaces in one spin-1 carrier are not three invariant family copies. That is exactly the kind of type preservation check that prevents a suggestive dimension count from becoming a physical claim.
- **Bud:** Specify B.5 as a typed intermediate representation with constructors for pullback, BV differential, gauge quotient, low-energy projection, mapping-cone acyclization, families pushforward, and record evaluation. Require every transformation to expose its symmetry level, domain, boundary data, and native/imported provenance before any proof search.
- **Thorn:** The overload of “observer” still permits implicit macro expansion from geometric section choice to physical reduction to operational readout. Unless these are separate symbols everywhere, a valid native observer restriction can be expanded into a nonexistent physical-family program. Conversely, a no-go for one expansion must not reject the conventional construction or all possible native differentials.
- **Confidence:** 9/10
- **Suggested experiment:** Encode ten current generation/observer arguments as typed diagrams and run a static audit that forbids arrows not declared in the four-level stack. Include planted errors: `T192 -> family`, `g^* -> BV quotient`, L2 model -> continuum theorem, and conventional polarization -> native physicalization.
- **Suggested theorem or mathematical direction:** Define a category of admissible metric sections and a derived physicalization functor `P_{S,g}`. Extend the released mirror lemma to prove that any natural, mirror-equivariant chain map preserves paired chiral cohomology; then identify the minimal typed datum that permits a non-equivariant but source-owned deformation retract onto `E_g^+\otimes M_g`.
- **Suggested falsification test:** Produce the source-owned natural deformation retract demanded in `explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`, with an acyclic mirror/extra-mode cone and checked symmetry/domain obligations. That construction would falsify the current “does not type-check” diagnosis for B.5.
- **Evidence touched:** `explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md`; `explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`; `GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`; `VERIFICATION.md`.

### P38. Financial Risk Modeler

- **Summary of understanding:** GU holds a portfolio of exact theorems, finite computations, conditional constructions, physical interpretations, and explicit breaks. The portfolio is not diversified merely because it contains many artifacts: several fronts share the same unresolved physicalization, source-domain, boundary-adapter, or normalization factors. Portfolio and tail-risk language here is a **cross-domain analogy**, not a statistical estimate of GU's truth probability.
- **Strongest insight:** Pre-registration, planted controls, construction-relative kills, and public retractions act like effective model-risk controls. `VERIFICATION.md` includes adverse outcomes rather than hiding them, while `lab/process/construction-space-exploration-protocol.md` predeclares what an empty joint intersection and a conditional survivor would mean.
- **Strongest criticism:** Confidence can be overstated if conditionally positive results are treated as independent confirmations. The source-object interface in `lab/process/construction-space-map.json` is a common latent factor across GR, QM, cosmology, and SM tracks; no source instance exists. Failure of native physicalization could therefore downgrade several apparently separate opportunities at once. Conventional positive-Hilbert/BRST success would hedge physical viability only by switching constructions, not by rescuing this native dependency.
- **Hidden assumptions:** Numerical and formal errors are weakly correlated; finite-model controls capture the relevant continuum tails; dependency pointers expose all common factors; normalization choices do not couple results thought to be separate; and historical corrections have fully propagated.
- **Rose:** The repository prices uncertainty more honestly than most speculative programs: L1/L2/L3 separation, explicit `OPEN`/conditional labels, named falsifiers, and a retraction as a flagship credibility anchor reduce the chance that one attractive result masks the portfolio's real exposure.
- **Bud:** Build a claim-dependency stress graph and report minimal cut sets: which small sets of assumptions, domains, source objects, or construction choices simultaneously support the most downstream claims? Report survival under native-only, conventional-only, no-boundary-adapter, and no-physicalization scenarios rather than one blended confidence score.
- **Thorn:** The strongest systemic exposure is concentration in an uninstantiated source object. C10/C11 conditional grades and the B.5 program can remain useful search assets, but they should not be counted as several independent reasons to expect physical success while they share that one missing factor.
- **Confidence:** 8/10
- **Suggested experiment:** Remove or negate each high-centrality dependency in turn—native BV-BFV source, boundary relation, chirality selector, absolute scale, and normalization convention—and automatically recompute which claims retain their stated grade. Add a correlated two-factor stress in which physicalization and source-owned boundary data fail together.
- **Suggested theorem or mathematical direction:** Prove monotonicity and cut-set results for a typed claim DAG: weakening or deleting an assumption cannot increase an earned grade, and every multi-sector Rung-1 survivor has a computable minimal dependency cut. This would formalize robustness accounting without assigning speculative probabilities.
- **Suggested falsification test:** If dependency reconstruction shows no small common cut capable of downgrading multiple flagship or top-candidate results, and native/conventional fork separation leaves the main portfolio robust, then the concentration-risk criticism is falsified.
- **Evidence touched:** `VERIFICATION.md`; `lab/process/construction-space-exploration-protocol.md`; `lab/process/construction-space-map.json`; `explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`; `RESEARCH-STATUS.md`.

### P39. Economist

- **Summary of understanding:** The construction-space program allocates scarce research effort across candidate cells while trying to prevent flexible constructions from earning credit through hidden tuning. Rungs act as product grades, typed imports as costs, and sharp constraint lists as entry conditions. This market-design vocabulary is a **cross-domain analogy**, not evidence for an economic mechanism in GU.
- **Strongest insight:** Import counting and the expressiveness guard in `lab/process/construction-space-exploration-protocol.md` create good incentives: merely hosting known physics is not rewarded as unification, and only native Rung-2 selection supports the charter claim. This reduces the payoff to relabeling fitted data as derivation.
- **Strongest criticism:** `RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md` asks whether GU is a more compressed, parsimonious unifying fit than alternatives, but the repository does not yet define a common price system for imports, complexity, parameter tuning, or failed predictions across rival frameworks. An import count treats unlike choices as equal units, and a large expressive construction may look cheap if one powerful adapter is counted as “one.”
- **Hidden assumptions:** Imports have comparable cost; candidate generation is not biased toward familiar GU structures; the constraint lists are hard enough to prevent Goodharting; research attention follows information gain rather than artifact completion; and conventional repairs are neither unfairly discounted nor allowed to masquerade as native selection.
- **Rose:** The Rung ladder correctly separates compatibility, hosting, and selection, so the repo's institutional incentives at least point toward the right scarce good: target-independent explanatory compression rather than accumulating matches.
- **Bud:** Run a target-blind model-selection tournament with a frozen description language, held-out constraints, and explicit costs for parameters, external structures, sector-specific adapters, and unfixed domains. Compare the native C10/C11 candidates with at least one conventional physics construction on identical outputs, while keeping fork-specific claims separate.
- **Thorn:** Without shadow prices for adapter expressiveness and source-object complexity, “one welded payload” could conceal many choices and allow conditional survivors to crowd out sharper alternatives. The native construction may ultimately be cheaper, but native provenance alone is not a zero price; conversely, the familiarity of standard BRST or a positive Hilbert space does not make the conventional route free.
- **Confidence:** 8/10
- **Suggested experiment:** Freeze a set of training constraints and genuinely held-out consequences, a coding language for constructions, and a cost rule before fitting. Let each candidate spend the same tuning budget; then measure survival, held-out accuracy, description length, and number of sector-specific repairs.
- **Suggested theorem or mathematical direction:** Formalize the expressiveness guard as an identifiability result: if a construction admits unrestricted sector-specific adapters, Rung-1 hosting has zero discriminatory value; under a bounded adapter class and held-out constraints, a consistent complexity penalty can distinguish reusable structure from retuning.
- **Suggested falsification test:** A native GU construction that reaches Rung 2, predicts a held-out sharp constraint without retuning, and does so at lower predeclared description cost than the frozen comparison class would defeat this criticism in the tested domain.
- **Evidence touched:** `RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md`; `lab/process/construction-space-exploration-protocol.md`; `lab/process/construction-space-map.json`; `GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`.

### P40. Legal Scholar

- **Summary of understanding:** GU is governed by a hierarchy of authority: exact proofs, model computations, interpretations, active-research records, corrections, and historical explorations have different precedential weight. No-go holdings also have limited jurisdiction over their stated assumptions and construction fork. The legal framing is a **cross-domain analogy**, not a claim that scientific truth is decided by precedent.
- **Strongest insight:** The repo explicitly practices overruling, narrowing, and distinguishing. `VERIFICATION.md` foregrounds a retracted modular realization, `RESEARCH-STATUS.md` records corrections, and `GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md` requires that a no-go be confined to the native or conventional construction it actually adjudicated.
- **Strongest criticism:** Controlling authority is distributed across a very large record in which superseded propositions may remain readable behind banners. Without a machine-readable citator, a downstream document can rely on an overruled holding or apply a valid scoped no-go outside its jurisdiction. The retired `T192` family dictionary demonstrates why “same terminology” is not sufficient precedent for a new object.
- **Hidden assumptions:** Every correction identifies all affected descendants; current summaries always outrank historical narrative in practice; readers know which file controls; citations preserve assumption scope; and repo sovereignty/ownership boundaries prevent an external source claim from becoming GU evidence without adjudication.
- **Rose:** The willingness to publish adverse holdings and preserve an honesty boundary is institutionally strong. The promotion rule in `RESEARCH-STATUS.md` also demands that no stale stronger status remain before promotion, which is the right doctrine even if enforcement is difficult.
- **Bud:** Add a claim citator: each proposition receives a stable identifier, controlling-status record, jurisdiction tuple (grade, assumptions, native/conventional fork, object, domain), and treatment edges such as affirmed, narrowed, distinguished, superseded, or overruled. Generate an automated report whenever a current surface cites a noncontrolling claim.
- **Thorn:** A scoped judgment can leak into a program-wide verdict. For example, the full-symmetry obstruction to the `T192` dictionary binds that proposed bridge; it does not overrule every first-order native B.5 route. Likewise, a successful conventional BRST reduction would govern that construction but would not establish the source-owned GU mechanism.
- **Confidence:** 9/10
- **Suggested experiment:** Audit fifty randomly selected current claim dependencies from papers, canon, status, and active explorations. For each, identify controlling authority, construction jurisdiction, subsequent treatment, and whether every cited proposition remains good law at its used scope.
- **Suggested theorem or mathematical direction:** Model the claim corpus in a paraconsistent, nonmonotonic logic with explicit defeat and jurisdiction relations. Prove that an overruled or out-of-scope premise cannot propagate into the generated current-verdict view, while preserving historical records without explosion.
- **Suggested falsification test:** If the random audit finds that all operative claims cite current controlling evidence, every correction propagates transitively, and no no-go is applied beyond its construction/domain, then the precedent-propagation concern is not substantiated.
- **Evidence touched:** `VERIFICATION.md`; `RESEARCH-STATUS.md`; `GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`; `explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md`; `explorations/observation-to-family-b5-campaign-2026-07-20.md`.

### P41. Linguist

- **Summary of understanding:** GU coordinates several technical registers in which the same words—“observer,” “source,” “family,” “outside,” “physical,” “selection,” and “metric”—can denote different objects or operations. The repo has begun to disambiguate them through fork tables and a four-level observer stack. Linguistic analysis here is a **cross-domain analogy and terminology audit**, not mathematical evidence.
- **Strongest insight:** The first-person/third-person adjudication is fundamentally a semantic-role correction with mathematical consequences. “Observer” can be the agent/section that restricts an ambient field or the experiencer/reference that receives a record; neither role denotes the missing physicalization process. Once those roles are separated, a dimension mismatch ceases to be an automatic contradiction while the missing `Fam_g` arrow becomes visible.
- **Strongest criticism:** Core lexemes still invite equivocation. “Three copies” can mean isotypic multiplicity, Cartan weight spaces, labeled family lines, or physical low-energy generations; “source” can mean a manuscript, a BV source action, or external boundary data. Context often repairs the meaning, but a long cross-disciplinary corpus makes that repair unreliable.
- **Hidden assumptions:** Readers share the same domain lexicon; notation fixes semantic roles consistently; translations from podcast language to representation theory preserve entailment; “outside” has a defined geometric or categorical complement rather than only a narrative contrast; and same-named native and conventional objects are recognized as non-synonymous.
- **Rose:** `GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md` is a strong contrastive glossary: it shows that gauge group, ghost clearance, generation count, metric, and RS cure are not translation equivalents across the two constructions. The four-stage diagram in `explorations/source-wave-observer-view-adjudication-2026-07-20.md` likewise turns viewpoint into explicit argument roles.
- **Bud:** Create a controlled vocabulary with stable sense IDs and typed semantic frames—for example `observer.section`, `observer.record`, `source.manuscript`, `source.BV`, `source.external`, `family.rep-multiplicity`, and `family.physical`. Annotate load-bearing claims and lint any sentence that moves between senses without a named map.
- **Thorn:** Semantic convergence can masquerade as mathematical convergence. Calling the exact `3E_+` multiplicity “three generations” or calling a Cartan triplet “three families” supplies the desired reading lexically before BV cohomology, mirror removal, and low-energy multiplicity have supplied it formally. A familiar conventional term also cannot be translated into the native GU fork without an explicit construction map.
- **Confidence:** 9/10
- **Suggested experiment:** Build a concordance of the seven high-risk terms across `RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md`, `VERIFICATION.md`, `RESEARCH-STATUS.md`, and the two 2026-07-20 observer/B.5 reports. Have blinded technical readers assign sense IDs and arrow types; measure agreement and locate sentences whose interpretation changes the claim grade.
- **Suggested theorem or mathematical direction:** Give the four-level observer stack a compositional semantics: objects carry viewpoint and construction indices, while pullback, physicalization, reference/preparation, and readout have distinct typed signatures. Prove that no well-formed derivation can infer a physical-family proposition from a restriction proposition without a `Fam_g` term.
- **Suggested falsification test:** If blinded readers achieve near-perfect sense agreement without the proposed lexicon and reconstruct the same typed dependency graph and claim grades, then semantic drift is not a material repository problem.

### P42. Poet / Literary Scholar

- **Summary of understanding:** GU's formal and computational record is accompanied by a powerful narrative vocabulary: observer universe, outside, boundary, blindness, mirror, sterile interior, and an outside that “lights” the inside. These images can organize inquiry, but they must not do inferential work that belongs to a construction. Literary analysis is explicitly a **cross-domain analogy**, not a criticism of the mathematics merely for using metaphor.
- **Strongest insight:** The repository shows unusual awareness that rhetoric and result are separable. `VERIFICATION.md` says to trust levels rather than adjectives, while correction receipts demonstrate that an attractive story can be surrendered without discarding valid mathematics. That separation is a genuine cultural strength.
- **Strongest criticism:** `RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md` can turn a localized absence into a satisfying creation story: a balanced, “sterile” interior receives chirality from an outside that makes a “living matter world.” The prose is memorable, but it risks making externality feel explanatory before the external datum is named, constrained, and transported through a native physicalization map.
- **Hidden assumptions:** Narrative closure tracks explanatory compression; “inside” and “outside” name precise mathematical regions; an externally supplied chirality is less arbitrary than other imports; mirror balance is physically sterile rather than merely representation-theoretic; and the observer metaphor maps consistently to either geometric section or operational record.
- **Rose:** The metaphors are productive when quarantined as hypotheses: “located, not forced” compactly names a real distinction, and the honesty boundary prevents the phrase from being treated as a derivation of three. The visible retractions give the rhetoric an earned undertone of self-correction.
- **Bud:** Produce a shadow edition of the North Star and B.5 story in deliberately metaphor-free language. Link every retained noun to a defined object, every verb to a typed map, and every causal phrase to a theorem or explicit conjecture; preserve the poetic version only as an orientation layer.
- **Thorn:** Metaphor can bridge the exact missing arrow: “observation,” “selection,” or “outside issuance” may sound like the act that turns `3E_+\oplus3E_-\oplus X` into physical families even though `Fam_g` is absent. The native geometry deserves its own terms, but poetic distinctiveness does not waive physical certificates; conventional familiarity likewise does not prove the positive-Hilbert/BRST fork is the right construction.
- **Confidence:** 8/10
- **Suggested experiment:** Give blinded reviewers a metaphor-rich and a formally equivalent metaphor-free version of the program frontier. Compare their confidence, inferred causal links, and recommended priorities; separately check whether either group mistakes section pullback for physicalization.
- **Suggested theorem or mathematical direction:** Define rhetorical invariance for a claim graph: replacing or deleting metaphorical gloss must leave formal premises, typed arrows, evidence grades, and conclusions unchanged. Prove invariance for the program's central “external chirality” argument or identify the exact sentence where it fails.
- **Suggested falsification test:** If blinded readers assign the same claim grades, causal dependencies, and next experiments to both versions, and every metaphor resolves to an exact object without supplying a missing premise, then the overextension concern is falsified for the tested corpus.
