# Convergence audit

## Scope and counting rule

This audit was performed only after the coverage gate reported exactly one
complete response for every persona P01-P63. It is a content audit, not a vote
on GU's truth.

The counting unit is one persona's explicit **Rose**, **Bud**, or **Thorn**
field. A persona is counted at most once in a cluster when that field directly
states the cluster's proposition or proposes its corresponding action. Clusters
may overlap, so their counts do not sum to 63. Counts are accompanied by the
persona IDs that produced them. I did not infer a theme merely from a persona's
discipline or from its confidence score.

There is an important priming limitation. The frozen common brief already
named the physicalization gap, the observer/physical distinction, the
construction fork, the operator-domain limitation, and several current buds.
The counts therefore measure **independent uptake, sharpening, and
prioritization under a common dossier**, not 63 independent discoveries of the
same facts. Convergence is strongest when different disciplines turn the same
admitted issue into compatible tests or theorem targets.

## Rose convergence

### R1. The earned strength is disciplined partial knowledge, not completed physics (63/63)

Every persona, P01-P63, placed its principal rose in one or more of the
following: evidence grading, correction behavior, construction typing,
predeclared falsification, exact bounded mathematics, provenance, or explicit
visibility of the missing physical arrow. No rose claimed that the Standard
Model, a physical quantum sector, Einstein dynamics, or cosmology had been
natively recovered. This is the strongest panel-wide convergence and it is
consistent with `VERIFICATION.md`, `RESEARCH-PROGRAM.md`, and
`GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`.

This finding supports the repository's present honesty boundary; it does not
raise the grade of any GU physics claim.

This is the deepest convergence, but it does not newly falsify GU: the gap is

many sectors because it contains the answers; a failed source can collapse
many apparently separate advances at once.

## Construction-fork audit

The mandatory fork survived the panel instead of being averaged away.

- **51/63 thorns explicitly named native GU/Krein/BV-BFV/source-owned scope:**
  P01-P09, P11-P14, P22-P37, P39-P43, P45-P46, P48, and P50-P63.
- **49/63 thorns explicitly named a conventional positive-Hilbert, BRST,
  Higgs, APS, replicated-family, or generic conventional comparator:** P01-P09,
  P11-P13, P22-P37, P39-P43, P45, P48, and P50-P63.

Forty-nine personas did both explicitly. Their common disposition is not
"native good, conventional bad." It is:

1. a native no-go kills only the native construction class whose assumptions
   it covers;
2. a conventional construction may provide a sound host or comparator;
3. importing that construction does not establish native GU selection; and
4. a native survivor does not automatically validate standard positive-Hilbert
   physics.

The main fork-dependent disagreement is therefore about **what success would
mean**, not about which current calculation passed. Philosophical and
operational lenses (P25, P30) also warn that the two forks could be
observationally underdetermined until a discriminating observable exists.
Specialist lenses (P46, P48, P50) keep the native mirror/BV obstruction
separate from a conventional completion.

## Direct GU judgments versus cross-domain analogies

### Direct, object-level judgments

The most authoritative convergence comes from responses that cited exact GU
objects and matched them to a same-domain test:

- P01-P08 and P45-P50: the exact branching, first-order symbol class,
  mirror-chain condition, BV master equation, families index, and physical
  quotient;
- P02-P04, P12, P17, P45, P58-P59: descent, base change, rank stability, and
  observer-loop holonomy;
- P03, P15, P18, P47, P59, P62: compact/noncompact and finite/uniform analytic
  quantifiers;
- P07-P08, P25, P30, P48: positivity, observables, probability, and operational
  readout.

These judgments are anchored in the B.5, observer-view, and continuum-pencil
explorations and can legitimately shape the mathematical work queue.

### Cross-domain contributions

The systems, learning, legal, language, game, ecology, economy, bandwidth, and
metabolic lenses supplied useful **design analogies**: claim DAGs, typed
intermediate representations, stress tests, cost ledgers, reconciliation
tests, and evidence packets. Their executable translations can improve the
repo or its test design, but their native vocabulary is not evidence for a GU
object.

The responses themselves usually respected that limit. P28, P29, P43, P44,
and P57-P63 explicitly labeled their cross-domain transfers. P63 gave the
clearest negative control: no metabolic exponent is licensed until an actual
GU source defines the requisite conserved flow, hierarchy, terminals, and
optimization problem. The synthesis should preserve this restraint and not
turn broad disciplinary agreement into physics corroboration.

## Confidence pattern

The 63 reported confidence values are: 7/10 from 7 personas, 8/10 from 25,
9/10 from 30, and 10/10 from 1; mean 8.40 and median 8. These values are best
read as confidence in each **evaluation**, not posterior probability that GU is
true or false. P06's sole 10/10 attaches to exact representation-theoretic
typing. Several analogy-heavy evaluations use 7/10 or 8/10, while P63's 9/10
attaches to a well-bounded negative transfer claim. No confidence aggregation
should change a claim grade.

## Audit disposition

Recommended preregistered outcome: **`RBT-COHERENT`**.

The panel converges across more than four genuinely different disciplinary
clusters on a small set of compatible conclusions:

1. preserve the repo's correction, grading, and construction-typing discipline;
2. keep the B.5/source-owned physicalization as the principal scientific hinge;
3. prevent source/adapter expressiveness and post-selection from masquerading
   as explanatory compression;
4. require global descent, operational positivity, and uniform continuum
   control after any local algebraic success; and
5. harden the claim/provenance surface so these distinctions survive repo scale.

This is not `RBT-THORN-DOMINANT`: the central thorns are already admitted and
reinforce rather than invalidate the current adversarial posture. It is not
`RBT-BUD-DOMINANT`: no new cross-domain idea clearly outranks the already
predeclared middle-differential/source-object swing. It is not
`RBT-FRAGMENTED`: the disciplinary variations mostly specify different gates
on the same construction rather than incompatible priorities. It is not
`RBT-METHOD-OBSTRUCTION`: the frozen evidence packet supported scoped judgments
from all 63 personas, although repo staleness remains a real thorn.

The audit-level recommendation to the final synthesis is to retain the
middle-differential/source physicalization swing as the next scientific move,
but make its acceptance packet explicitly include: a frozen narrow native
candidate class, a source/normalization/domain commitment before target use,
mirror-complement acyclicity, construction-fork identity, and predeclared
failure outcomes. Global descent, positive operational semantics, and
noncompact uniformity should be named as subsequent independent gates rather
than silently included in a local success.

No claim-status, canon, verdict, Lane, or public-posture change follows from
this audit.
