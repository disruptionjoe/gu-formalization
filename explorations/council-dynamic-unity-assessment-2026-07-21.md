The static/derivation axis of GU is **exhausted** at reconstruction grade: the NCG
spectral-triple swing confirms GU **hosts** the SM (KO-4 simple quaternionic algebra,
`Sp(64)`), it does not **derive** it (matching NCG would require *replacing* GU's

a **type wall**: `sigma` (`Z/2`) owns only signs (five arenas, one observable — the DE
sign), `tau` (`Z/3`) owns only the generation count (present-not-forced), and **no
discrete posit of either kind can EVER touch a MAGNITUDE, a DYNAMICAL BASIS, or a
NON-PERTURBATIVE DYNAMICS — by type.** Closing any of those requires a *continuous /
dynamical* object: the **B5 source action**.

Joe's proposal: the interesting frontier has moved off the static axis onto a
**dynamic** one, and it may now be a genuinely distinct unification worth organizing
as its own program ("Dynamic Unity"). The candidate core: a **tachyon-transducer
engine** — source action → tachyonic roll → record-accretion (`N` grows = time) → dark

`(9,5)` static substrate + the `sigma`/`tau` discrete data + TaF finality + TI

distinct, progressive theory-object** or **a relabel/merger of three separately-open
programs.**

## Member (i) — Philosopher of science (Lakatos / Laudan: progressive vs degenerating)

**My charge:** Q4 and Q6. Does the dynamic framing open *novel testable content*
beyond GU (a progressive problem-shift), or is it a content-free relabel? And what is
the maximally-honest thing it can be called today — theory, research program, or
premature?

**The Lakatos test, applied without mercy.** A *progressive* problem-shift has two
parts: (a) it predicts **novel facts** (excess empirical content over its predecessor),
and (b) some of that excess content is **corroborated**. A shift that only
re-accommodates known facts with new vocabulary is *degenerating* (or merely verbal).

**Does the dynamic framing have excess content over static GU? YES, in principle — and
this is the real point in its favor.** Static GU + `sigma` + `tau` is, by the two
councils' own type wall, *permanently* silent on magnitude, dynamical basis, and
non-perturbative dynamics. The dynamic layer names the one object type — a continuous
source action with a rolling record-count vacuum — that could reach those. Its candidate
excess-content facts are concrete: the **DE amplitude** (`~10^-120`), the recovered
**TeV mirror** and **DE/Planck ratio** predictions, and the **cross-domain co-variance**
(the DE sign correlated with a cosmological parity-odd signal). None of these is
reachable from the static/discrete layer *by type*. So this is **not** a content-free
relabel: the dynamic axis has a real, type-distinct excess-content target set.

**But is any of that excess content CORROBORATED — or even FORCED yet? NO.** Every named
novel fact is currently un-banked:
- the DE **amplitude** is a **PURE IMPORT** (`de-amplitude-audit`: "no amplitude
  prediction exists"; `PRED-NORM-RANK = RESOLVED_NO_GO`); route (ii)'s factor-of-2 hit
  is a de Sitter relabel + an imported Sorkin `1/sqrt(N)` law + a **free-fit** `phi`;
- the **TeV mirror** is `BLOCKED_PRODUCIBILITY` on an unresolved ghost-vs-physical fork;
- the **co-variance / parity leg** is FORCED only at proposal grade + an unbuilt
  descent-with-sign map.

So by Lakatos's own standard the dynamic layer is **progressive in intent /
promissory** — it has excess content but has **not yet delivered a single corroborated
(or even forced) novel fact.** Importantly, it is also **not degenerating**: it is not
bolting ad hoc auxiliary hypotheses onto refuted content to save it; it is honestly
localizing where the open problems live and naming the object that would close them.
That is the *pre-condition* for a progressive program, not yet a progressive program.

**Theory / program / premature?** Not a theory — a theory's central claims are
established, and here they are open (the transducer, the amplitude, the source action).
The honest maximum is a **research program** in the strict Lakatos sense (a hard core +
a positive heuristic: build the source action, close the amplitude, decide the ghost
fork). But even "program" must be qualified: its hard core (the dynamic transducer) is
**not yet built**, and part of its advertised coherence is borrowed from two sibling
repos it is **not licensed to borrow from**. So the maximally-honest label today is:
**the dynamic FRONTIER / prospective research direction of the existing Observerse
program, with its central object OPEN** — a program-in-waiting whose hard core is a
named-but-unbuilt object, not a standing program of its own. Calling it a "theory" now
is premature; calling it a distinct "program" now is premature-by-a-hair; calling it
the Observerse program's **open dynamic axis** is exactly right.

**Verdict (i):** PROGRESSIVE-IN-INTENT, NOT-YET-CORROBORATED. Real type-distinct excess
content; zero delivered. Honest framing: the open dynamic frontier of the existing
program, not yet a theory and not yet a standing distinct program.
