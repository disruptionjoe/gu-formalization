# Objective 3 -- pre-registered adversarial fork for "the wall becomes the prediction"

**What this document is.** A *pre-registration*. The observer conjecture's strongest move (`W94`,
`sectorial-relative-j-2026-07-11.md`) is a **reinterpretation**: the global modular conjugation `J`'s
**non-existence** is not a failure of GU's physical firewall realization but the **confirmed prediction**
that the firewall is intrinsically *observer-relative* -- "there is no observer-INDEPENDENT firewall" is
exactly what "the source action IS the observer" asserts. A skeptic's fair complaint is that such a
reinterpretation can be **unfalsifiable spin**: any wall gets relabelled a prediction.

This file removes that complaint. It fixes, **in advance and exhaustively**, the four outcomes `A/B/C/D`
of the adversarial test, states **before** reading the verdict exactly what evidence lands on each, then
records which outcome the already-run break (`W98`) hit and -- the deliverable -- states **exactly what
new evidence would reverse** the recorded verdict to each other outcome. Success is **legibility**: a
skeptical expert can see precisely what would flip the conclusion. That is what makes `W94`'s
interpretation genuinely falsifiable.

**This is a specification, not a new result.** It adds no computation. The verdict it records (**B**) and
that verdict's evidence are imported from `break-sectorial-closure-interacting-2026-07-11.md` /
`tests/W98_break_sectorial_closure.py`, at that file's honest grade. **Not committed. Not a claim-status
change. Exploration-grade.**

## 0. The pre-registered question (fixed before reading the verdict)

> **Q.** In a genuinely interacting, infinite-mode (continuum) realization -- not the free / mode-diagonal
> toy on which `W94` was established -- does the absence of a bounded **global** modular conjugation `J`
> become a **confirmed PREDICTION** (the firewall is necessarily observer-relative; the sectorial `J`
> carries every physical operation), or does it remain an **OBSTRUCTION** (some load-bearing physical
> quantity still needs global control the sectorial construction cannot provide)?

**The load-bearing objects (named, per `GEOMETER-VS-PHYSICS-OBJECTS.md`).** The three simultaneity
properties the reinterpretation needs (from `W98`):

- **(P1)** every physically finite **region** has a **bounded** valid modular conjugation `J_O`;
- **(P2)** the per-region `(Delta_O, J_O)` are **mutually coherent on overlaps** (net gluing);
- **(P3)** **no** observer-independent **global** bounded `J` exists.

`W94`'s reinterpretation is the claim that `P1 & P2 & P3` all hold at once: the sectorial `J` suffices
(`P1`), coheres into a net (`P2`), and its lack of a global limit (`P3`) *is* observer-relativity. The
fork below is exhaustive over what an adversarial relaxation can find.

## 1. The four pre-registered outcomes (exhaustive; A and B mutually exclusive by construction)

Stated **before** re-reading the verdict. Each row fixes the outcome, the physical reading, and -- in
advance -- **the exact evidence that would land the test on that outcome.**

### Outcome A -- sectorial modularity is INDEPENDENTLY SUFFICIENT for every physical operation
*(the wall is a genuine confirmed prediction; `W94` is right)*

- **Statement.** Every load-bearing physical operation of the firewall -- both the observer's
  value-selection **and** the firewall grading -- factors through a **bounded** modular conjugation on a
  **definitizable** sub-sector; the region's own `J_O` is bounded (`P1`); the sub-sector `J`'s cohere
  (`P2`); and the only object that fails to exist is the **observer-independent global** `J` (`P3`),
  whose non-existence is *precisely* the content of observer-relativity.
- **Landing evidence (fixed in advance).** (a1) a demonstration that **no** load-bearing operation
  depends on the region's UV tail -- everything needed is carried by a bounded `Pi_kappa` sub-sector `J`;
  **and** (a2) `sup_k cond(eta_+(r_k)) < inf` inside a fixed finite region (the region is
  **definitizable** despite being type-III_1), so `P1` genuinely holds for a **genuine region**, not
  only for a finite-rank cutoff. **A requires BOTH.**

### Outcome B -- some LOAD-BEARING quantity still requires GLOBAL control the sectorial construction cannot provide
*(the wall remains an obstruction)*

- **Statement.** At least one physically necessary object -- specifically a **genuine finite region's
  modular conjugation `J_O`** -- cannot be built from a bounded definitizable sub-sector: it needs the
  region's **whole UV mode tower**, which is exactly the global control the sectorial construction
  disclaims. Either `P1` fails (no bounded regional `J_O`), or `P2` fails (the sub-sector `J`'s do not
  cohere), or `P1` and `P3` are **mutually exclusive** (you cannot have a genuine firewall *and* a
  bounded regional `J`). The "wall" is therefore an **obstruction**, and calling it a "prediction" is
  premature.
- **Landing evidence (fixed in advance).** Any one of: (b1) a computation that a finite type-III_1
  region's metric conditioning **diverges** in its UV modes under the physical interaction (no bounded
  `J_O`; `P1` fails); (b2) a proof that `P1` and `P3` are **mutually exclusive** in every regime (no
  regime has both a genuine firewall and a bounded regional `J`); (b3) a computation that the per-region
  `J`'s **disagree UV-divergently on overlaps** (`P2` fails).

### Outcome C -- sectorial closure holds but is GENERIC to any local QFT
*(the "prediction" is empty of GU-specific content)*

- **Statement.** `P1`/`P2`/`P3` hold, but for reasons that hold in **every** Poincare-covariant QFT:
  standard Tomita-Takesaki attaches `(Delta, J)` to each `(region, state)`, and a global **geometric**
  `J` is proved only for the **wedge** (Bisognano-Wichmann). So "no observer-independent global `J`" is
  textbook AQFT, not a GU prediction; the reinterpretation is **true but content-free** -- it predicts
  nothing that distinguishes GU from a free scalar.
- **Landing evidence (fixed in advance).** (c) a demonstration that the entire sectorial-closure +
  no-global-`J` structure is **reproduced verbatim** in an ordinary (positive-metric, ghost-free) local
  QFT, with **no** step using the Krein indefiniteness, the ghost sector, HORN K, or any GU-specific
  input. If the same "wall = prediction" story runs for a free scalar, it carries no GU content.

### Outcome D -- a BOUNDED GLOBAL J exists in a better formulation
*(there is no wall to reinterpret)*

- **Statement.** The wall is an artifact of the specific Krein higher-derivative quantization. In a
  better formulation the ghost sector carries a **bounded, boundedly-invertible** global metric, a
  bounded **global** `J` exists (`P3` fails), the firewall is observer-**independent**, and the whole
  "observer-relativity from `J`'s non-existence" reading collapses (there is nothing absent to
  reinterpret).
- **Landing evidence (fixed in advance).** Any one of: (d1) a proof the physical ghost coupling is
  **UV-soft**, `g(k) = o(1/k)` (mixing dies faster than the frequency splitting -> `r_k -> 0` in the UV
  -> definitizable -> bounded global `J`); (d2) an explicit **interacting double-cone modular-conjugation
  construction** delivering a bounded `J_O` (hence, by type-III_1 sup=global, a bounded global `J`) for a
  genuine region; (d3) a **non-Krein / different-quantization** resolution of the ghost (e.g. a genuine
  positive-metric formulation) in which a bounded global metric exists.

**Exhaustiveness and exclusivity.** `A` and `B` are **mutually exclusive by construction**: `A` asserts
the load-bearing quantity needs only a bounded sub-sector; `B` asserts it needs global control. Exactly
one holds. `C` is the "closure holds but is generic" branch (orthogonal: it can co-occur with a nominal
`A`, and is the degenerate reading in which the prediction is empty). `D` is the "there is no wall"
branch (`P3` fails). Together `{A, B, C, D}` cover: prediction genuine and GU-specific (`A`); obstruction
(`B`); prediction generic/empty (`C`); no wall (`D`).

## 2. RECORDED VERDICT: **B** (the wall remains an obstruction)

**The adversarial test was run** -- `break-sectorial-closure-interacting-2026-07-11.md`,
`tests/W98_break_sectorial_closure.py` (7/7, exit 0) -- in a **larger interacting** Krein higher-
derivative / Pais-Uhlenbeck **continuum region** model, exactly the relaxation the fork pre-registers.
**It landed on Outcome B.**

**The load-bearing quantity that needs global control:** a **genuine finite spacetime region's modular
conjugation `J_O`**. By Reeh-Schlieder / the type-III_1 classification (Buchholz-D'Antoni-Fredenhagen), a
finite region is **infinite rank** and contains the **whole UV momentum tower** -- it is *not* the
finite-resolution cutoff `W94`'s "sectorial suffices" reading silently assumed. The sectorial `J`
`W94` built is bounded only on a finite-rank `Pi_kappa` truncation; a genuine region's `J_O` needs the
full tower, which is the global control the sectorial construction cannot supply.

**The exact evidence that landed B (the two `W98` findings):**

1. **P1-vs-P3 mutual exclusivity for a type-III_1 region** (`W98` T5, matches landing-evidence b2).
   Because a finite region contains all UV modes, `sup_region cond = sup_global cond`. Hence
   `P1` (region definitizable) `<=>` a bounded global `J` exists `<=>` NOT `P3`. Two regimes, no third:
   - **HORN Q (free / removable):** `P1` True, `P3` **False** -- a bounded global `J` exists, the
     firewall is trivial, the closure is **vacuous**.
   - **HORN K (interacting / genuine):** `P3` True, `P1` **False** -- no global `J`, the firewall is
     genuine, but every finite region is non-definitizable (no bounded regional `J_O`).
   A **genuine** firewall forces `P1` to fail; a **definitizable** region forces a removable ghost.
   **No regime has both** -- so the `A`-reading (a bounded regional/sub-sector `J` carrying the firewall)
   is unavailable exactly when the firewall is genuine.

2. **P2 overlap-coherence break for a type-III_1 region under a non-UV-soft interaction** (`W98` T6,
   matches landing-evidence b3). The interacting double-cone modular conjugation is region-dependent and
   non-geometric (only the wedge is geometric, Bisognano-Wichmann; only free fields are solved). A shared
   overlap mode acquires a region-dependent modular weight, so `r^{O1}_k != r^{O2}_k` and `J_{O1}`,
   `J_{O2}` **disagree on `M(O1 cap O2)`**, the disagreement **diverging in the UV** (`0.32 -> 10.97 ->
   34.70` as UV reach doubles; free: `0`). The per-region firewalls do **not** patch into a consistent
   net.

**Why B and not A.** The `A`-reading needs the load-bearing `J_O` to live on a bounded definitizable
sub-sector. Finding 1 shows that whenever the firewall is genuine (HORN K), the region is
non-definitizable -- the sub-sector escape is available only when the ghost is removable and the whole
firewall is trivial. Finding 2 shows the sub-sector `J`'s do not even cohere under interaction. The
load-bearing quantity therefore **does** require global control, which is the definition of **B**.

**Honest grade of the verdict (imported, not re-derived).** Reconstruction / strong-argument grade,
conditional on (i) the higher-derivative Krein-doublet reading of the ghost, (ii) a **non-UV-soft**
interaction (the physical derivative-coupled ghost is not UV-soft), (iii) HORN K (`W87`,
truncation-conditional). The **single load-bearing assumption** is (ii): a coupling `g(k)=o(1/k)` would
open the one survival window (that window is Outcome D's `d1`).

## 3. REVERSAL CONDITIONS -- exactly what evidence would flip B to each other outcome

**This is the deliverable.** Each condition is stated concretely enough that an outsider could pursue it.
A skeptic reading this section can see precisely what would overturn the "wall = obstruction" verdict.

### B -> A (the wall really is a confirmed prediction after all)
**Requires BOTH of the following** (because `A` needs the load-bearing quantity to need only a bounded
sub-sector, and Finding 1 blocks that whenever the firewall is genuine):

- **(a1) Physical-sufficiency proof.** A proof that **no** load-bearing physical operation depends on the
  region's UV tail: both the observer's value-selection **and** the firewall grading factor through a
  **bounded** modular conjugation on a **finite** definitizable `Pi_kappa` sub-sector, with **zero**
  dependence on the modes past the exceptional locus. (Partial progress exists -- `W91` argues the
  value-selection is positivity-free -- but the **firewall grading** provably needs an `eta`-positive
  `Delta^{1/2}`, i.e. definitizability, and that is what must be shown to localize.)
- **(a2) Regional-definitizability proof.** A demonstration that a genuine **type-III_1** region has
  **bounded** metric conditioning inside a fixed finite region: `sup_k cond(eta_+(r_k)) < inf` as `k ->
  inf` within the region. Concretely this means overturning either the Reeh-Schlieder "region contains
  all momenta" input or the `r_k -> 1` UV-degeneracy mechanism -- e.g. by exhibiting a physical mechanism
  that keeps `r_k` bounded away from 1 over the region's UV tail.

*Note.* `a2` overlaps Outcome `D`'s `d1`/`d2`: a UV-soft coupling or a bounded regional `J_O` would
supply `a2`. The difference is that `A` additionally needs `a1` (sufficiency) **and** insists the global
`J` still not exist (`P3` holds), whereas `D` has the global `J` **existing** (`P3` fails). If you prove
`a2` via a bounded **global** `J`, you have landed `D`, not `A` -- `A` needs `a2` to hold **without**
resurrecting a bounded global `J`, i.e. a bounded regional/sub-sector `J` on a type-III_1 region whose
global limit still diverges. Finding 1 says that combination is impossible (region sup = global sup), so
**`A` is reachable only by breaking the type-III_1 sup=global identity** -- the sharpest single target.

### B -> D (there is no wall to reinterpret)
**Requires any one of:**

- **(d1) UV-soft coupling.** A proof that the **physical** ghost coupling is UV-soft, `g(k) = o(1/k)`
  (mixing dies faster than the frequency splitting `Dw(k) ~ 1/k`). Then `r_k -> 0` in the UV, the region
  is definitizable, and a bounded global `J` exists. `W98` T7 verifies a `1/k^2` coupling keeps the
  conditioning bounded -- so this is a concrete, checkable target; the physics question is whether the
  Weyl^2 derivative vertices actually give such softening (they appear not to, which is why B was
  recorded).
- **(d2) Interacting bounded regional/global `J` construction.** An explicit **interacting double-cone**
  modular-conjugation construction that delivers a **bounded** `J_O` for a genuine (type-III_1) region.
  By the type-III_1 sup=global identity this is equivalent to a bounded **global** `J`. This is a
  recognized open problem in AQFT (Casini-Huerta: solved only for free/conformal fields); solving it in
  the interacting Krein setting would land `D` directly.
- **(d3) Better formulation.** A **non-Krein** or otherwise different quantization of the higher-
  derivative / ghost sector (e.g. a genuine positive-metric formulation, or a resolution in which the
  ghost is not a Krein doublet at all) carrying a **bounded, boundedly-invertible** global metric. Then
  `P3` fails and the reinterpretation has nothing to reinterpret.

### B -> C (the closure holds but the "prediction" is empty)
**Requires:**

- **(c) Genericity demonstration.** A demonstration that the sectorial-closure + no-global-`J` structure
  is **reproduced verbatim** in an **ordinary positive-metric, ghost-free** local QFT -- with **no** step
  invoking the Krein indefiniteness, the ghost sector, HORN K, or any GU-specific input -- so that "no
  observer-independent global `J`" is pure Bisognano-Wichmann / per-region Tomita-Takesaki content that
  holds for a free scalar. If the identical "wall = prediction" narrative runs for a free field, the
  reinterpretation carries **no GU-specific predictive content**: it is true but empty.
- *Status.* `C` is a **partial** threat even at B: `W94`'s own D2 cross-check concedes that per-region
  modular conjugation and wedge-only geometric `J` are **standard** AQFT facts. The open question `C`
  poses is whether the GU-specific content (the Krein ghost driving `P1`/`P3` mutual exclusivity) adds
  anything *beyond* the generic per-region story. Under B it does add something -- the **obstruction**
  (mutual exclusivity + overlap break) is Krein/ghost-specific, not generic -- but a skeptic pursuing `C`
  would try to show even that is a repackaging of ordinary type-III entanglement UV-divergence.
