practice** — which the meta-pattern *predicts* rather than excuses. The day's strongest positives (renormalizable,
asymptotically free, the loop-cost map, the generation no-go) are all *arena* results, several **GU-independent**;
the day's honest limits (framework-not-theory, DE cornered, zero standing predictions) are all *value* failures.
The pattern accounts for both.

### (c) Grade, and what a skeptic still withholds

**Grade: a promising organizing principle — specifically, an *established description* of the program's outputs
at a *proposed-organizing-principle* grade for the physics.** "Framework-not-theory" is now audited fact (H53);

*decoupling and untestability*; that is coherence, not confirmation. (3) **Zero standing predictions** — the one
value that was forced to the end (`mu_DW`-linked short-range gravity, via the H36 identification) *self-falsified*;
GU has demonstrated predictive *capacity* but no standing prediction.

**A model of the epistemic structure; still a narrative about the physics.** "Forces the arena, not the value"

narrative-about-physics into a model-of-physics. Honest line: **we have a model of the program and a narrative of
the theory.**
