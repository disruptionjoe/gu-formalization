## 1. Prior-art sweep: W107 (grading-blindness / the finer invariant)

### 1.1 The serious threat, read properly: graded/super-KMS and twisted KMS

The state-vs-grading-twist pairing is NOT virgin territory. The mature corpus:

- **Buchholz-Longo 1999** (arXiv:hep-th/9905102): the modulus of any graded or twisted KMS
  functional of a C*-dynamical system is proportional to an ordinary KMS state, and **the twist is
  weakly inner** in the GNS representation; for asymptotically abelian (hence supersymmetric)
  systems, graded KMS functionals **do not exist**. This is the closest structural prior: a theorem
  whose content is exactly an obstruction living in the (state, grading) pairing.
- **Stoytchev 1993** (Lett. Math. Phys. 27, 43): reconstruction from a self-adjoint graded-KMS
  functional, presented as "a complete extension of the theory of Tomita and Takesaki to the
  nonpositive case"; modulus is KMS.
- **Kastler 1989 / Jaffe-Lesniewski-Osterwalder 1988**: super-KMS functionals as generators of
  entire cyclic cocycles (quantum K-theory / Chern character). The graded pairing feeds **index**
  invariants there, not classification-refining modular invariants.
- **Hillier 2015** (Ann. H. Poincare 16, 1899): super-KMS for graded-local conformal nets; also
  **unbounded** graded-KMS functionals -- but "unbounded" there means the *functional* is densely
  defined; not W107's unboundedness of the relative modular operator between a bounded state and its
  twist.
- **arXiv:2402.15574 (2024)**, KMS states on Z2-crossed products and twisted KMS functionals --
  the closest recent work, **read in full text** for this sweep. It classifies twisted KMS
  functionals by the **twisted center** `Z(M,V) = {x : Jx*J = xV}` of the GNS von Neumann algebra
  (Thm 2.9), splits by **weakly inner vs freely acting** grading (Thm 2.13), and for CAR/Bogoliubov
  systems gives existence by the **Gibbs trace-class condition** `Tr_{H_odd}(e^{-|beta H_odd|}) < inf`.
  Checked explicitly: it does **not** use relative modular operators `Delta_{omega o theta, omega}`
  or Connes cocycle continuations, states no boundedness-of-relative-modular-data criterion, and does
  not connect twisted-KMS existence to the type classification.

**Is W107's invariant a known super-KMS obstruction under another name? NO -- the axes differ, and
W107 itself proved the separation.** The graded-KMS obstruction axes in the literature are:
(i) inner/outer-ness of the twist (Buchholz-Longo; 2402.15574's dichotomy); (ii) existence /
trace-class Gibbs conditions (2402.15574); (iii) quasi-equivalence/disjointness of `phi` and
`phi o theta` (Kakutani/Araki machinery). W107 T6 computed exactly these filtration-free candidates
against the wall and found them **transverse or inequivalent**: grading-twist disjointness is
transverse (MARG disjoint, no wall); global domination `phi o theta <= C phi` is strictly too strong
(fails even for Powers factors at bounded conditioning). The wall invariant -- **sup over the mode
filtration of `||Delta_{phi o AdJ, phi}||_k`** -- is a genuinely different object: an
(algebra, state, grading, **filtration**) invariant, which is precisely why it is not on any of the
graded-KMS lists.

### 1.2 The graded-classification field exists -- act one must be retold

"Does the graded classification literature already contain a grading-sensitive refinement of the
Connes invariants?" **YES, as a matter of principle**: the classification of Z2-actions (= gradings)
on injective factors is a completed field -- **Jones 1980** (Memoirs AMS: finite group actions on the
hyperfinite II_1 factor, classified by a normal subgroup + a relative-cohomology obstruction + a
further invariant), extended to type III by **Sutherland-Takesaki** and (III_1) by
**Kawahigashi-Sutherland-Takesaki**. Graded factors carry conjugacy invariants strictly finer than
the factor type. **Therefore the story cannot present "the classical classification is grading-blind"
as a discovery** -- it is the well-understood premise of an existing field.

**The precise delta that IS new:** the action-classification invariants are conjugacy invariants of
the pair `(M, theta)` alone (discrete data: inner/outer, cohomology class, Connes-Takesaki module).
W107's wall invariant is **not** of that kind and provably cannot be reduced to `(M, theta)` data --
its own filtration-free reductions fail (W107 T6 residual). What W107 adds, found nowhere in the
sweep under any name:

1. **The exact identity** `||Delta_{phi o AdJ, phi}||_k = cond(eta(r_k))` -- the Krein
   definitizability wall (bounded-metric-with-unbounded-inverse, the pseudo-Hermitian similarity
   obstruction) **equals** the grading-relative modular data, per mode, exactly.
2. **The invariant**: wall `<=>` `sup_k ||Delta_{phi o AdJ, phi}||_k = inf` over the mode filtration
   -- with the Connes-cocycle form (flow half unitary at all real t; continuation to `-i/2`
   obstructed) as its intrinsic packaging.
3. **The four-cell transversality theorem** against the ITPFI/Connes invariants on the explicit tower
   class (and, from 1.1, against the standard graded obstruction axes too).

### 1.3 The remaining flanks (lighter, checked)

- **Krein-space operator theory (Azizov-Iokhvidov, Langer):** the wall itself -- Gram operator with
  unbounded inverse, failure of uniform definitizability -- is classical there; **no
  modular-theoretic repackaging** and no confrontation with the von Neumann classification appears.
- **Pseudo-Hermitian/PT (Mostafazadeh; Krejcirik-Siegl; Antoine-Trapani):** bounded metric with
  unbounded inverse as the obstruction to bounded similarity (only quasi-similarity survives) is
  **known** -- this is the wall under its pseudo-Hermitian name, and W106 already cites
  Krejcirik-Siegl for it. But it lives as a similarity/spectral statement; never as a
  grading-relative modular invariant, never against the classification.
- **Twisted Araki-Woods (Correa da Silva-Lechner 2023):** "twisted" there = twisted second
  quantization (T-twisted Fock spaces; vacuum standard iff crossing + Yang-Baxter). Adjacent
  machinery, disjoint question.
- **Strocchi-Morchio:** the indefinite-metric axiomatics (HSSC etc.) do **not** classify -- no
  Krein-tower or graded-factor classification exists there to have anticipated W107.

Adjacent literature is real, mature, and must be cited as context (graded/super-KMS; Z2-action
classification; pseudo-Hermitian singular metrics). The **specific statement** -- the wall as the

## 2. Prior-art sweep: W106 (per-state/sup separation) -- be brutal

**The MECHANISM is classical, full stop.** The dichotomy "finite exactly on the form domain of an
unbounded densely-defined quadratic form; unit-ball sup = the operator norm = infinity" is
Reed-Simon textbook material -- W106 says so itself. Two further deflations the story must absorb:

- **The framing is folklore-adjacent in indefinite-metric QFT.** That individual matrix elements /
  Wightman functions stay finite while uniform positivity/majorization fails is close to the daily
  bread of the field: **Morchio-Strocchi's Hilbert-space-structure condition is literally a
  uniformity condition** on the majorization of Wightman functions, and gauge-theory Krein QFT lives
  on exactly the per-quantity-finite / no-uniform-bound split. **Gottschalk 2002** already gives the
  per-state strip (KMS/BW) analyticity on dense analytic-vector domains in Krein QFT -- the per-state
  leg of W106's target, in the continuum, 24 years ago.
- **So what is actually new?** Only the sharp quantitative content on the model: (a) the sharp class
  `D(|k|^{(p+1)/4})` with boundary `alpha*(p) = (p+3)/4` and the endpoint excluded (log-divergent);
  (b) the mandatory-states-inside verification (Paley-Wiener beats every polynomial order) and the
  three named exclusions with their standard-QFT parallels; (c) the nested hierarchy of audited forms
  (expectations L^2 / entropy log / strip `|k|^{1/4}` / `Delta^{-1}` `|k|^{1/2}`); (d) the honest
  exponential would-kill and the named Weinberg input; (e) the exact restatement of the **W98 wall as
  the unit-ball sup of the SAME form** -- tying the break and the per-state finiteness into one
  object.

**W106 VERDICT: MECHANISM-CLASSICAL / APPLICATION-NEW.** The story may use it only as "the sharp
quantitative version, on the model, of a split the indefinite-metric literature has always lived
with" -- i.e. a theorem-grade sharpening, not a new dichotomy.
