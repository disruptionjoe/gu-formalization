# Parsimony / unexplained-joints ledger — GU vs three rivals

(council-reframe-arena-abduction) warned this converter **degenerates into

`sigma` is Godel-independent / oracle-relative — LC-SELECTOR). But the rivals
derive powerfully only inside a **tiny arena** (collider rates, g-2, CMB) while
leaving the deep **joints** brute. GU inverts the trade — weak on native
field-markings, but it wires the QM sign, the GR vacuum sign, the DE sign, and
the arrow to **one bit**. **Claim:** counting unexplained *joints* (not just
knobs), GU leaves fewer brute facts at equal coverage, so it is the better
*explanation* by IBE. This document tests that honestly — to substantiate OR to
expose it.

**One structural fact up front, load-bearing for everything below.** `sigma` is
external at operator grade (LC-SELECTOR: the genuine flat-geodesic fiber ends
cross `{q<0}`, the K-definite cut does not exist, the `n+=n-` guarantee is void,
an external `Z/2` orientation bit is required). So no line below is a

What is genuinely unusual is that this ONE supplied bit does **quadruple duty** —
QM Krein sign = GR vacuum-cancellation sign = DE sign = record/time arrow, a

## 1. The four disciplines (mandatory — this is the whole point)

1. **FAIR / flag GU's losses explicitly.** A ledger that only shows GU winning is
   rigged and is rejected. GU **imports** the SM field-markings the SM merely
   posits (6 typed imports); it is **weak on native masses/mixings**; **dark
   matter is out of GU's scope**; the **DE amplitude is unexplained** (ratio-only,
   freely specifiable — as brute as `Lambda`). These are booked below as
   non-wins, not smoothed.
2. **OBSERVABLE vs INTERNAL-only.** For GU's wired joints, mark which are
   OBSERVABLE (real evidence exposed to data — the DE no-phantom-crossing) vs
   INTERNAL-only (the QM/GR/arrow co-flip, wired by the construction axiom
   SRC-COH-1 — currently **unification, not consilience**, hence **not
   independent evidence**; council Member 5 / Chairman (ii)).
3. **PLANTED CONTROL.** Build a deliberately-rigged ledger (count the internal
   co-flip as evidence; hide GU's imports) and show the disciplined scorer
   **rejects** it (Section 5). Method with teeth, not an advocacy engine.
4. **Order-of-magnitude only.** The posterior is a log10 sketch, explicitly
   conditional on what would have to be observed. **No false precision.**

**Equal-coverage rule (the anti-degeneration guard).** GU is credited a "win" on
a joint ONLY where it renders non-brute something a rival leaves brute **and**
GU actually covers the same ground. Where GU is silent (dark matter, the DE
amplitude, baryogenesis), it does **not** win — it is equal-brute or *worse*
(the rival at least parametrizes the thing; GU says nothing). Collider rates,
g-2, precision electroweak, BBN abundances, structure growth — domains GU does
**not** address — are **excluded from the count entirely** so GU gets no free
credit for silence and no penalty for not competing where it never entered.
