# Can we build the B.5 source-action spine ourselves?

**One-line answer.** **Yes for the build; no for the escape from ambiguity — and a sibling
repo (P2C) is already partway there with a genuinely independent, no-Weinstein specimen.** An
operator-grade GU source action that RECOVERS the six legs is feasible: the substrate, the
standard-field template, a toy that already passes four of the six spec mechanisms, and now a
published-literature σ-habitat specimen all exist. But no construction — ours, P2C's, or
Weinstein's — forces a *unique native* differential, because the selecting datum `σ` is
proven external (Gödel-independent, LC-SELECTOR). P2C's discrete-GfE specimen does **not**
reconstruct B.5; it covers a *complementary* slice of the obligations (the target-free
variational action + the operator-carried Z/2) that the GU toy lacks, while lacking the
BV/BRST + coupling half the GU toy has. Net: **independent B.5 reconstruction is feasible at
operator grade, worth a Lane-1 build, and strictly more uncontestable than waiting on
Weinstein — but it caps below native, and P2C's route is an ingredient, not a finished
answer.**

The cross-repo (P2C) material below is cited **as evidence and as a candidate ingredient
only**; per the tri-repo guards it moves no GU claim grade.
