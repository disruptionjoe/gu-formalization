# Construction swing — posit σ = the shard-cycle closure orientation

## MODE (binding, read first)
This is CONSTRUCTION, not derivation. We ALREADY know σ is external / not forced by GU
structure — that is the PREMISE, not a defect. So the numerology / forcing gate does NOT
apply at the front. We POSIT the thing, build the coherent picture around it, and put the
gate at the BACK: does the posited picture force INDEPENDENT consequences (things NOT
inserted by hand) that VERIFY against banked facts and reality? Rules:
1. The posit `S` is DECLARED and kept labeled throughout; NEVER re-report the built picture
   as "we derived σ = X." (This one discipline is the whole of the old numerology gate —
   it catches the conflation that burned three swings this session, nothing more.)
2. The payoff is INDEPENDENT consequences: a consequence that merely restates the posit is
   worthless; a consequence the posit FORCES but we did NOT insert (a relation, a number, a
   constraint) is the result. Separate the two explicitly.
3. Back-gate = VERIFY THE OTHER WAY: check independent consequences against banked results
   (Q2-FREE, Q3, σ=w₁(L_time), the ~8% null stratum, one-global-arrow) and against reality.
   A CONTRADICTION with a banked fact KILLS the posit (honest). A coherent picture that
   forces nothing independent is a SCAFFOLD, not a result (three-tier rule).

## Prong 1 — STRUCTURAL / GEOMETRIC construction
Build the coherent picture from P1+P2. With σ INSERTED as the cycle-closure orientation:
what surrounding structure follows, and what does it FORCE independently? Targets for
INDEPENDENT consequences (not inserted): the relation between the inserted cycle-orientation
and σ=w₁(L_time) (is it forced COMPATIBLE — the realization map — even though neither is

## Prong 2 — DYNAMICAL construction (Joe's static-vs-dynamic gap)
Build the picture from P3. Model record-issuance as a dynamical SELF-MAP; posit σ as its
fixed point. Answer: does the loop have a fixed point (Lawvere / point-surjectivity)? UNIQUE
(⇒ the dynamics self-supplies σ — the inside supplies it after all, overturning static
"can't-supply"), MULTIPLE (⇒ the loop doesn't fix the value — still a free/external choice),
or NONE (⇒ Gödel obstruction, consistent with external)? PUSH vs PULL: is the external
adapter a push (environment writes) or a pull (system queries an opaque token it can't read)
or self-constitution (loop settles)? Derive INDEPENDENT dynamical consequences (a record-rate

Note: gated on the product-uniformity theorem where point-surjectivity bottoms out; say so.

## Prong 3 — VERIFY THE OTHER WAY (the back-gate; fires after 1 & 2)
Collect every INDEPENDENT consequence from Prongs 1 & 2. For each: (a) does it hold against
banked facts / does it CONTRADICT one (→ kills the posit)? (b) is it directly FALSIFIABLE
(→ a real prediction, add to the battery) or a SCAFFOLD (→ keep, name the downstream test)?
(c) does any consequence force something the posit did NOT insert AND that checks out (→ the
construction earns its keep)? Verdict: POSIT-PRODUCTIVE (forces verified independent
consequences) / POSIT-COHERENT-SCAFFOLD (coherent, consequences not yet falsifiable) /
POSIT-CONTRADICTED (an independent consequence contradicts a banked fact — the posit is
wrong). Keep S labeled as a posit throughout; do not promote to canon.
