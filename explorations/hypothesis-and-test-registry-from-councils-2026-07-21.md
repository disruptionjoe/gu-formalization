# Hypothesis & Test Registry (from the 22-construction councils)

Purpose: preserve **every reasonable hypothesis or test** the 22 committed
constructions (5 science, 6 systems, 4 extended, 7 math) plus the coherence
cross-assessment produced, so none is lost. This is a **registry**, not a
synthesis — diversity is preserved on purpose. True duplicates (the same claim
proposed on many substrates) are **clustered into one entry that lists all
proposers and their distinct substrate framings**; minority / idiosyncratic
posits are kept, not dropped (they are the most losable).

**Proposer IDs.** SCI-ORTH (superselection/θ-sector), SCI-HET (records/finality),
SCI-WILD (participatory self-excited circuit), SCI-COM (buildable ledger),
SCI-PHIL (indexical first-person primacy); SYS-ZK (ZK/FHE circuit), SYS-NN
(training run / flat objective), SYS-MMO (server-authoritative netcode), SYS-META
(metastable sampling consensus), SYS-HASH (gossip/hashgraph), SYS-FLP (async
consensus / FLP); EXT-EVO (replicator/ESS), EXT-STAT (non-identifiable nuisance
parameter), EXT-HYPER (hypergraph incidence class), EXT-INTER (intersubjective
coequalizer); MTH-DIFF (fibered pseudo-Riemannian geometry), MTH-TOPO (homotopy
type / char. classes), MTH-CLIF (Cl(9,5) spinor), MTH-CAT (topos/Lawvere),
MTH-SHEAF (finality sheaf cohomology), MTH-INFO (Shannon channel), MTH-KOLM
(algorithmic-information/oracle machine); X-ASSESS (cross-assessment "what falls
out" shared-consequence rows).

**Tier legend.**
- **DIRECTLY-FALSIFIABLE** — a shadow that could fail: a near-term experiment,
  observation, buildable system, or a concrete computation whose result could
  come out the other way.
- **STRUCTURAL-DERIVATION** — a math claim provable/refutable internally to GU.
- **SCAFFOLD** — coherence-bearing, not-yet-falsifiable, points downstream.

**Anchor legend** (vs banked results): CONSISTENT (restates/honors a banked
result), EXTENDS (adds testable content beyond what is banked), IN-TENSION
(pushes against a banked result or an open seam). Banked touchstones: **Q2-FREE**
(σ a hosted-not-supplied free Z/2 coin), **Q3-TWO-INDEPENDENT** (σ⊥τ,
Z/6=Z/2×Z/3), the **~8% q<0 / K_S-null non-constructible stratum**
(P0-FAILS-OBSTRUCTED), **σ = w₁(L_time)** over F≃RP³ (circle/cycle reading a
*class-relative, local* no-go — OPEN, not globally refuted), the **WEAK Prong-3
"one globally consistent record arrow."**
