# published-papers/

The **top level of this folder is empty by default** -- it holds only papers that have been **published
publicly** (arXiv, a journal, a live blog post). Joe moves a paper up here by hand when, and only when, it
actually goes public, recording the venue / date / arXiv id.

Near-final papers that Joe has explicitly decided to publish but has **not yet posted** live one level down,
in **`candidates/`** (see `candidates/README.md`).

## The three-stage lifecycle

1. `../draft-papers/` — work in progress; all drafts, notes, and **previous/superseded versions**. Nothing
   here is a public claim. Edit freely.
2. `candidates/` — staged for publication. A paper graduates here **only when Joe has explicitly said he
   wants to publish it**; its earlier versions stay in `draft-papers/`. Near-final, not yet public.
3. `published-papers/` (here) — the public record. A paper moves up **only after Joe confirms it has actually
   been published**. Append-mostly; do not silently rewrite a published artifact (publish a revision and note
   it).

A paper lives in exactly one stage at a time, so its location always tells the truth about its status.
