## Key convergent findings

Across all eight syntheses, three convergent claims carry the highest weight:

1. **The four no-go theorems compute forgetful images of substrate-level invariants in the smooth bundle shadow.** Each theorem is correct about what it computes. The theorems compute the wrong object for the purpose of evaluating Geometric Unity-class claims.

2. **The forgetful operation depends on at least six structural axes.** A Geometric Unity-class program specification requires fixing all six. The combinatorial space contains roughly 16,000 candidate hextuples; the mathematically tractable subset is roughly 20-50.

3. **Connes Type II_1 spectral triples extended to the non-embeddable regime (witnessed by MIP* = RE) constitute the strongest mathematically-adjacent substrate-class candidate.** Three other candidates have substantial existing mathematics: holographic quantum error-correcting codes, Wolfram multiway hypergraph rewriting, and higher categorical substrates valued in motivic / tmf / prismatic cohomology.

The supplementary syntheses (06, 07) operationalize these findings into specific tests and open problems. The four deep-research briefs in `../deep-research/` corroborate that the literature does not currently host published positive constructions or published counter-results for the two highest-priority directions (Connes Type II_1 non-embeddable internal algebra, observer-pairing-relative Freed-Hopkins anomaly classification).
