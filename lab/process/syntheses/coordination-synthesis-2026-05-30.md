## Section 1. Six-axis is the scaffold; downstream cards confirm AND extend it

The six-axis template (WRK-375) is the load-bearing organizing structure. Every other GU card slots into it as an axis-instance or axis-drop. Downstream cards produced four updates the six-axis README should absorb:

**1a. L5/L6 coupling is constitutive for universality-class substrates (NEW from WRK-385).** A universality class is a quotient construction under RG flow — the equivalence relation IS RG flow. Sextuples with one but not the other are incoherent. WRK-384 §4 supplies a blocked-combination table.

**1d. L1 menu may need an (h) / (h') split (NEW from WRK-383).** Cartan-parabolic-on-Met(X) and twistor-substrate-inversion are structurally different L1 choices that have been conflated in casual GU discourse. Distinguishing them clarifies which falsifiable question a given draft is answering.

**1e.
