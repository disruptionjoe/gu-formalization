## Ledger Row

| field | entry |
| --- | --- |
| Required datum | A typed record-boundary adapter that preserves the signed-readout theorem's separation between monotone provenance and signed, cancellation-bearing scalar readout. | Local constraint | `canon/signed-readout-boundary-theorem-RESULTS.md` already closes the abstract M/P/C core and compact finite Z/K cases, while `lab/active-research/signed-readout/README.md` keeps the non-compact OC1/OC2 extension behind a Uniform Invertibility at Infinity estimate. `explorations/time-as-finality-crosswalk/signed-readout-record-graph-test.md` requires finality, evidence, causality, and readout not to be conflated. |

The tri-repo division of labor explicitly blocks identity claims before adapter contracts prove out. Observer-finality vocabulary is therefore only a candidate modeling language unless it preserves the theorem's monotone-provenance / non-monotone-readout split in a concrete record graph. |
| Non-arbitrariness test | The adapter must define the four relations before examples are scored, run both a positive-only monotone case and a signed-cancellation case, and show that finalized records can grow or stabilize while scalar readout decreases or cancels.
