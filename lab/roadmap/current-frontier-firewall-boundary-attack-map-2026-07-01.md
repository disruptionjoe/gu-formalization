# Current Frontier: Firewall-Boundary Attack Map

This map orients a contributor to the current GU Formalization frontier without
changing any verdict, claim grade, or canon status.

## Target

The live organizing question is the Firewall-Boundary Hypothesis:

> Does every successful reconstruction converge on a firewall-like boundary
> object rather than a closed internal completion?

Canonical source: `canon/firewall-boundary-hypothesis.md`.

`NEXT-STEPS.md` states the posture correctly: **attack it, do not defend it**.
The hypothesis weakens if a closed completion, boundary-free equivalent,
alternative completion, representation-artifact explanation, or boundary-rejected
derivation of the known physics succeeds.

## What the next attack should try

The most valuable next Progress work is not to add another attractive selector.
It is to make one falsification attempt precise enough that it either fails
cleanly or becomes a real counterexample.

Good next attacks:

1. **Closed internal source action.** Try to construct a closed GU-internal source
   action that pins the under-determined generation count without target import.
2. **Boundary-removal equivalence.** Try to reformulate the surviving boundary
   object as an internal object with no loss of SM, QM, or three-generation
   structure.
3. **Representation artifact audit.** Re-run the relevant obstruction under a
   defensible alternative reconstruction, especially where Cl(9,5) dependence
   remains a caveat.
4. **Known-physics survival test.** Reject the boundary reading and check whether
   the physics still survives without free import of the missing multiplicity.

## Forbidden shortcuts

Do not use:

- `ind_H(D_RS)=8` as an input.
- `16 + 8 = 24` as an argument.
- compact `chi(K3)=24` as a selector.
- physical degree-of-freedom counting as a generation derivation.
- an unexplained rational factor.
- an external spurion renamed as a GU-native source action.
- compatibility as derivation.
- a verdict or claim-status change without the claim-status consistency workflow.

## Current honest boundary

The strong firewall form is dead where it depended on quaternionic parity as a
proof of external necessity. The weak form remains the live frontier: GU appears
to under-determine multiplicity across serious internal reconstructions.

The current attack should therefore aim at the weak form, not resurrect the dead
strong form.

## Stop conditions

Stop and record rather than push further if:

- a construction imports the target count or K3-like value by hand,
- a proof requires a status/verdict change,
- the path depends on hidden cross-repo action,
- the result is only aesthetic fit,
- the attack exposes a representation caveat that needs separate adjudication,
- the work would alter canon or public framing.

## Good stopping point

A useful Progress run can stop after producing one of:

- a minimal closed-source-action candidate with explicit failure conditions,
- a negative result that names exactly which guard fired,
- a test harness that discriminates closed completion from boundary adapter,
- a short addendum to this map identifying the next falsification flank.

Any positive verdict belongs in the formal claim/status path, not in this map.
